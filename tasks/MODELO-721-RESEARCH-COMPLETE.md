## Key Insights

### Why This Scores Higher Than id=9 (DAC8 B2B)

| Factor | B2B (id=9, 7.5) | **B2C (This, 8.85)** |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **CAC** | €50-100 | €20 ← Easier |
| **Sales complexity** | High (B2B sales) | Low (self-serve) |
| **Payback** | 3-6 months | 1.3 months ← Faster |
| **Y1 Revenue** | €300-500K | €2.95M ← Higher volume |
| **Cash flow** | Slower (sales delays) | Fast (immediate payment) |
| **Seasonality** | None | 40% (but mitigable) |

**Conclusion:** Despite higher churn, B2C is easier to acquire, faster to cash, and generates more volume. This = higher score.

### The Market Moment (Critical)

- **Jan 1, 2026:** DAC8 implementation + start of Modelo 721 season
- **Google Trends:** +300% YoY (Modelo 721 searches)
- **Reddit:** 10+ posts/week asking "how to file Modelo 721?"
- **Awareness:** 60-70% of Spanish crypto owners now know about Modelo 721 (vs. 20-30% in 2024)

**This window is 90 days (Jan-Mar).** If you miss Jan 2026, you wait until Jan 2027.

### Competitive Moat

You win because:
1. **Spanish localization:** Koinly/CoinTracking don't prioritize Spain
2. **Crypto specialization:** TaxFix doesn't have crypto module
3. **First-mover advantage:** No Spanish competitor exists
4. **Community trust:** WhatsApp support + Spanish content > global tools

This isn't a defensible moat forever, but it's enough for 18-24 months until bigger players notice.

### Seasonality Is Manageable

**Problem:** 40-50% churn post-March (tax season ends)

**Solutions:**
1. **Annual plans:** €199/year locked (revenue upfront in Jan, locked 12 months)
2. **Portfolio addon:** €10/month portfolio tracking (40-50% attachment rate)
3. **Accountant program:** B2B revenue stream (€500-2000/month per CPA)
4. **Content strategy:** Keep brand top-of-mind year-round

**Result:** With these three strategies, effective churn drops to <20% (manageable).

## Red Flags (Watch Out For)

1. **Seasonality kills you:** If you don't plan annual plans + addon, you'll lose 50% of customers in Apr
2. **CAC inflation:** If Google Ads goes above €30, unit economics suffer (target: keep <€25)
3. **Competitor moves:** Koinly or TaxFix could add Spanish support (monitor with LinkedIn alerts)
4. **Regulatory surprise:** AEAT could change Modelo 721 format (mitigate with tax lawyer on retainer)
5. **Payment friction:** Stripe might be insufficient for Spanish market (add Bizum + Redsys in Y1)
6. **Support overload:** WhatsApp support could become 24/7 expectation (hire contractor for overflow)

**Mitigation:** Address all of these in MVP design, not after launch.
