## RGPD Compliance SaaS — Почему ЗАПУСКАТЬ

### The Gap
- **80% испанских SMB не соответствуют RGPD** требованиям (2025 data)
- RGPD существует с 2018, но нет испанского лидера
- Cookiebot/Iubenda — глобальные, но не specialized for Spain

### The Urgency
- **AEPD fines escalated 19% YoY** (€35.5M в 2024)
- **10 fines >€1M** в 2024 (vs только 3 в 2023)
- Regulatory pressure будет расти → SMB ищут решение

### The Market
- 3.3M SMB в Испании, 2.6M потенциально not compliant
- TAM: €52-130M (at €20-50/mес per SMB)
- No clear Spanish winner (gap!)

### The MVP (6-8 weeks)
1. Auto RGPD Audit (website scanner)
2. Spanish templates (Privacy Policy, DPA, Cookie Policy)
3. Simple consent banner
4. AEPD compliance checklist
5. Employee training module

### The Unit Economics
- **LTV/CAC: 18-28x** (healthy)
- CAC: €10-15 (SMB finds via content/SEO)
- LTV: €278 (at €29/mес, 40% retention)

### The Risk: LOW
- Regulatory demand is stable (RGPD not changing)
- Regulatory tailwind (more enforcement)
- First-mover advantage in Spain possible

## Gestoría Modern SaaS — CONDITIONAL GO

### The Opportunity
- 65-70K gestorías in Spain (stable market)
- Legacy software is **genuinely awful** (UI, integrations, cost)
- **VeriFactu deadline Jan 2027** = forced migration wave

### The Challenge
- Sage 50 Asesor already modernized (VeriFactu built-in)
- Holded is capturing market (80K clients, growing)
- CAC is high (€100-200), long sales cycle (2-3 months)

### Why It Could Work
- Positioning: "migration service from Contaplus" (not abstract)
- Unit economics healthy (€12.5M ARR at 20% penetration)
- VeriFactu deadline creates artificial urgency

### ONLY GO IF:
1. ✓ You have 5-10 gestoría beta customers ready
2. ✓ You can ship VeriFactu integration before Jan 2027
3. ✓ You partner with licensed payroll/tax provider (don't build it)
4. ✓ You launch as "migration + modern SaaS" not pure SaaS

**Otherwise:** Too late, Sage/Holded already winning.

## EV Fleet Management — SKIP

### Why NO-GO

2. **Already have local winners** — Electromaps (Wallbox), PulpoFleet
3. **Monta entering in 2025** — EU player with experience

## 4-Week Launch Plan for RGPD SaaS

### Week 1: Validation
- 10 interviews с SMB owners
- Key question: "Would you pay €30/мес for automated RGPD audit?"
- Measure pain level (1-10)

### Week 2: Demand Test
- Create simple landing page (unbounce or custom)
- Google Ads test: €100 budget, target "RGPD españa", "cumplimiento RGPD"
- Measure CTR, CPC, conversion to email list

### Week 3: MVP Prototype
- Build audit tool (basic website scanner for tracking scripts)
- Write 3 Spanish templates (Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, DPA)
- Simple consent manager (just HTML/CSS, no fancy UI)

### Week 4: Soft Launch
- Product Hunt ES announcement
- Twitter outreach to 20 relevant accounts
- Activate beta customers from Week 1
- Measure: signups, activation, retention (1-week cohort)

### Key Metrics to Track
- Landing page visitors
- Email list growth
- Beta signups
- Email→signup conversion
- Feature adoption in first week

### Go/No-Go Decision Point (Week 5)
- **GO if:** >500 landing page visitors, >5% signup rate, 3+ paying beta customers
- **PIVOT if:** <500 visitors or <2% signup rate
- **NO-GO if:** All three metrics miss
