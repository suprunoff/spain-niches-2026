## Top 3 Recommendations (Ranked by Confidence)

### 1️⃣ FUNERAL SERVICES AGGREGATOR (Score 9.1) — GO IMMEDIATELY
**Status:** Green light for immediate build
- **Why:** Proven model (Beyond UK), massive TAM, zero competition, high customer willingness (60%)
- **MVP:** 6-8 weeks, €5-10K budget
- **GTM:** Cold calls to funeral homes + Google Ads
- **Path to Revenue:** 10-50 transactions/month × €50-500 = €1-25K MRR
- **Year 1 Goal:** €10-50K MRR (30-50 funeral homes in Madrid)
- **Year 2 Goal:** €100-300K MRR (expand Barcelona, Valencia)

**Key Success Factor:** Get first 5 funeral homes, become default aggregator in Madrid

### 2️⃣ NOTARY DIGITAL PORTAL (Score 8.2) — GO (Phase 2)
**Status:** Green light, but longer sales cycle
- **Why:** Law 11/2023 enabled digital, but old software (Wordplex, JPA, Notin) hasn't evolved
- **MVP:** 8 weeks, €15-20K budget
- **GTM:** B2B, partner with notary associations
- **Path to Revenue:** 50-100 notaries × €200-400/mo = €10-40K MRR
- **Year 1 Goal:** €20K MRR (10 notaries in Madrid + 5 in Barcelona)
- **Leverage:** Once 5 notaries in city adopt → others copy (network effect)

**Key Success Factor:** First mover advantage. Notaries are conservative but will adopt if shown ROI.

### 3️⃣ VET + PET INSURANCE INTEGRATION (Score 8.4) — GO (Growing Market)
**Status:** Green light, fastest growing market
- **Why:** Insurance penetration 5% → 15% in 18 months. No integrations exist yet.
- **MVP:** 12 weeks, €20-30K budget
- **GTM:** Partner with 1 insurer (Adeslas), clinic adoption follows
- **Path to Revenue:** 500-1000 clinics × €50-150/mo + insurer subscriptions = €25-150K MRR
- **Year 1 Goal:** €30-50K MRR (50-100 clinics + 1 major insurer)
- **Year 2 Goal:** €150-300K MRR (all 4 major insurers integrated)

**Key Success Factor:** Lock in one major insurer early. They become your growth engine.

## Markets NOT Recommended

### Opticians (Score 7.2) — NO-GO
- **Why:** Gesvision, Optiplus, OmiAE dominate. Low margins (30-40%). Independents resist change.
- **Verdict:** Too crowded for bootstrapped entry

### Auto Dealers (Score 7.8) — MAYBE
- **Why:** High CPC (€2.5+), real pain (EV trade-in), but Cazoo/Vroom coming in 12-18 months
- **Verdict:** Window closing. Jump in only if confident in 6-month build

### Fitness (Score 7.8) — MAYBE
- **Why:** Large market, but Freeletics/Future dominate. Integration with gym software = complex.
- **Verdict:** Team of 3+ needed. Not solo/duo play.
