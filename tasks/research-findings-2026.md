## 1. CONSUMER LEGAL TECH (Spain)

### Testamento Digital (Digital Will)
- **Legal Status:** Fully legal in Spain under Ley Orgánica 3/2018 (RGPD)
  - Digital wills must be formalized before a notary (same as traditional wills)
  - Notaries already drafting digital testaments, though no official registry exists yet
  - Source: [Legalitas - Digital Wills](https://www.legalitas.com/actualidad/que-es-el-testamento-digital-y-para-que-sirve), [Abogacía Española](https://www.abogacia.es/actualidad/noticias/testamento-digital-manual-de-uso/)

- **Market Gap:** Notaries handling intake, but no end-to-end digital platform simplifying the process exists
- **Consumer Need:** Manage digital assets (passwords, accounts, social media inheritance) + traditional estate planning

### Divorcio Express Online (Express Divorce Online)
- **Legal:** Mutual agreement divorces fully online in Spain
  - Timeline: 30 days (no children), 45-60 days (with children)
  - Requirements: 3 months marriage, at least one spouse in Spain

- **Price Range:** €100–€400 depending on complexity
- **Active Platforms:**
  - Divorcio Express (€100 no children, €125 with children)
  - Divortium (€400)
  - El Divorciador Express (€150+)
  - Source: [Divorcio Express](https://www.divorcioexpress.pro/), [El Divorciador Express](https://eldivorciadorexpress.com/)

- **Market Gap:** Fragmented, mostly lawyer-led. No consumer-friendly, transparent pricing leader yet.

### Reclamar Online (Bank Fee & Airline Compensation Claims)
- **Market Status:** [Source needed] — No direct app found; appears to be underserved
- **Opportunity:** Spanish banks charge extensive hidden fees (mortgage cancellation, account maintenance, etc.)
- **Consumer Pain:** 60%+ don't know fees are recoverable; manual process via lawyers costly

### Herencia Online (Inheritance Management)
- **Platforms Found:**
  - Plataforma Hereditaria — €500+ for complete management
  - Herencias Online — 40+ years experience, offices Madrid/Barcelona/Zaragoza/Valencia
  - Source: [Plataforma Hereditaria](https://plataformahereditaria.es/), [Herencias Online](https://www.herenciasonline.es/)

- **Service Model:** Full civil + tax management (no travel required)
- **Market Size:** [Source needed] — no user/revenue data found

## 2. PERSONAL FINANCE & MICRO-INVESTING (Spain)

### Robo-Advisors Market (Wealth Management)
- **Market Size:** €225M (2018) → €4,300M (2021) → Continued growth 2025+
  - Source: [Rankia — Robo Advisors España](https://www.rankia.com/blog/fondos-inversion/4328390-mejores-robo-advisors-espana)

- **Users & AUM:**
  - **Indexa Capital** — 138,000+ clients, €4,550M AUM (market leader)
  - **Finizens** — 20,000+ clients
  - **inbestMe** — 5,000 clients
  - Source: [Finect — Comparativa](https://www.finect.com/usuario/Kaloxa/articulos/comparativa-roboadvisors-carteras-indexadas)

- **Profitability:** Only Indexa Capital and Finizens are profitable in Europe
  - Source: [Carlos Galán — Robo Advisors 2026](https://carlosgalan.net/los-mejores-roboadvisors-comparativa-y-analisis-2023/)

### Household Savings Rate
- **2024:** 13.6% of disposable income
- **2025 Forecast:** ~13% (stable)
- **2026 Forecast:** Slight decline expected
- **Average Savings per Household:** €5,800 (2023) → €7,000+ (2024)
- **Growth Drivers:** +5% wage growth, employment growth
- Source: [CaixaBank Research — Household Savings](https://www.caixabankresearch.com/en/economics-markets/activity-growth/increase-spanish-household-savings-2024), [ABANCA — Ahorro Medio Español](https://www.cuentasclaras.es/ahorro/cual-es-ahorro-medio-espanol/)

### Micro-Investing Apps
- **Platforms:** Revolut, MyInvestor, Trade Republic, Bux Zero
- **Spanish Entrant:** Vesto — 20,000+ active users, invest from €5 in fractional US stocks
- **Minimum Entry:** €1–€10 per investment
- **User Growth:** 15% YoY increase in retail investors (4.5M total in Spain, 2023)
- **CNMV Data:** 4.5M retail investors (+15% growth documented)
- Source: [Vesto — Spanish Micro-Investing](https://vesto.app/), [Rankia — Apps Invertir](https://www.rankia.com/blog/mejores-brokers/5829952-mejores-brokers-online-espana)

### Private Pensions (Pensión Privada)
- **Public Pension Crisis:**
  - 60% of Spanish population considers public pension insufficient for retirement
  - Expected spending: 17.3% of GDP by 2050 (EC projections)
  - 2026 increase: +2.7%, avg pension rises €40.8/month to €1,552/month
  - Source: [EAE — Pensiones Privadas](https://www.eae.es/actualidad/noticias/la-segunda-mano-se-extiende-la-moda-3-de-cada-5-espanoles-ya-compra-ropa-usada), [Banco de España](https://www.bde.es/f/webbe/SES/Secciones/Publicaciones/Informesituacionfinancierafamiliasyempresas/2024/S1/Fich/SituacionFinanciera_Informe_012024.pdf)

- **Private Pension Market:** Growing segment as complement to public system
- **Opportunity:** Very low penetration; high perceived anxiety about retirement sustainability

## 3. HOME SERVICES & ON-DEMAND REPAIR (Spain)

### Market Size & Growth
- **2030 Projection:** €424.4M revenue
- **CAGR 2023–2030:** 17.9%
- **Spain's Share:** 2.6% of global market (2022)
- **Mobile Dominance:** 69.87% of transactions (2022)
- Source: [Grand View Research — Spain On-Demand Home Services](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/horizon/outlook/online-on-demand-home-services-market/spain)

### Key Platforms
- **Oscar** — 150+ task types (cleaning, repair, laundry, beauty); price-fixed delivery in 30 min
- **Helpling** — Focus on insured cleaners, integrated chat, card payments
- **Webel** — Cleaning + handyman; subscription models available
- **Habitissimo** — Largest platform (acquired by HomeServe 2017); mainly B2B professional marketplace
- **Cronoshare** — Competing with Habitissimo for local pro services
- **Wolly, Zaask, Homerd** — Growing specialists
- Source: [Oscar App](https://oscar-app.com/en), [Helpling](https://www.helpling.com/), [Habitissimo](https://www.habitissimo.es/)

### Competitive Gap
- **Helpling in Spain:** Primarily cleaning (not repairs despite global position)
- **Habitissimo Competitors:** Planreforma.com (49.9K visits), Hazmeprecio.com (5.4K visits)
- **Opportunity:** Subscription-based "monthly maintenance" models underexploited vs. one-off repairs
- Source: [Similarweb — Habitissimo Competitors](https://www.similarweb.com/es/website/habitissimo.es/competitors/)

### Cleaning Service Pricing
- **Hourly Rate:** €15/hr (range €10–€20)
- **Subscription Discount:** 10–20% reduction for weekly/monthly contracts
- **Flexibility:** Weekly, biweekly, monthly without minimum commitment
- Source: [Zaask — Limpieza del Hogar](https://www.zaask.es/cuanto-cuesta/limpieza-del-hogar)

## 4. FOOD & NUTRITION PERSONALIZATION (Spain)

### Meal Kit Delivery Market
- **Europe Size (2025):** €8.71B → projected €10.23B (2026) at 17.44% CAGR
- **Global Forecast (2026):** €30B
- **Spain Status:** Emerging market; Barcelona innovation hub
- Source: [Market Data Forecast — Europe Meal Kit](https://www.marketdataforecast.com/market-reports/europe-meal-kit-market), [Scoop Market — MKD Statistics 2026](https://scoop.market.us/meal-kit-delivery-services-statistics/)

### Key Players in Spain
- **HelloFresh** — Arrived 2022; ~50% global market share (struggling with customer acquisition in Spain)
- **Wetaca** — Local competitor, major player in prepared foods
- **Lola Market** — Food marketplace (not meal kits); connects Mercadona/Día/Supercor; 1-hour delivery in 11 cities (A Coruña, Bilbao, Pamplona, Zaragoza, Barcelona, Madrid, Valencia, Alicante, Murcia, Seville, Málaga)
- Source: [HelloFresh España](https://es.hellofresh.com/), [Merca2 — HelloFresh vs Wetaca](https://www.merca2.es/2025/06/30/hellofresh-wetaca-mercadona-2200666)

- **Prepared Foods Context:**
  - Consumption +6.6% in 2024
  - Market volume: 702.27M kg
  - Sales: €4.197B (+5.9% YoY)
  - Source: [Merca2](https://www.merca2.es/2025/06/30/hellofresh-wetaca-mercadona-2200666)

### Personalized Nutrition (DNA-Based)
- **Platforms:**
  - **Instituto Nutrigenómica** — 33,000 genes × 750,000 polymorphisms analyzed; partnership with Tellmegen (Spanish genetics lab)

  - **Oorenji** — AI + nutrigenomics; plans €3.99–€199/month; saliva test based
  - **Alimentomica (Mefood Omics)** — Platform for personalized dietary plans using nutrigenetics + ML
  - Source: [Instituto Nutrigenómica](https://institutonutrigenomica.com/), [Oorenji](https://oorenji.com/en/tu-adn/), [Alimentomica](https://alimentomica.com/en/servicios/nutricion-personalizada/)

- **Market Gap:** Services exist but not mass-market; high price + complex genetics knowledge barrier

## 5. SECOND-HAND & CIRCULAR ECONOMY (Spain)

### Market Size & Growth
- **Market Volume (2024):** €8.525B (+55% vs 2023)
- **Global Forecast (2025):** €244B second-hand fashion
- **Global Forecast (2030):** €84B clothing resale
- Source: [Emprendedores — Mercado Segunda Mano](https://emprendedores.es/ideas-de-negocio/segunda-mano/)

### Consumer Attitude Toward Second-Hand
- **Usage:** 85% of Spanish population uses second-hand platforms regularly
- **Consideration:** 94% of Spanish population considers second-hand in any purchase
- **Sustainability Priority:** 67% of consumers consider brand sustainability important
- **Preference:** 9 of 10 Spaniards prefer sustainable brands
- **Decision Factors:** Price-quality ratio (64%), item condition (61%), quality (53%)
- Source: [EAE — Moda Segunda Mano](https://www.eae.es/actualidad/noticias/la-segunda-mano-se-extiende-la-moda-3-de-cada-5-espanoles-ya-compra-ropa-usada), [Kantar — Consumo Consciente](https://www.kantar.com/es/inspiracion/consumidor/consumo-consciente-y-tendencias-de-consumo-en-espana)

### Major Platforms & Market Share
- **Wallapop** — 29.6% market share (2026 Basque region data); acquired by Naver (SK) for €600M in Jan 2026
- **Vinted** — 18.3% (specialized in second-hand fashion)
- **Milanuncios** — 10%
- **Blablacar** — 6.8%
- **eBay** — 6.5%
- Source: [Notícias de Gipuzkoa — Wallapop King](https://www.noticiasdegipuzkoa.eus/sociedad/2026/02/15/wallapop-rey-mercado-segunda-mano-10699172.html), [Movilzona — Wallapop vs Vinted](https://www.movilzona.es/noticias/aplicaciones/wallapop-vs-vinted-diferencias/)

### Luxury Second-Hand
- **Vestiaire Collective** — Targeting 5% revenue from Spain; 3M items, 12,000+ brands, up to 70% off
- **Chicfy** — Spanish startup (founded 2013); women's second-hand specialist; balanced buy/sell ecosystem
  - Founders invested €5,000 initial capital
  - No specific user/GMV data published
  - Source: [BBVA — Chicfy](https://www.bbva.com/en/chicfy-startup-dresses-second-hand-success/), [Modaes — Vestiaire Collective España](https://www.modaes.com/empresa/vestiaire-collective-se-sube-a-la-ola-del-ecommerce-en-espana-para-generar-el-5-de-sus-ventas-en-el-pais)

### Vertical Niches: Kids' Clothing
- **Platforms:**
  - **Micolet** — Thousands of children's items reviewed by hand
  - **Mooms** — Kids clothes + toys, strollers, chairs, home accessories
  - **Percentil** — Specialized in women/children clothing; bag-based selling (min 10 items)
  - **Vinted Kids Section** — Part of main platform
  - Source: [Micolet](https://www.micolet.com/blog/apps-ropa-segunda-mano-ninos/)

- **Market Gap:** No dominant specialist in kids second-hand (unlike adult fashion); opportunity for vertical focus

## HIGHEST OPPORTUNITY NICHES (Verdict)

1. **Bank Fee Reclamation** — Completely underserved; clear pain point (60%+ unaware)
2. **Private Pensions (Consumer-Friendly)** — 60% anxiety; low adoption; no friendly app
3. **Kids' Second-Hand Clothing** — Vertical gap in mature category; high repeat purchase rate
4. **On-Demand Home Services (Subscription Focus)** — Growing 18% CAGR; current players weak on recurring model
5. **Digital Will Management** — Legal infrastructure ready; no consumer platform
