## Risk Assessment

### High Severity
- **Knot integrates Zankyou (2024-2025):**
  - Threat: Free 200K+ vendor directory kills pricing power
  - Mitigation: Speed to market (12-mo window); vendor community lock-in; LATAM expansion Y2

### Medium Severity
- **Instagram/Google improve organic:** Couples discover vendors for free
  - Mitigation: Curation edge (verified reviews); comparison tools; ease of use

- **Low repeat frequency:** Couples book 1 wedding lifetime
  - Mitigation: Expand to corporate events, anniversaries, vow renewals (10x frequency)

- **Vendor churn:** If vendor doesn't get bookings, they quit
  - Mitigation: Commission model = zero upfront risk; vendor dashboards improve retention

### Low Severity
- **WhatsApp dependency:** Platform loses data if vendor uses WhatsApp directly
  - Mitigation: Offer in-app messaging alternative; both channels work
