## 1. Grief & Bereavement Tech (Death Tech)

### Market Fundamentals

**Mortality Context:**
- Spain: 439,000 deaths in 2024 vs 322,000 births (36% more deaths than births)
  - Source: [Statista Deaths in Spain 2024](https://www.statista.com/statistics/450171/number-of-deaths-in-spain/)
- Crude death rate: 9.58 per 1,000 population (2024) — highest in 20 years
  - Source: [Macrotrends Spain Death Rate](https://www.macrotrends.net/global-metrics/countries/esp/spain/death-rate)

**Funeral Market Value:**
- Spain funeral & cremation services: €2,060.2M (2024) → €3,279.1M (2030)
- CAGR 2025-2030: 8.1%
  - Source: [Grand View Research Spain Funeral Market](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/horizon/outlook/funeral-and-cremation-services-market/spain)

### Existing Players

**Spain-based:**
- **Vivo Recuerdo** — Digital memorial service for funeral homes (49" screens displaying photos + messages during wakes)
  - Sells to funeral homes, not direct-to-consumer
  - Sources: [Sifted Death Tech Corona](https://sifted.eu/articles/death-tech-corona), [LinkedIn Grief Tech Article](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/grief-tech-implications-digital-afterlife-death-dead-liu-m-f-a--q4a9e)

**Global Players:**
- GriefTech.com (international platform)
- US/UK platforms: BetterHelp ecosystem, various memorial apps (none explicitly verified in Spain market)
  - Source: [Grief Tech Documentary](https://www.gingerliu.com/grief-tech-documentary-addresses-death-and-the-digital-afterlife/)

### Digital Transformation Signals

- European funeral industry seeing acceleration of digital memorials, live-streaming ceremonies post-COVID
- Families increasingly want virtual guestbooks, custom memorial websites, participation from distant relatives
- Spain specifically identified as "growing market with strong focus on digitalization" in funeral services
  - Source: [Globe Newswire European Funeral Markets](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/03/19/3045172/0/en/Funeral-Services-Industry-Forecast-Report-2025-2030-Digital-Memorialization-and-Green-Burials-are-Transforming-the-Funeral-Services-Market-Reaching-Revenues-of-103-5-Billion-by-2030.html)

### Post-Mortem Admin Gap (Underserved)

**Global Solutions Exist:**
- EstateExec, Estateably (probate/estate admin)
- Eternal Dashboard (digital estate management)
  - Source: [The Legal Practice Estate Admin Software 2026](https://thelegalpractice.com/tools/best-estate-administration-software/)

**Spain-Specific Gap:**
- No verified Spain-specific platform for post-death account closure (banks, subscriptions, utilities)
- Digital asset management still fragmented across US/UK tools
- Spain has unique legal framework (SEPA transfers, Redsys, local banks) not covered by generic tools
  - Sources: [Estateably](https://www.estateably.com/), [Eternal](https://eternal.me/index.php?rp=/knowledgebase/)

## 2. Caregiver Support & Coordination Platform

### Caregiver Population

**Scale of Informal Care:**
- 1 in 5 Spaniards aged 50+ provide informal care (unpaid)
- Estimated 7.5M informal caregivers in Spain (majorly women)
- 45-64 age group: up to 61% of all caregivers
  - Source: [Nature Scientific Reports Quality of Life Spanish Caregivers](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-08067-2)

**Relationship Distribution:**
- 60%+ are spouses caring for partners
- 18% are adult children caring for parents
  - Source: [PMC Caregiver Burden Predictors Spain](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36102835/)

### Alzheimer's-Specific Data

- 200,000+ people with Alzheimer's in Spanish households receiving informal care (43% increase since 2008)
- Average care hours: 86-101 per week = €31,584–€37,019/year value per caregiver
  - Source: [European Journal of Health Economics Alzheimer's Care Spain](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10198-024-01713-y)

### Mental Health Burden

- Majority of caregivers = women (65% informal, 83% formal care)
- Health-related quality of life significantly impaired in caregivers (documented in multiple studies)
- No mention of widely-adopted Spanish caregiver coordination apps in literature
  - Sources: [García-Mayor Caregiver Lifestyle](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/fare.12689), [Wiley Health Quality of Life](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/28780713/)

### US/UK Players Operating in Spain?

**CareZone:**
- Features: Medication tracking, task lists, document storage, family sharing
- No verified Spain availability in search results
  - Source: [Medical Guardian Tech Savvy Apps](https://www.medicalguardian.com/medical-alert-blog/technology/tech-savvy-apps-for-caregiver-support)

**Lotsa Helping Hands:**
- Features: Volunteer coordination, meal prep, transportation, appointment booking
- No verified Spain availability in search results
  - Source: [Happy To Help Caregiving](https://happytohelpcaregiving.com/10-essential-caregiver-calendar-apps-for-family-coordination/)

**InCARE (European Pilot):**
- Non-professional care relationships, well-being focus
- Pilot phase, not widely commercialized
  - Source: [InCARE Ongoing Training Spain](https://incare.euro.centre.org/ongoing-training-for-caregivers-in-spain/)

## 3. Career Retraining Platform for 40+ Displaced Workers

### Target Population

**Digitalization Displacement:**
- 40-55 year old Spaniards being pushed out by digital transformation
- No specific population numbers found, but implicit in SEPE program existence
  - Source: [CEDEFOP Spain Digital Skills](https://www.cedefop.europa.eu/en/news/spain-promotion-training-digital-skills-workers)

### Government Programs (SEPE)

**SEPE (Servicio Público de Empleo Estatal):**
- Offers free/subsidized vocational training courses
- Online delivery available
- Covers: digital marketing, programming, business management, languages
  - Source: [CEDEFOP SEPE Initiatives](https://www.cedefop.europa.eu/en/news/spain-prepara-retraining-programme-people-no-longer-eligible-regular-benefits)

**Digitalízate & Digitalízate Plus:**
- Free e-learning platform for digital skills (launched 2019, expanded 2021)
- Multi-level, multiple languages
  - Source: [CEDEFOP Spain Boosting Digital Skills](https://www.cedefop.europa.eu/en/news/spain-boosting-tools-upskilling-and-reskilling-citizens-digital-skills)

**eFundae (FUNDAE):**
- Free online training platform for workers, jobseekers, companies
- Topics: digital skills, entrepreneurship, sustainability, transversal competencies
  - Source: [Digital Skills Jobs Platform eFundae Spain](https://digital-skills-jobs.europa.eu/en/inspiration/good-practices/efundae-spain)

### Market Gaps

**Issues with Existing Programs:**
- Government programs are fragmented (SEPE, eFundae, Digitalízate)
- No consolidated "career coaching + retraining" platform found
- Career coaching + skills + job matching = not integrated in public offerings
- SEPE reach limited (long wait times, bureaucracy typical in public systems)

**International Comparisons:**
- US: AARP, Encore.org (dedicated 40+ retraining)
- Spain: No equivalent private/consumer platform identified in research
  - Source: [Autonomo Spain SEPE Definition](https://www.autonomospain.com/what-is-sepe/)

## 4. Online Mental Health / Therapy & Burnout Apps

### Supply-Side Crisis

**Psychiatrist Shortage:**
- Spain: ~12 psychiatrists per 100,000 population (lowest in Europe)
- Regional variation: 1.6 (Extremadura) to 7.2 (Asturias) per 100,000
- EU average: 18–20 per 100,000
  - Source: [PMC Psychiatry in Spain](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6733142/)

**Wait Times:**
- Long wait times + limited talk therapy in public health system
- No specific queue length numbers found, but stated as chronic problem
  - Source: [Expatica Mental Health Spain](https://www.expatica.com/es/healthcare/healthcare-services/mental-health-in-spain-170747/)

### Mental Health Prevalence

**Baseline Mental Health:**
- 22% of Spanish population suffers ≥1 mental health condition (2020 data)
- 14% anxiety, 10% depression, 6% other conditions
- Antidepressant consumption tripled over 20 years = highest in Europe
- Spain reports highest anxiolytic consumption globally
  - Source: [WHO Mental Health Atlas 2020 Spain](https://www.who.org/publications/m/item/mental-health-atlas-2020-country-profile--spain), [Statista Mental Health Spain](https://www.statista.com/topics/8060/mental-health-in-spain/)

### Burnout (Healthcare Workers)

**COVID-Era Data:**
- Peak burnout: 43.4% of healthcare workers (2020-2021)
- COVID vs non-COVID workers: 50% vs 35% burnout rate
- 1 in 4 physicians experience professional burnout (systematic review, Carlos III Institute)
  - Source: [Frontiers Evolution of Burnout Spain](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/medicine/articles/10.3389/fmed.2025.1522134/abstract), [PMC Burnout COVID Spain](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7907836/)

**Post-Pandemic Trends:**
- Emotional exhaustion decreased
- Depersonalization increased, self-fulfillment decreased (2020-2022 cohort)
- WHO: At least 1 in 4 health workers report anxiety/depression/burnout (no improvement since 2022)
  - Source: [WHO Health Care Workers Mental Health](https://www.who.int/news/item/25-04-2024-202404_protecthw_mentalhealth)

### Existing Global Platforms

**BetterHelp & Competitors:**
- Talkspace, Online-Therapy.com, SonderMind (US-focused)
- Europe presence limited

**Europe-Specific Options:**
- **It's Complicated:** Active in London, Hamburg, Munich, Amsterdam, Lisbon, Vienna. Therapists speak 100+ languages including Spanish.
  - Source: [It's Complicated Europe Therapy](https://complicated.life/blog/best-alternative-therapy-platform-to-betterhelp-2026/)

- **Mindler:** Hybrid therapy + diagnostic (psychologists issue diagnoses). CBT, anxiety, depression focus.
  - Source: [It's Complicated Best Therapy Europe 2026](https://complicated.life/blog/comparing-the-best-online-therapy-services-europe-2026/)

**Spain-Specific Search Gap:**
- BetterHelp Spain availability NOT confirmed
- No native Spanish online therapy unicorn identified
- Waitlists at public system remain problematic

## 5. Slow Tourism / Hyperlocal Experience Booking

### Domestic Tourism Scale

**2025 Data:**
- International tourists: 96.8M (all-time high, +3.2% vs 2024)
- International tourism revenue: €134.7B (+6.8% vs 2024)
- Domestic tourism: "Remains at historically high levels" but lower growth rate than international
  - Source: [IndexBox Spain Tourism Record 2025](https://www.indexbox.io/blog/spain-breaks-tourism-record-with-968-million-visitors-in-2025/), [BNN Bloomberg Spain Tourism 2026](https://www.bnnbloomberg.ca/business/2026/02/03/spain-sets-a-new-tourism-record-with-nearly-96-8-million-foreign-visitors-in-2025/)

**Regional Distribution:**
- Catalonia: 20.1M tourists
- Mediterranean islands + Canary Islands: Major hubs
- Top source countries: UK (19M), France (12.7M), Germany (12M) — mostly international
  - Source: [La Moncloa Tourism Spending 2024](https://www.lamoncloa.gob.es/lang/en/gobierno/news/paginas/2025/20250203-international-tourists-spending.aspx)

### Slow Tourism Movement (Global)

**Definition & Trend:**
- Disconnect from rush, focus on connection to surroundings, culture, people
- Originated in Italy 1980s, now necessity for sustainable travel
- Emerging preference for authentic, sustainable, conscious travel
  - Source: [Spain.info Slow Tourism](https://www.spain.info/en/top/spain-slow-tourism-travelling-no-rush/)

**Featured Spanish Slow Tourism Destinations:**
- El Hierro (Canary Islands): Self-sufficient island, Biosphere Reserve, 46 dive sites
- La Graciosa: No paved roads
- Minorca: Outdoor painting, local food markets
- Asturias: Forests, sea, mountains, rivers, local cuisine
- Huesca: Rural cottages (Pyrenees), gastronomic & eco-tourism options
  - Source: [ARTIEM Hotels Slow Travel Spain](https://www.artiemhotels.com/en/blog/slow-tourism-a-more-sustainable-pleasant-way-to-travel)

### Local Experience Platforms

**Spain-based Operators:**
- **Northern Spain Travel:** Culinary journeys, slow travel, private guides to local winemakers, chefs, artists
- **Totally Spain:** Licensed travel agency since 2000, 45K+ travelers served, custom itineraries
- **Spain Savvy:** Boutique company, custom itineraries + full booking
- **Spain Private Guides:** Tours, itineraries, consultations
- **Barcelona Slow Travel:** Established player in Catalonia
  - Source: [Spain.info Slow Tourism](https://www.spain.info/en/top/spain-slow-tourism-travelling-no-rush/)

### Airbnb Alternatives Preferred by Spanish Residents

**Domestic Market Preference:**
- Spaniards increasingly avoid Airbnb (seen as expensive, for tourists)
- Prefer direct bookings of "apartamentos turísticos"
- Local platforms preferred over global intermediaries
  - Source: [Gamin Traveler Why Spaniards Don't Use Airbnb](https://www.gamintraveler.com/2026/01/27/the-airbnb-alternative-spanish-travelers-use-that-americans-havent-discovered/)

**Popular Platforms:**
- **Rentalia:** Offshoot of idealista, owner-run, eliminating intermediaries
- **Fairbnb.coop:** Cooperative model, ethical tourism focus
- **EcoBnb:** Sustainability-focused, 10 criteria minimum, strong in Andalusia
- **Plum Guide:** Selectivity, luxury vetting
- **Idealista, Fotocasa Vacacional:** Domestic platforms, legal compliance verified
- **Casas Rurales:** Rural/country houses (farmhouses, caves), nightly rates <$20/person
  - Source: [Idealista News Airbnb Alternatives](https://www.idealista.com/en/news/lifestyle-in-spain/2025/05/05/843430-best-alternatives-to-airbnb-in-spain), [Islands Casas Rurales Alternative](https://www.islands.com/1957845/airbnb-alternative-casas-rurales-escape-city-crowds-spains-secret-affordable/)

### Market Gap

**Hyperlocal Experiences Integration:**
- Existing platforms focus on accommodation
- No integrated "find local artisan workshops + cooking classes + homestays" marketplace identified
- Combination of experience booking + slow travel focus + domestic market = underserved
- Northern Spain Travel, Totally Spain do this manually, not via scalable platform
  - Source: [Northern Spain Travel](https://northernspaintravel.com/), [Totally Spain](https://www.totallyspain.com/)
