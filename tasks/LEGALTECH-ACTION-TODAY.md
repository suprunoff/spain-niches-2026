## SCRIPT TEMPLATES

### Interview Opener (Finiquito)
```
"Hi [name], I'm building an AI calculator for severance pay in Spain.
I'm talking with 20 people who were recently dismissed to understand the pain.
Would you have 15 minutes this week to help?"

When confirmed, call and ask:
1. When were you dismissed? Details?
2. Did you calculate finiquito yourself or hire a lawyer?
3. Were you confident in the result?
4. How much did you estimate vs what you should get?
5. Would you pay €20 to talk with a lawyer before accepting?"
```

### Interview Opener (Due Diligence)
```
"Hi [name], I'm researching how real estate lawyers do due diligence.
Currently doing some interviews to understand pain points.
Would you have 15 minutes for a quick call?"

When confirmed, ask:
1. Walk me through your last due diligence (what steps, how long?)
2. What's the most time-consuming part?
3. What's the biggest risk you've seen (missed cargas, wrong plus, etc)?
4. If I built a tool that automated this, would you use it?
5. How much would you pay per property check?"
```

### Lawyer Recruitment (Finiquito)

Workers get: AI-powered finiquito calculation + lawyer verification (€20)
You get: Pre-qualified leads + 30% commission

Interested in a 5-min call to discuss?"

## WATCH OUT FOR (Red Flags)

🚩 **Interviewing only your friends/network**
   → Ask for introductions to strangers only

🚩 **Building full MVP before validation**
   → Test landing page first (cheap, fast feedback)

🚩 **Not talking to 5+ people per segment**
   → Need minimum 5 to spot patterns, not 1-2

🚩 **Ignoring "No" responses**
   → If 70% say "No pain", pivot to different niche

🚩 **Over-optimizing landing page**
   → Simple > pretty. Speed > perfection. Validate > polish.
