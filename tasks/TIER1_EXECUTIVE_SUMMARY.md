- **Hotmart competition**: 12-18 month window before market dominance

## Unit Economics Highlight

All three have exceptional LTV/CAC ratios (healthy > 3:1, excellent > 10:1):

- **Crypto Tax**: 45:1 LTV/CAC (freemium + regulatory urgency = low CAC)
- **E-Commerce**: 21:1 LTV/CAC (marketplace seller communities = low CAC)
- **Spanish Infobiz**: 156:1 LTV/CAC (coaching communities, affiliate potential)

**Bottom line**: Acquisition is cheap, retention is strong (regulatory lock-in or switching costs).
