### NICHE A: Digital Nomad Visa Ecosystem (ID=11)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Market Size:
  • 32K+ visas issued since 2023, 7.8K in 2025, 40% YoY growth
  • 14,255 main visa holders (remote workers, not freelancers)
  • Spain = #1 destination globally 2025-2026
  • €200-300M annual economic activity from nomads
  • Addressable: ~14,255 annual new visa holders + 50K+ broader nomads

Customer Pain Points (Validated):
  1. NIE (Foreigner ID): 2-8 weeks process, required for bank + tax + work
  2. Bank Account: €0-3K deposit, 1-3 weeks after NIE
  3. Tax Complexity: 24% Beckham Law (non-residents) vs 45% (residents), requires gestora
  4. Housing: Must have on visa app, long-term rentals require deposit + pagaré
  5. Visa Paperwork: Consulate queues 2-6 weeks, apostille needed

Product Idea:
  "Spain Digital Nomad OS" — integrated platform (NIE tracker + tax calc + housing + community)

  Features:
  • DNV application tracker (docs checklist, deadlines, status)
  • NIE appointment bot (scrape Policía Nacional scheduler, notify slots)
  • Beckham Law tax calculator (input income, show €savings vs normal tax)
  • Housing finder (filter Idealista/Airbnb, realtor contacts)
  • Expat community hub (forum + events + resource library)

Monetization:
  • SaaS Tier 1 (Basic): €4.99/month — Freemium (NIE tracker, tax calc)
  • SaaS Tier 2 (Pro): €14.99/month — Gestora marketplace, housing integration
  • Gestora Affiliate: 15-25% commission on first month fee → €50-100K/year potential
  • Bank Referral (Wise, N26): €30-50 per signup
  • Coworking Partner Affiliate: €10-20 per booking
  • Premium Community (coaching): €29/month (2-3% of users)

Unit Economics:
  • CAC: €2-5 (organic SEO, forums, affiliate partnerships)
  • Avg Subscription: €8-12/month (SaaS + affiliate blended)
  • Churn: 10-12%/month (visa cohort ages out after 8-12 mths)
  • Conversion to Paid: 8-12% of signups
  • Y1 Revenue: €80-100K | Y2: €200K | Y3: €400K

Score: 7.69 → **8.08/10** (↑ +0.39 after research)
  d=8 (strong demand, 32K growth), g=9 (no solution exists), r=7 (stable but inspections rising)
  s=9 (high search), m=7 (TAM €200-300M), a=9 (gestora+bank affiliate), f=6 (not solo-simple)
  t=8 (fast monetization)

Go/No-Go Signals:
  ✅ GO: Market proven (32K visas), pain points acute (NIE, tax, housing), affiliate revenue, high LTV
  ⚠️ CAUTION: High churn (10-12%/month), no competitors = no validation, visa policy risk

Recommendation: SECONDARY LAUNCH (Q4 2026, after WhatsApp proves team can execute)


### NICHE B: WhatsApp-First Automation for Spanish SMBs (ID=10)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Market Size:
  • 3.5M SMBs in Spain, 76% service-based = 800K-1M targets
  • 30M+ WhatsApp users (64% penetration), 80%+ use WA for business comms
  • Only 5-15% have WhatsApp Business API (white space!)
  • TAM: €600-900M/year (avg €75/month × 800K-1M SMBs)

Customer Pain Points (Validated):
  1. Clinics: 15% no-show rate = €100K+ annual revenue loss
  2. Appointments: Manual scheduling, missed messages, lost customers
  3. Patient Communication: Appointment reminders, treatment follow-ups, payment asks
  4. Team Coordination: Multiple staff handling WA = chaos, missed messages
  5. Automation Cost: Competitors like Treble charge $200-500/month (expensive for SMBs)

Best Vertical (Clinic-First Launch):
  • 6,500 dental clinics in Spain, 3,000-4,000 are SMBs (1-5 dentists)
  • Pain: 15% no-show (clinic loses 10-15% revenue annually)
  • Price Tolerance: €100-400/month (can ROI in 2-3 weeks if solves no-shows)
  • WA Setup Cost: €150, training 1-2 hours
  • Payback: 2-4 weeks
  • Expected no-show reduction: 15% → 3% (80% improvement, proven by AIONIX)
  • Sales uplift: 27% average, conversion +20%

Product Idea:
  "Clinic Assistant" — WhatsApp automation for dental clinics

  Features (MVP, 4 weeks):
  • Appointment chatbot (customer texts → bot shows available slots → confirms)
  • Integrations (Dentrix, Oryx, QDent — major Spain clinic software)
  • Appointment reminders (24h before)
  • Post-visit reminders (follow-ups, payment collection)
  • Receptionist dashboard (unified WA messages, avoid scattered chats)
  • Feedback automation (request Google reviews)

Monetization:
  • SaaS Tier 1: €49/month (Bot + 1 integration)
  • SaaS Tier 2: €99/month (Dashboard + advanced templates + upsell)
  • Setup Fee: €150 one-time
  • Message Overage: €0.05 per message beyond 100/month
  • Dental Software Affiliate: 10% of annual fee

Unit Economics:
  • CAC: €30-50 (Google Ads, dental forums, software partnerships)
  • ARPU: €60-100/month blended
  • Churn: 5-8%/month (sticky if working, reducing no-shows)
  • Conversion to Paid: ~37% of signups
  • CAC Payback: 2-3 months
  • Y1 Revenue: €280-400K | Y2: €950K | Y3: €2.5M

Score: 7.53 → **8.41/10** (↑ +0.88 after research)
  d=9 (3.5M SMBs), g=8 (LeadSales exists but not vertical-specific), r=8 (stable API)
  s=9 (high search volume, €2-5 Google CPC), m=9 (€600M+ TAM), a=6 (modest affiliate)
  f=8 (solo-buildable MVP in 4-6 weeks), t=9 (fast monetization, €2.9K MRR at 50 customers)

Go/No-Go Signals:
  ✅ GO: Market proven (80% use WA, high pain), fast product (4 weeks), quick payback (2-3 months)
         revenue grows 3x Y1→Y2, vertical expansion path clear
  ⚠️ CAUTION: Fragmented tech stack (each clinic uses different software), SMB sales slow (2-4 months)
             WhatsApp API pricing may increase, churn if not solving problem

Competitors:
  • LeadSales (Spain-first, official Meta partner): €50-300/month, strong but generalist
  • Whaticket: €30-200/month, Brazil-heavy, self-hosted
  • Treble.ai: $200-500/month, expensive for SMBs
  • Cliengo, Bitrix24: Omni-channel but not clinic-focused

Recommendation: PRIMARY LAUNCH (Q2 2026, start immediately)
