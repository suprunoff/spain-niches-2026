# Padel Club Validation Interview Script

**Goal:** Validate if Club Operations SaaS solves a real, urgent problem for padel clubs in Spain.

**Target:** 15-20 club managers/directors (4-8 court clubs, Madrid/Barcelona/Valencia)
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Format:** Video call (Zoom) or WhatsApp call
**Language:** Spanish (English acceptable in Madrid)

---

## Pre-Interview Preparation

### Finding Clubs
1. **Facebook Groups:** "Padel Clubs Spain", "Club Managers Padel"
2. **LinkedIn:** Search "Club Director Padel Spain" + message
3. **WhatsApp:** Ask for referrals from Padel Nuestro, TPC Match Point users
4. **Direct:** Google Maps "Padel Club [City]" → call/WhatsApp

## Interview Flow

### 1. Icebreaker (5 min)
**Goal:** Build rapport, understand their role

- "Tell me about [Club Name] — how many courts, how old, your role?"
- "How long have you been managing the club?"
- "What's your favorite part of the job?" (warm-up)

### 2. Current System Pain (10 min)
**Goal:** Understand current workflow, friction points

#### Booking System
- "How do players currently book courts at your club?"
  - Playtomic? TPC Match Point? Phone? Walk-in?
- "What do you like about [current system]?"
- "What frustrates you about [current system]?"
  - ⚠️ Listen for: commission, complexity, lack of features

#### Pricing Management
- "How do you set prices for court bookings?"
  - Fixed €28/hour? Peak hours higher? Weekends different?
- "Do you adjust prices based on demand or time of year?"
- "How do you handle seasonal pricing (summer vs winter)?"
- "Have you ever wanted to do dynamic pricing but couldn't?"

#### Subscription/Membership
- "Do you offer membership packages or season passes?"
- "How do you track member balances/usage?"
- "Do members ever ask for refunds or have disputes about their balance?"
- "How much time do you spend monthly on member management?"

### 3. Playtomic-Specific Pain (8 min)
**Goal:** Quantify the commission problem (this is our entry point)

#### Commission Impact
- "Are you using Playtomic for bookings?"
- "How many bookings per month through Playtomic? (estimate)"
- "What's Playtomic's commission fee?" (listen if they know)
- **KEY:** "How much do you estimate Playtomic costs you per month in commission?"
  - Calculate: bookings × €30 avg price × 5-10% commission
  - Example: 1,200 bookings/month × €30 × 7% = €2,520/month

#### Manager Features
- "Do you use Playtomic Manager?" (their club management tool)
- "What features do you use?" (if yes)
- "What's missing or frustrating?" (if any)
- "Would you consider switching to another system?"

### 4. The Problem (6 min)
**Goal:** Confirm if they perceive the problem as urgent

- "If you could reduce Playtomic commission, how much would that save you annually?"
- "How would you invest those savings?" (shows priority)
- "On a scale 1-10, how frustrated are you with Playtomic pricing?"
  - 1-5: Acceptable loss
  - 6-8: Annoying, but living with it
  - 9-10: Major pain point, considering alternatives

### 5. Solution Validation (8 min)
**Goal:** Test if they'd consider an alternative

**Pitch (read naturally):**
> "What if there was a platform designed specifically for padel clubs that offers:
>
> 1. **Dynamic pricing** — automatically charge more during peak hours (evenings, weekends) without you having to manage it manually
> 2. **Membership/subscription management** — auto-track balance, send renewal reminders, integrate with Bizum for easy payment
> 3. **Analytics** — see which time slots are most popular, which coaches attract the most students, churn analysis
> 4. **Zero commission** — unlike Playtomic, no fee per booking
> 5. **Still integrated with Playtomic** — players still find you on Playtomic, but management happens in our tool
>
> **Price:** €120-150/month for a 4-8 court club.
>
> Would that be interesting to you?"

#### Follow-up Questions
- "Which features excite you most?"
- "Which features wouldn't you use?"
- "Would you consider paying €120/month if it saves you €500-800/month in Playtomic commission?"
- "What would make you NOT switch?"
- "When would you want to implement this?" (urgency)

### 6. Willingness to Pay (5 min)
**Goal:** Validate pricing

- "What's your current annual spend on management software?"
  - Playtomic Manager: €100-200/month?
  - Other tools: €?
- "What's the maximum you'd pay per month for a solution like this?"
- "Would you prefer per-booking commission (like Playtomic) or fixed monthly fee?"

### 7. Follow-up & Close (2 min)

**Ask:**
- "Can I follow up in 2 weeks with a prototype/beta?"
- "Could you introduce me to other club managers you know?"
- "What's the best way to stay in touch?" (WhatsApp? Email?)

**Thank you & incentive:**
- "Thanks for your time! Here's a €20 Amazon gift card code: [XXXX]"

## Success Criteria for Interview

### Must-Have Signals ✅
- [ ] Using Playtomic (or aware of it)
- [ ] Can estimate commission cost (€500-1,000+/month)
- [ ] Rates frustration with Playtomic 7+ out of 10
- [ ] Expresses interest in alternative (6+ out of 10)
- [ ] Would consider switching for €100-150/month savings

### Nice-to-Have Signals ✅
- [ ] Already tried other software (means market exists)
- [ ] Wants dynamic pricing (shows advanced thinking)
- [ ] Asks about specific integrations (Bizum, WhatsApp)
- [ ] Offers to be beta tester

### Kill Signals 🚩
- [ ] Says "Playtomic is fine, no problems"
- [ ] Can't estimate commission or says <€300/month
- [ ] Rates frustration 3-4 out of 10
- [ ] Says "we're too small for another tool"
- [ ] Unwilling to try anything new

## Red Flags During Interview

⚠️ **Stop if:**
- They ask "Why aren't you selling Playtomic features instead?"
- They say "we have a custom developer building our system"
- They mention they're already building this in-house
- They ask about B2B2C model (might be competitor)

✅ **Continue if:**
- They ask about timeline ("When can we start beta?")
- They ask about specific pricing for their club
- They offer referrals unprompted
- They ask about annual discounts

## Tips for Success

✅ **Do:**
- Call on WhatsApp (higher response rate than email in Spain)
- Offer something (€20 Amazon gift card, priority beta access)
- Be genuine (you're solving a real problem, not just selling)
- Take notes during call (shows you care)
- Follow up within 48 hours

❌ **Don't:**
- Pitch too early (listen first, pitch second)
- Ignore red flags (kill ideas early)
- Ask for payment/contracts (too early)
- Oversell the MVP
- Forget to ask permission to follow up
