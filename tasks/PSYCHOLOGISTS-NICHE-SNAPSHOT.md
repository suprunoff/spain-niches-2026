## Unit Economics (Simplified)

### B2C Marketplace
- Patient pays: €50/session
- Platform takes: 15% = €7.50
- **Revenue per therapist: €400/month (200 patients/month)**
- Scale: 200 therapists = €960K/year gross revenue
- Margin: 58%

### B2B EAP
- Contract size: €150K/year
- CAC: €4K
- LTV: €1.2M (8 years)
- **LTV/CAC: 300x** (exceptional)
