## Executive Summary

This document maps **20 US consumer/B2C startups & platforms** that achieved clear product-market-fit (2023-2025) but remain **largely absent or underpenetrated in Spain**. Spain is 3-7 years behind US digital adoption—this is a replication window.

## Key Patterns: Why Spain Lags

### 1. **Language + Localization**
- Every consumer app needs Spanish-language content, avatars, bots, coaches
- Translation ≠ localization (cultural nuances matter)

### 2. **Payment Infrastructure**
- Spain = Bizum for payments (not BNPL)
- EWA requires employer integration (SMB economy weak on tech)

### 3. **Regulatory Friction**
- RGPD stricter than GDPR on minors + mental health data
- Legal tech requires civil law adaptation (not just translation)

### 4. **Structural Differences**
- Mental health stigma still high (digital adoption lower than US)
- SMB-heavy (fewer large employers → EWA adoption hard)
- Subscription model = cultural adoption lag

### 5. **Creator Economy**
- Spain = fewer professional creators (vs. US TikTok/YouTube density)
- Niche communities (tabletop, 3D design) = nascent
