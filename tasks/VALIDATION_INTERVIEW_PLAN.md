## NICHE 1: E-Commerce Seller Compliance (VeriFactu)

### Interviewee Profile
- **Minimum criteria**: Active seller on Amazon.es (FBA or FBM)
- **Preferred**: Sells on 2+ platforms (Miravia, Etsy, Allegro, eBay.es)
- **Revenue level**: €5K-500K/year (PYME segment)
- **Pain point signal**: Mentions invoicing, compliance, accounting, VeriFactu

### Interview Script (20-30 minutes)

#### Discovery (5 min)
"Tell me about your e-commerce business. How many marketplaces do you sell on? What's your annual revenue?"

#### Pain Points (8 min)
1. **Invoicing**: "How do you currently handle invoicing across all your marketplaces?"
   - Current tool/process? Manual? Outsourced?
   - Time spent per week on invoicing?
   - Pain level (1-10)?

2. **VeriFactu**: "Have you heard of VeriFactu? What do you know about it?"
   - Awareness level?
   - Concern about Jan 2027 deadline?
   - Currently preparing (yes/no/unsure)?

3. **Compliance**: "How much are you currently spending on accounting/tax compliance?"
   - Annual cost?
   - Who handles (accountant, software, DIY)?
   - Satisfaction with current solution (1-10)?

#### Willingness to Pay (4 min)
4. **Pricing**: "If there was a tool that auto-generates VeriFactu invoices from all your marketplaces, what would you pay?"
   - Monthly subscription: €29 / €49 / €79 / €99 / €199?
   - One-time setup: €0 / €100 / €500 / €1,000?
   - Comparison: "More/less than your current accountant cost?"

#### Timeline & Urgency (3 min)
5. **Decision timeline**: "If this tool was available today, when would you buy?"
   - Immediately / Next 3 months / 6 months / After Jan 2027 / No interest?
   - Why that timeline?

#### Qualification Check
- **Pain confirmation**: "On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is solving invoicing across marketplaces?" (7+ = qualified)
- **WTP confirmation**: "Would you seriously consider a €49/mo solution?" (Yes = qualified)

### Target Channels
- Amazon Seller Central forums (Spanish section)
- r/ecommerce_es, r/emprendimiento Reddit communities
- WhatsApp seller groups (warm intro via seller associations)
- LinkedIn outreach to "Amazon seller Spain" profiles
- AECEM (Spanish e-commerce association) if accessible

### Success Metric
- **70%+ pain confirmation** (rate urgency 7+ on 1-10)
- **50%+ WTP confirmation** (willing to pay €49+/mo)
- **30%+ decision timeline** (would buy within 3 months)

## NICHE 2: Crypto Tax + MiCA Compliance

### Segment A: B2C (Individual Crypto Traders)

#### Interviewee Profile
- **Minimum criteria**: Has crypto holdings OR made crypto trades in 2025
- **Holdings**: €1K-€1M (retail/prosumer investor)
- **Trading**: At least 1 taxable event (buy/sell/stake) in 2025
- **Pain point signal**: Mentions taxes, AEAT, compliance, confusion about reporting

#### Interview Script (20-30 minutes)

##### Discovery (5 min)
"Tell me about your crypto portfolio. What exchanges do you use? Are you a hodler or active trader?"

##### Pain Points (8 min)
1. **Tax Confusion**: "Do you know how much crypto tax you owe for 2025?"
   - Knows exact amount / rough estimate / no idea?
   - Confidence level (1-10)?

2. **Reporting**: "Have you heard of Modelo 100 or Modelo 721 for crypto?"
   - Awareness (yes/no/confused)?
   - Filed before (yes/no/unsure)?
   - Current approach (accountant / software / manual / ignore)?

3. **Compliance Anxiety**: "Are you worried about AEAT crypto audits or DAC8 reporting?"
   - Worry level (1-10)?
   - Specific concern?

#### Willingness to Pay (4 min)
4. **Pricing**: "If there was a tool that automatically calculates your crypto tax AND generates Modelo 100/721 reports, what would you pay?"
   - Annual: €19 / €49 / €79 / €99 / €199?
   - vs accountant cost (€500-1,500/year)?

#### Timeline & Urgency (3 min)
5. **Filing deadline**: "When do you need to file taxes?"
   - ASAP (before Q2) / Q1 (Jan-Mar) / Eventually / No hurry?

#### Qualification Check
- **Pain confirmation**: "On 1-10, how urgent is understanding your crypto tax?" (7+ = qualified)
- **WTP confirmation**: "Would you pay €49/year?" (Yes = qualified)

### Segment B: B2B (CASP / Crypto Exchange)

#### Interviewee Profile
- **Target**: Compliance officer, CTO, or founder at crypto exchange/broker in Spain
- **MiCA status**: Under transitional regime (deadline July 2026)
- **Size**: €50M-€1B AUM (mid-market CASP)
- **Pain point signal**: Mentions CNMV compliance, MiCA licensing, DAC8 reporting, AML/KYC

#### Interview Script (30-40 minutes)

##### Discovery (5 min)
"Tell me about your platform. How many users? What licenses/registrations do you have? MiCA status?"

##### Pain Points (12 min)
1. **MiCA Compliance**: "What's your plan for July 1, 2026 MiCA deadline?"
   - Already licensed / applying / undecided / confused?
   - Key challenges (capital, ops, tech)?

2. **DAC8 Reporting**: "Are you currently generating DAC8 reports for AEAT?"
   - Manual / semi-automated / fully automated?
   - Time/cost per report?
   - Confidence in accuracy?

3. **Compliance Infrastructure**: "What tools/platforms do you use for KYC/AML and reporting?"
   - Current stack (compliance software, manual processes)?
   - Gaps or pain points?

#### Willingness to Pay (4 min)
4. **Budget**: "If there was a platform to automate DAC8 generation + MiCA compliance documentation, what budget do you have?"
   - €1K-€3K/mo / €3K-€10K/mo / €10K+/mo?
   - Setup fee tolerance (€10K-€50K)?

#### Timeline & Urgency (3 min)
5. **Implementation**: "By when would you need this running?"
   - Immediately / Next 2 months / By July 2026 / Post-July?

#### Qualification Check
- **Pain confirmation**: "On 1-10, how critical is MiCA compliance?" (9-10 = qualified, regulatory requirement)
- **WTP confirmation**: "Would your budget allow €5K/mo + €20K setup?" (Yes = qualified for B2B tier)

### Target Channels
- CNMV registry of CASPs (public list, cold outreach)
- Spanish fintech associations (AEF, Asabif)
- Crypto industry news (CriptoNoticias, Bitcoin.es)
- LinkedIn: Search "CASP Spain" + "crypto exchange" + "compliance officer"
- Crypto user communities: r/CryptoES, Spanish crypto Twitter/X, Telegram groups

### Success Metric
- **B2C: 70%+ pain confirmation** (urgency 7+ on tax filing)
- **B2C: 50%+ WTP** (willing to pay €49-99/year)
- **B2B: 80%+ pain confirmation** (MiCA deadline = critical)
- **B2B: 60%+ WTP** (willing to allocate €5K+/mo)

## NICHE 3: Spanish Infobiz Platform (Kajabi Alternative)

### Interviewee Profile
- **Target**: Online coach, course creator, or consultant in Spain
- **Current setup**: Using Kajabi, Hotmart, or cobbled-together tools
- **Revenue**: €500-€50K/year from online courses/coaching (established or aspiring)
- **Pain point signal**: Mentions course hosting, email marketing, payments, student management

#### Interview Script (20-30 minutes)

##### Discovery (5 min)
"Tell me about your online course/coaching business. How much revenue? What tools do you use?"

##### Pain Points (8 min)
1. **Platform fragmentation**: "What tools do you use to run your coaching/course business?"
   - List all tools (email, payment, hosting, student management)?
   - Time integrating/managing them?
   - Pain level (1-10)?

2. **Payments & Bizum**: "How do your students pay you?"
   - Payment methods (cards, bank transfer, Bizum)?
   - Conversion rate if Bizum available? (if not currently available)
   - Pain with current payment setup?

3. **Course/Email Management**: "What's hardest about managing your students/course?"
   - Email sequences?
   - Video hosting?
   - Membership access control?
   - Student engagement tracking?

#### Willingness to Pay (4 min)
4. **Pricing**: "If there was ONE platform that did courses + email + payments (including Bizum) + student management, what would you pay?"
   - €49/mo / €79/mo / €99/mo / €199/mo?
   - vs current tool costs combined (estimate)?
   - Would you prefer: Flat fee OR percentage of revenue?

#### Timeline & Urgency (3 min)
5. **Switch decision**: "If this platform existed today, when would you switch?"
   - Immediately / Next 3 months / Next 6 months / Thinking about it / Not interested?
   - What would make you switch today?

#### Hotmart Awareness (2 min)
6. **Competition check**: "Are you aware of Hotmart? Would you consider it?"
   - Yes, using it / No / Heard of it?
   - If yes: What do you like/dislike?
   - Why not using (if not): Too cheap (free makes you suspicious)? Missing features?

#### Qualification Check
- **Pain confirmation**: "On 1-10, how much pain is your current setup?" (7+ = qualified)
- **WTP confirmation**: "Would you seriously consider €79/mo if it included Bizum?" (Yes = qualified)
- **Bizum importance**: "How important is Bizum support in your decision?" (8+ = validates key differentiator)

### Target Channels
- Spanish coaching communities (LinkedIn groups, Facebook groups)
- Spanish entrepreneur networks (Entrepreneur.es, Somiedo, Growth Hacking Spain)
- Kajabi user Facebook groups (Spanish section)
- Instagram/TikTok Spanish creators (DM direct outreach)
- WhatsApp warm intros via coach friends
- LinkedIn: Search "coach online" + "course creator" + "Spain"

### Success Metric
- **70%+ pain confirmation** (tool fragmentation = urgent)
- **50%+ WTP** (willing to pay €79+/mo)
- **60%+ Bizum importance** (validates key differentiator)
- **30%+ switch timeline** (would switch within 3 months)

## Interview Execution Checklist

### Pre-Interview (Day Before)
- [ ] Send calendar invite with Zoom link
- [ ] Share 1-page overview of product concept (optional, to frame discussion)
- [ ] Confirm timezone (Spain is UTC+1/+2)
- [ ] Record permission: "Is it OK to record this for internal reference?"

### During Interview
- [ ] Record (with permission)
- [ ] Take notes (backup if recording fails)
- [ ] Stay quiet during answers (don't bias)
- [ ] Ask follow-up: "Tell me more" if answer is vague
- [ ] End with: "Any other pain points I should know about?"

### Post-Interview (Same Day)
- [ ] Transcribe key quotes (use Otter.ai if available)
- [ ] Score pain confirmation (1-10) for key questions
- [ ] Note WTP answer + confidence
- [ ] Flag any surprising insights or red flags
- [ ] Send thank-you email + €5-10 gift card (if budget allows)
