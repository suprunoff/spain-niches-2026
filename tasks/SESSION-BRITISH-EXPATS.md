## Key Findings

### Why This Niche?

1. **Blue ocean:** Zero direct competitors specializing in British expats in Spain
2. **Post-Brexit pain:** NIE, residencia, UK pension tax, driving license — all painful bureaucratic problems
3. **High purchasing power:** Income in GBP, willing to pay €10-40/mo for solutions
4. **Affiliate goldmine:** Lawyers, tax advisors, insurance brokers all desperate for leads
5. **Network effects:** First-mover in British community = stickiness

### Unit Economics (Exceptional)

```
CAC: €2.50 (Facebook groups, Reddit, referral)
LTV: €687 (€17 ARPU × 48 months × 85% retention + €75 affiliate)
LTV:CAC: 275x (exceptional! 50x = good)

Year 2 projection (5,000 users, 1,000 paid):
- Revenue: €252K
- Profit: €112K (44% margin)
- Payback period: <1 month
```

### Top 3 Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Churn >20% (expats leave) | Medium | Affiliate lock-in, sticky features (tracker, calendar, forum) |
| Low willingness to pay | Low | Freemium model (50% free), corporate sponsorship |
| CPC inflation/Low conversion | Low | Organic (SEO), direct Facebook group partnerships |

**Biggest risk is churn.** Mitigation: Build community + affiliate ecosystem, not just portal.

### Competitive Advantage

1. **Language:** Only platform 100% British English (Expatica = generic, The Local = news)
2. **Specialization:** UK pension expertise, Brexit knowledge, driving license guidance
3. **Integration:** Bridge between British expats + Spanish service providers
4. **Timing:** Post-Brexit maturation (5 years → concrete, not transient pain)
