## Key Findings

### Why Spain FinTech Works

1. **5-7 year tech lag** → digital solutions = blue ocean
2. **Mandatory adoption** (VeriFactu) → 100% TAM guarantee
3. **Large underserved segments** (renters, non-residents, autónomos)
4. **WhatsApp first market** → 40-60% reply vs 1% cold email
5. **High price acceptance** → SME willing to pay €50-200/mth for compliance

### Market Validation

- Rental Insurance: Idealista proved demand (June 2025)
- Non-Resident Mortgage: 100K+ transactions/year, growing
- VeriFactu: 100% mandatory adoption by law, €3000+ penalty
- Open Banking: 95% banks ready, 18% SME adoption growing
- Embedded: €2-3B TAM, zero competition in Spain
