## 🎯 The Core Opportunity

**Problem:** Playtomic charges clubs 5-10% commission per booking
- Average club: €600-900/month in Playtomic fees
- No alternative solution exists

**Solution:** Club Operations SaaS
- Dynamic pricing (evening +20%, weekend +15%)
- Subscription management (auto-renewal, Bizum)
- Occupancy analytics
- Price: €120-150/month (save clubs €500+/month)
- NO commission on bookings

**Why It Works:**
- Clear ROI (€600-900 monthly savings)
- Weak competition (TPC expensive, Playtomic doesn't care)
- Sticky product (controls club revenue)
- Unit economics: LTV/CAC = 25x (ideal)

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Why This Wins
1. **Uncharged market:** No one solving club revenue optimization
2. **Clear pain:** Every club knows Playtomic takes 5-10%
3. **Weak competition:** TPC expensive, Playtomic doesn't care
4. **Spain-specific advantage:** Local team, Bizum, WhatsApp
5. **Scalable unit economics:** LTV/CAC 25x (best in B2B SaaS)

### Competitive Moat
- Not trying to replace Playtomic player app (they win there)
- Complementary to Playtomic (improves club margin)
- Too specific for Playtomic to add as feature (doesn't fit their model)

### Market Timing
- Padel clubs in Spain are YOUNG (built 2015-2025)
- Willing to try modern SaaS (mobile-first)
- Already using Playtomic (pain validated)
- Haven't found alternative yet (gap exists)
