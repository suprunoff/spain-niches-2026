**Date:** March 2, 2026
**Purpose:** Confirm JTBD, ARPU, and Go/No-Go assumptions before MVP

## NICHE 1: AUTÓNOMO COMPLIANCE BUNDLE

### Persona A: Foreign Autónomo (Primary)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-45
- Nationality: EU + LATAM (non-Spanish)
- Years in Spain: 1-5
- Visa type: Digital Nomad, Student/Residence, Work, Self-employed
- Income: €1,500-5,000/mo
- Tech comfort: High (uses Stripe, GitHub, Zapier)

**Screening Questions:**
1. "Are you self-employed or run a freelance business in Spain?"
2. "Have you registered as an autónomo with Spanish tax authorities?"
3. "Do you primarily invoice in English, or have language barriers with Spanish forms?"

**Interview Flow (30-45 minutes)**

#### Part 1: Problem Validation (10 min)
1. **Current process:** "Walk me through how you file your taxes each quarter. What's the most frustrating part?"
   - Look for: Gestor dependency, language confusion, time spent, fear of errors

2. **Trigger moment:** "What made you decide to do this yourself vs. hire a gestor?"
   - Look for: Cost concern (€200-500/mo), DIY preference, recent arrival (not yet aware of gestores)

3. **Frequency:** "How often do you invoice clients per month? How many different countries/currencies?"
   - Look for: High invoicing volume (5+/mo) = pain point

#### Part 2: Alternative Evaluation (8 min)
4. **Current tools:** "What tools do you currently use for invoicing, expense tracking, tax prep?"
   - Look for: Scattered tools (Stripe invoicing, personal spreadsheet, accountant email)
   - Note competitive awareness: "Have you heard of Holded, Quipu, or other Spanish tools?"

5. **Friction points:** "If you tried a Spanish tool (like Holded), what stopped you?"
   - Look for: Language, UX, lack of trust, missing features (gestor integration)

6. **Willingness to switch:** "What would make you want to consolidate these tools into one platform?"
   - Look for: Single dashboard, quarterly reminders, English support, peace of mind

#### Part 3: Pricing & ARPU (8 min)
7. **Price anchor:** "How much do you currently spend on tax/accounting per year?"
   - Expected range: €0 (DIY) to €3K (gestor)

8. **Willingness to pay:** "If a tool could save you 8 hours/quarter on tax prep + give you peace of mind about compliance, what would you pay per month?"
   - Probe with options:
     - "Would €9/mo work?" (compare to Billin)
     - "Would €29/mo work?" (freemium tier)
     - "Would €49/mo work?" (pro tier)
   - Expected answer: €20-50/mo

9. **Value perception:** "What features would be most valuable to you?"
   - Rank: Invoicing → Quarterly tax auto-calc → Hacienda validation → Gestor booking

#### Part 4: Urgency & Timeline (6 min)
10. **VeriFactu awareness:** "Have you heard about VeriFactu (electronic invoicing requirement Jan 2027)?"
    - If YES: "How worried are you about this deadline?"
    - If NO: "If your invoices had to be electronically filed by Jan 2027, would that change your approach?"
    - Look for: Urgency level (low/medium/high)

11. **Conversion timeline:** "If we launched a tool like this next month, when would you consider trying it?"
    - Expected: 1-3 months (after they research + compare)

12. **Referrals:** "Who else in your network (expat freelancers, coworkers, other autónomos) would benefit from this?"
    - Look for: Viral coefficient (word-of-mouth potential)

**Scoring Template:**

| Dimension | Evidence | Score |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Problem clarity** | "I'm confused every quarter about what to file; currently use gestor" | 9/10 |
| **Pain severity** | "I spend €300/mo on gestor; wish I could do 60% myself" | 8/10 |
| **Willingness to pay** | "€39/mo seems reasonable; currently pay €300/mo" | 8/10 |
| **Urgency** | "VeriFactu scares me; need solution by Nov 2026" | 9/10 |
| **Adoption timeline** | "I'd try it within 4-8 weeks" | 8/10 |
| **Viral potential** | "I'd tell 3-5 expat friends about this" | 7/10 |
| **GO/NO-GO** | **6+ out of 6 dimensions = GO** | ✓ |

### Persona B: Spanish Autónomo (Secondary)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 30-55
- Nationality: Spanish
- Self-employment type: Freelancer, consultant, tradesperson
- Income: €1,200-3,000/mo
- Tech comfort: Medium (uses email, maybe mobile banking)

**Screening Questions:**
1. "¿Eres autónomo en España?"
2. "¿Hablas Inglés?"
3. "¿Cuánto gastas en gestor cada mes?"

**Interview modifications:**
- Conduct in Spanish
- Focus on: Language barrier ≠ issue (no pain point)
- Emphasis: "Would you use this instead of hiring a gestor?"
- Expected ARPU: Higher (€49-79/mo) due to trust + simplicity
- Expected urgency: Lower (already familiar with Spanish system)

**Why include:** Validates product isn't expat-only; proves broader TAM.

### Persona C: Gestor/Tax Advisor (Partnership Stakeholder)

**Demographics:**
- Role: Gestor, tax advisor, accountant
- Company size: Solo to 50 people
- Clients: 50-500 autónomos
- Tech adoption: Low-medium

**Screening:** "Do you manage books/tax prep for autónomos? Interested in partnerships?"

**Interview Flow (20-30 minutes)**

1. **Client pain points:** "What's the #1 complaint you hear from autónomos about taxes?"
   - Expected: Complexity, cost, quarterly filing stress

2. **Current tools:** "What tools do your clients currently use for invoicing/expense tracking?"
   - Look for: Scattered (Stripe, Excel, nothing)

3. **White-label opportunity:** "Would you be interested in reselling a white-labeled invoicing + tax tool to your clients?"
   - Expected: YES (if commission model is attractive)

4. **Margin expectations:** "What commission would make this worth your time to recommend?"
   - Offer: 10-15% of monthly SaaS revenue per referral
   - Expected: YES if ≥10%

5. **Switching cost:** "How many of your clients would switch tools if you recommended it?"
   - Expected: 40-60% (trust in their recommendation is high)

**Scoring:**

| Dimension | Evidence | Score |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Client demand** | "60% of my clients ask for cheaper tax solutions" | 8/10 |
| **Partnership interest** | "I'd definitely recommend if commission is good" | 9/10 |
| **Referral potential** | "I manage 200 autónomos; could recommend to 80-100" | 9/10 |
| **Implementation risk** | "My clients trust me; easy to get them to try" | 8/10 |
| **GO/NO-GO** | **Partnership moat confirmed** | ✓ |

## NICHE 2: LEY ATENCIÓN AL CLIENTE COMPLIANCE PLATFORM

### Persona A: CS Manager at 250-500 Employee Company (Primary)

**Demographics:**
- Title: Head of Customer Service, Customer Operations Manager, Service Director
- Company size: 250-500 employees
- Revenue: €50M-100M
- Industry: Telco, finance, retail, e-commerce
- Tech stack: Zendesk, Freshdesk, or custom
- Urgency: High (compliance deadline Dec 2026)

**Screening Questions:**
1. "Do you manage customer service for a company with 250+ employees?"
2. "Are you aware of Spain's new customer service law (Ley 10/2025) with Dec 2026 deadline?"
3. "Do you currently track SLA compliance (response time targets)?"

**Interview Flow (30-45 minutes)**

#### Part 1: Regulatory Awareness (8 min)
1. **Law knowledge:** "What do you know about Ley 10/2025 requirements?"
   - If HIGH knowledge: "What concerns you most about compliance?"
   - If LOW knowledge: Explain: "Law requires 95% of inquiries answered within 3 min + AI-powered CS by Dec 2026"
   - Look for: Concern level, urgency

2. **Internal readiness:** "Has your company started planning for this law?"
   - Expected: 60% have HEARD but not started planning

3. **Owner:** "Who in your organization owns compliance responsibility (you, legal, operations, IT)?"
   - Look for: Decision-making clarity, required sign-offs

#### Part 2: Current Process Pain (10 min)
4. **Current SLA tracking:** "How do you currently measure if you're meeting the 3-min response target?"
   - Expected: Manual (Excel, Zendesk native, or gut feel)
   - Pain points: "It's hard to automate," "We don't have real-time visibility," "End-of-month surprise"

5. **AI readiness:** "Do you currently use AI (chatbots, smart routing) in your CS operations?"
   - If YES: "What % of inquiries does AI handle?"
   - If NO: "Is this a requirement or nice-to-have?"
   - Expected: 40% have AI, 60% don't

6. **Audit trail:** "Do you currently document how you're complying with regulations for audits?"
   - Expected: NO (most companies don't prepare audit evidence until forced)

7. **Competitive anxiety:** "Are your competitors complying with this law? Is this creating competitive pressure?"
   - Look for: Fear of being behind + motivation to match

#### Part 3: Pricing & ARPU (8 min)
8. **Budget availability:** "If you needed to buy a compliance solution, what budget is available?"
   - Expected: €1,500-5,000/year (soft budget, not hard)

9. **Pricing model preference:** "Would you prefer:"
   - A) SaaS subscription (€200-300/mo)
   - B) One-time implementation (€2-3K) + cheap SaaS (€50/mo)
   - C) Audit service (€3-5K annual retainer)
   - Expected: B (low risk, pay-as-you-go preferred)

10. **Value perception:** "Rank these features by importance:"
    - Real-time SLA dashboard
    - Pre-built compliance checklist
    - Automated audit report generation
    - AI readiness validator
    - Integration with Zendesk/Freshdesk
    - Expected ranking: 1 (dashboard), 3 (audit report), 5 (integration)

#### Part 4: Urgency & Timeline (6 min)
11. **Decision timeline:** "If we launched this tool in April 2026, when would you want to be live?"
    - Expected: By June 2026 (6 months before enforcement)

12. **Vendor evaluation:** "How long is your typical software evaluation process?"
    - Expected: 2-4 weeks (faster than typical enterprise due to regulation deadline)

13. **Required sign-offs:** "Who else needs to approve this purchase (legal, IT, finance)?"
    - Look for: Number of stakeholders (too many = slow), single decision-maker = fast

**Scoring Template:**

| Dimension | Evidence | Score |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Problem clarity** | "We have no way to prove we meet the 3-min rule; audit would fail" | 9/10 |
| **Pain severity** | "If we fail audit, we could face €50K fine; board will kill us" | 9/10 |
| **Willingness to pay** | "€200-300/mo is acceptable; we already spend €2K/mo on Zendesk" | 8/10 |
| **Urgency** | "We have 9 months; need something by Q3 2026" | 9/10 |
| **Adoption timeline** | "We could be live in 4-6 weeks if it integrates with Zendesk" | 8/10 |
| **Multi-user commitment** | "5+ people would use this (CS, legal, operations, IT)" | 8/10 |
| **GO/NO-GO** | **6+ out of 6 dimensions = GO** | ✓ |

### Persona B: Compliance Officer / General Counsel (Secondary)

**Demographics:**
- Title: Compliance Officer, General Counsel, Data Protection Officer
- Company size: 250+ employees
- Tech comfort: Low (legal background)
- Motivation: Regulatory risk avoidance

**Screening:** "Do you manage regulatory compliance for a company with 250+ employees?"

**Interview modifications:**
- Focus on: Legal liability, audit trail, regulatory defense
- Key question: "What would give you confidence that your customer service is compliant?"
- Expected pain: "We need documentation we can show regulators if audited"
- Price sensitivity: LOW (compliance budget separate from CS budget)

**Why include:** Validates that buy-in from legal/compliance team is critical (slow down decision) vs. CS-only (faster).

---

### Persona C: HR Manager / HR Director (Tertiary)

**Demographics:**
- Title: Head of HR, CHRO
- Concern: Labour law compliance (not just CS law)
- Motivation: Team management, labor inspection risk

**Screening:** "Are you responsible for labor law compliance for 250+ employees?"

**Interview flow:**
1. **Current compliance:** "How do you currently ensure your company meets labor law requirements?"
2. **Customer service law awareness:** "Have you heard about Ley 10/2025?"
3. **Cross-functional need:** "Does your HR team need visibility into CS compliance (for employee training, etc.)?"

**Expected outcome:** HR is slow buyer, but customer service law overlaps with HR obligations (employee capacity planning). Secondary decision-maker.
