Actionable templates to evaluate and rank niches systematically.

## 1. Opportunity Scorecard Template

**Purpose**: Rank multiple niche candidates on 7 critical dimensions.

**Instructions**:
1. List 3-5 niche candidates
2. Rate each on 1-10 for all dimensions
3. Weight by importance (default weights provided)
4. Calculate final score
5. Rank and compare

### Template

```
NICHE 1: ___________________

Personal Fulfillment (Weight: 1) ___/10
- Do YOU care about this niche?
- Will you stay interested 12+ months?
- Alignment with personal values?

Market Demand (Weight: 2) ___/10
- Search volume (500-5000/month ideal)
- Monthly audience growth
- Social media mentions/engagement
- Reddit/Quora question volume

Competition Level (Weight: 2) ___/10
- Number of direct competitors
- Market saturation (10-50 competitors = sweet spot)
- Competitor quality/funding
- Barriers to entry

Monetization Potential (Weight: 2) ___/10
- Clear revenue model (affiliate, SaaS, products, services)?
- Average price point
- Customer lifetime value
- Multiple revenue streams possible?

Audience Size (Weight: 1) ___/10
- Total addressable market
- Can you find 20+ customers easily?
- Geographic restrictions (local vs global)?

Evergreen Relevance (Weight: 1) ___/10
- Trending (hype cycle) vs permanent need
- Lifespan (10 years or 6 months?)
- Resistant to disruption

Personal Alignment (Weight: 1) ___/10
- Existing expertise level
- Learning curve if starting fresh
- Network in this space

TOTAL SCORE = (____×1 + ____×2 + ____×2 + ____×2 + ____×1 + ____×1 + ____×1) / 10 = ____/10
```

**Score Interpretation**:
- 8-10 = Excellent (pursue immediately)
- 6-8 = Good (validate with interviews first)
- 4-6 = Risky (test assumptions before committing)
- <4 = Avoid (either oversaturated or too small)

## 2. TAM/SAM/SOM Calculation Template

**Purpose**: Estimate realistic market opportunity size.

### Top-Down Approach (for vision)

```
TOTAL ADDRESSABLE MARKET (TAM)
Total Industry Size: $_______ billion/year
Source: [Industry report, government data, analyst report]

Target Customer Segment: ___________________
Industry Growth Rate: ___% annually

TAM = Industry Size × Segment % = $_______ billion
```

### Bottom-Up Approach (for credibility)

```
Target Customer: ___________________
Total addressable customer base: _________ (number of customers)
Average annual spend per customer: $________
Average revenue per account (ARPA): $________

TAM = Customer Count × ARPA = $_______ / year
```

### SAM Calculation

```
SAM = Portion of TAM you can realistically serve

Constraints:
- Geographic: [list regions you'll focus on] → ___% of TAM
- Product scope: [what features/use cases] → ___% remaining
- Go-to-market: [how you'll reach them] → ___% remaining

SAM = TAM × ___% × ___% × ___% = $_______ / year
```

### SOM Calculation

```
SOM = SAM × realistic market share capture

Market Share Assumptions:
- Year 1 capture: ___% of SAM
- Year 3 capture: ___% of SAM
- Year 5 capture: ___% of SAM

SOM Year 1 = SAM × ___% = $_______ / year
SOM Year 3 = SAM × ___% = $_______ / year
SOM Year 5 = SAM × ___% = $_______ / year
```

**Example** (SaaS for agencies):
```
TAM: Global project management software = $30B
SAM: Agencies only (8% of TAM) × Remote-first focus (3% of SAM) = $72M
SOM Year 1: Capture 0.5% of SAM = $360K revenue (40% margins = $144K profit)
SOM Year 3: Capture 2% of SAM = $1.44M revenue
```

## 7. Validation Checklist

**Purpose**: Before committing to a niche, verify key assumptions.

```
NICHE: ___________________

□ DEMAND VALIDATION
  □ Found 20+ potential customers in <2 hours
  □ Search volume >300/month (Google Trends)
  □ >50 Reddit posts/month discussing the problem
  □ Communities active and engaged (Discord, Facebook group, etc.)
  □ Problem mentions increasing (trending up)

□ CUSTOMER INTERVIEWS (Target: 20-50)
  □ Scheduled 20+ interviews with target customers
  □ Each interview 20-30 minutes
  □ Identified 3+ pain points mentioned repeatedly
  □ Customers express frustration with current solutions
  □ Customers willing to pay for better solution (>70% say yes)

□ MONETIZATION VALIDATION
  □ Clear revenue model identified (affiliate/SaaS/products/services)
  □ Competitor/adjacent products generate revenue (proof market pays)
  □ Customers in interviews mention budget for solving problem
  □ Average revenue potential per customer estimated: $________
  □ Multiple monetization paths exist (not dependent on one model)

□ COMPETITION VALIDATION
  □ Identified 5-30 direct competitors (sweet spot, not 0 or 100+)
  □ Analyzed top 3 competitors' weaknesses
  □ Found at least 2 ways to differentiate
  □ Not facing massive, well-funded incumbents
  □ Can realistically compete within 6-12 months

□ MARKET SIZE VALIDATION
  □ TAM estimated: $_________ billion
  □ SAM estimated: $_________ million
  □ SOM Year 1 estimated: $_________ thousand
  □ Market size supports $50K-500K ARR within 2 years
  □ Growth trajectory positive (not declining)

□ PERSONAL ALIGNMENT
  □ Genuine interest in niche (not just money)
  □ Have or can quickly develop required expertise
  □ Have network/access to target customers
  □ Can sustain motivation for 2+ years
  □ Unique advantage or perspective on this niche

□ FEASIBILITY VALIDATION
  □ Can build MVP in <2 months
  □ Can acquire first customer in <3 months
  □ Don't require external funding to validate
  □ Can bootstrap to profitability

OVERALL ASSESSMENT: ✓ GO / ✗ NO-GO / ? NEEDS MORE VALIDATION

High-risk assumptions to mitigate: _______________________________________
```
