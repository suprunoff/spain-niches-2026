## Metric Quick Reference

Good Demand Signs:
- Search volume: 500-5,000/month on Google
- Reddit: 50+ posts/month
- YouTube: 100+ videos on topic
- Google Trends: Rising trend
- Social mentions: 100+ per month

Good Competition Signs:
- 5-15 direct competitors
- Competitors bootstrapped/small
- Reviews mention pain points
- No well-funded incumbents
- 2+ differentiation angles possible

Good Monetization Signs:
- CPC > $1.00
- 2+ revenue models possible
- Existing profitable products
- Average order value: $50+
- Customer lifetime value: $500+

Strong Customer Signals (>70% say yes):
- Would you pay for solution?
- Is this urgent problem?
- Could this save X hours/week?
- Would you recommend?

## Niche Scoring Calculator (Quick Math)

DEMAND SCORE (40% weight)
= (Search Volume + Reddit Activity + YouTube Interest + Trend) / 4 = __/10

COMPETITION SCORE (30% weight, inverted)
= 10 - [(Competitor Count + Saturation + Incumbent Strength) / 3] = __/10

MONETIZATION SCORE (30% weight)
= (CPC + Revenue Paths + Product Proof + AOV + LTV) / 5 = __/10

FINAL NICHE SCORE
= (Demand × 0.4) + (Competition × 0.3) + (Monetization × 0.3) = __/10

INTERPRETATION:
8-10 = GO (excellent)
6-8 = VALIDATE (interview)
4-6 = RESEARCH (verify)
<4 = NO-GO (reconsider)

## Interview Questions (Copy-Paste Ready)

Pain Point Validation:
- What's your biggest frustration with current solution?
- How much time weekly on this problem?
- What have you tried to solve this?
- Why didn't that work?
- How would your day change if problem disappeared?

Willingness to Pay:
- Would you pay for solution?
- What would you pay? ($10, $50, $100, $500/month?)
- Could you justify as business expense?
- Who else in your company needs this?

Urgency:
- How urgent is solving this? (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
- Actively looking for solutions now?
- What's preventing you from buying today?

Network Effect:
- Use this if friends/coworkers weren't on it?
- How many people you know with problem?

## Red Flags (Stop & Reconsider)

Market Red Flags:
- Declining search interest 2+ years
- No communities discussing it
- Massive incumbent (Google, Microsoft)
- Can't find 10 people with problem
- Zero existing products

Competition Red Flags:
- 100+ direct competitors
- Top 3 backed by VC
- Constant price wars
- >70% market share by top 5
- Zero switching costs

Monetization Red Flags:
- CPC < $0.25
- Only free model possible
- AOV < $10
- LTV < $50

Customer Red Flags:
- Can't find 20+ customers in 2 hours
- 70%+ say "nice to have"
- No willingness to pay
- Prefer free alternative

Personal Red Flags:
- Only money motivation
- No expertise/network
- Can't dedicate 3+ months
- Hate industry/problem

## TL;DR (30-Second Summary)

Best niche = Strong demand + Low competition + Clear monetization + Personal fit

Steps:
1. Generate ideas
2. Interview 30 customers
3. Run Opportunity Scorecard (score >6.0)
4. Calculate niche score (score >6.0)
5. Validate willingness to pay
6. Build pre-commits (5+ customers)
7. Launch MVP
8. Iterate

Key rule: Use multiple frameworks. Quantify everything. Interview customers. Make data-driven decisions.
