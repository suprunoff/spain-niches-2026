## Key Insights (You Need to Know This)

### The Single Best Method
**Problem interviews with real customers** > everything else.

Not surveys. Not trends. Not tools. Talk to 10 people in your target market. If 7+ confirm the problem exists, move forward.

### The Cost of Validation (Real Numbers)
- Week 1: $0 (use free tools)
- Weeks 2-4: $50-100 (landing page + email)
- Week 4+: $100-300/month (basic stack)

You can validate a niche idea for under $50. No excuses.

### The Most Common Mistakes
1. **Building before validating** (costs $1k-10k in wasted time)
2. **Only asking friends/family** (false positives)
3. **Assuming instead of testing** (no data)
4. **No distribution plan** (validated idea, can't reach customers)
5. **Wrong positioning** (can't explain the value)

## Validation Metrics Summary

### Demand Signals (Does Market Exist?)
- [ ] Google Trends shows stable or rising search volume
- [ ] 5+ online communities discussing the problem
- [ ] 3-5 existing solutions with paying customers
- [ ] Fewer than 10 direct competitors

### Customer Signals (Will People Pay?)
- [ ] 10+ problem interviews = real conversations
- [ ] 7+ people confirm problem exists
- [ ] 5+ express willingness to pay
- [ ] 3+ mention specific pain point

### Traction Signals (Ready to Build?)
- [ ] 20+ email signups on landing page
- [ ] 5+ pre-sales/early adopters
- [ ] Positive problem interview feedback
- [ ] You found a unique differentiation angle

**Rule:** If you hit all these → you have permission to build.

## Decision Tree (Use This)

```
DOES MARKET EXIST?
├─ YES: 5+ communities, search volume exists → Continue
└─ NO: <3 communities, declining trends → STOP, new niche

WILL PEOPLE PAY?
├─ YES: 7+/10 interviews say yes → Continue
└─ NO: <4/10 interested → STOP, pivot angle or niche

CAN YOU REACH THEM?
├─ YES: Clear distribution path → Continue
└─ NO: Can't reach customers cost-effectively → STOP

DO YOU HAVE DIFFERENTIATION?
├─ YES: Unique angle competitors miss → BUILD
└─ NO: Pure clone of existing idea → STOP, find angle

FINAL DECISION:
├─ All green lights → MOVE FORWARD WITH MVP
├─ 3 of 4 green → PROCEED WITH CAUTION
├─ 2 of 4 green → HIGH RISK, CONSIDER PIVOTING
└─ 0-1 green lights → STOP, FIND DIFFERENT NICHE
```
