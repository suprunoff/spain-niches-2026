## Success Checklist (20-Item Go/No-Go)

All must be YES to proceed:

**Demand (4 items):**
- Search volume 500-5,000/month
- 50+ Reddit posts/month in community
- 3+ successful competitors exist
- Trend growing (not declining)

**Competition (4 items):**
- 5-30 direct competitors (sweet spot)
- No venture-backed incumbents
- 2+ clear differentiation angles
- Can realistically compete in 6-12 months

**Monetization (4 items):**
- CPC > $1.00 OR existing profitable products
- 2+ revenue models possible
- AOV (average order value) > $50
- Path to $1,000+ MRR in 12 months

**Customer (4 items):**
- 20+ customers found in <2 hours
- 70%+ say problem is urgent/must-have
- 50%+ would pay for solution
- Clear willingness to pay amount

**Personal (4 items):**
- Genuine interest (not money-only motivation)
- Have or can develop expertise
- Have network/access to customers
- Can sustain 3+ month commitment

**Score: 17-20 = GO, 14-16 = VALIDATE, 10-13 = RESEARCH, <10 = RECONSIDER**

## Quick Math: Niche Scoring Formula

```
DEMAND SCORE (40% weight)
= Average of (Search Volume + Reddit Activity + YouTube Interest + Trend) = __/10

COMPETITION SCORE (30% weight, inverted)
= 10 - Average of (Competitor Count + Saturation + Incumbent Strength) = __/10

MONETIZATION SCORE (30% weight)
= Average of (CPC + Revenue Paths + Product Proof + AOV + LTV) = __/10

FINAL NICHE SCORE = (Demand × 0.4) + (Competition × 0.3) + (Monetization × 0.3)
= (__/10 × 0.4) + (__/10 × 0.3) + (__/10 × 0.3) = __/10

INTERPRETATION:
8-10 = GO (excellent opportunity)
6-8 = VALIDATE (interview customers)
4-6 = RESEARCH (verify assumptions)
<4 = NO-GO (reconsider)
```
