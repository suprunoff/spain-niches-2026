## Scoring Methodology (D/G/R/S/M/A/F/T)

Each niche is scored on 8 dimensions (1–10 scale):

| Dimension | What It Measures | Scoring Logic |
|-----------|------------------|---------------|
| **D**emand | Strength of problem + willingness to pay | 10 = forced adoption, 1 = "nice to have" |
| **G**ap | Competitive whitespace + differentiation | 10 = zero competitors, 1 = crowded |
| **R**etention | Churn rate + LTV health | 10 = <1% monthly churn, 1 = >10% monthly |
| **S**calability | Ability to grow without linear cost increase | 10 = pure SaaS, 1 = services-intensive |
| **M**argin | Gross margin after COGS + support | 10 = 90%+ margin, 1 = 20% |
| **A**doption | Friction to get first customers | 10 = viral/organic, 1 = high sales friction |
| **F**riendly to Build | MVP complexity + timeline | 10 = 1–2 weeks, 1 = 6+ months |
| **T**iming | Market readiness + regulatory urgency | 10 = critical deadline, 1 = low urgency |
| **COMPOSITE** | Weighted average (all 8) | 8.0–10.0 = strong, 6.0–8.0 = viable, <6.0 = weak |

**Formula:** COMPOSITE = (D + G + R + S + M + A + F + T) / 8

**Scoring notes:**
- Go/No-Go threshold: 6.0+ (anything lower = consider pivot/kill)
- 8.0–10.0 = "This will work" (execute carefully)
- 6.0–8.0 = "This could work" (must validate demand before building)
