- **Market growth:** €264M → €509M (93% by 2030)
- **AI acceleration:** Legal AI 18% CAGR
- **Regulatory tailwinds:** 3 major deadlines (VeriFactu, LAC, etc)
- **Competitor gap:** Kleos/SuDespacho have no AI yet

### 3. Why These Niches?
- **Finiquito:** Fastest MVP (3 weeks), clear monetization, urgent pain
