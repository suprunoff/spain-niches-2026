  • No Spanish market leader (Cookiebot/Iubenda are global, not local)
  • AEPD enforcement wave = urgency for SMBs
  • Low CAC (€10-15), high LTV/CAC ratio (18-28x)

MVP SCOPE (6-8 weeks):
  1. Website RGPD audit tool (auto-scan for tracking scripts)
  2. Spanish templates (Privacy Policy, DPA, Cookie Policy)
  3. Simple consent banner manager
  4. AEPD compliance checklist
  5. Employee training module (PDF)

UNIT ECONOMICS:
  • Pricing: €20-50/мес for SMBs
  • CAC: €10-15 (content/SEO)
  • LTV: €278 (€29/мес × 48 months × 40% retention)
  • LTV/CAC: 18x → HEALTHY ✓

4-WEEK PLAN:
  Week 1: 10 interviews with SMBs (ask "would you pay €30/мес?")
  Week 2: Landing page + Google Ads test (€100 budget)
  Week 3: MVP prototype (audit + 3 templates)
  Week 4: Soft launch (Product Hunt ES + Twitter)

GO/NO-GO GATE (Week 5):
  • GO if: >500 landing visitors, >5% signup rate, 3+ beta customers
  • PIVOT if: <500 visitors or <2% conversion
  • NO-GO if: all metrics miss

RISK: LOW
