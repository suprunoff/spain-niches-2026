## 10. REAL ESTATE TRANSACTIONS / NOTARY AUTOMATION

| Component | Current Status | Digitalization | Barrier |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Notary services** | Mandated | Partial (e-signatures, remote ID) | Strong professional resistance |
| **Property registry** | Digital | Good (Sede Electrónica, 30-day processing) | Government owned; slow changes |
| **Debt certificate (Nota Simple)** | Digital | Good | Already automated |
| **Tax (Plusvalía)** | Partially digital | Poor | Varies by municipality |
| **Community approval** | Manual | None | No digital standard exists |
| **Title insurance** | Rare in Spain | Sparse | Not standard practice |

**Competitive gap:** Possible but requires notary cooperation (they control gatekeepers); low political urgency.

## FINAL RECOMMENDATION RANKING

1. 🔴 **MUST START:** Tourist Rental Compliance
   - Whitespace, urgent, high TAM, fast MVP

2. 🟡 **STRONG CONSIDER:** Construction PM (niche C: permitting) or Rental PM (segment A: large landlords)
   - Clear gaps, defensible niches, solid TAM

3. 🟡 **SECONDARY:** Smart Buildings or Investment Analysis
   - Larger TAM but more competition; longer time to market

4. ⚠️ **HIGHER RISK:** Home renovation, Energy efficiency
   - Fragmented markets; need strong differentiation

5. ❌ **SKIP:** Comunidad de propietarios, Building permits, Transaction automation
   - Too much friction, too much fragmentation, or incumbent moats
