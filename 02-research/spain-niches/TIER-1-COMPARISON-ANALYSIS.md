## Scenario 1: Solo Founder / Bootstrap (€2K budget)

**Best Choice:** Telegram Media Empire

**Why:**
- Single channel: €500 initial investment (domain, tools, scheduler)
- No engineering required (copywriting + community building)
- Breakeven: Month 6 (cumulative positive)
- Can scale to 3-4 channels within year with profits
- Outsource writing by month 4 (paid from revenue)

**Path:**
1. Launch personal finance TG channel (week 1)
2. Grow to 2K subs organic (month 2)
3. Test sponsorships (month 3)
4. Hire VA/writer (month 4, paid from revenue)
5. Scale to 3-4 channels (month 6-12)
6. Revenue: €35K-50K/year by year-end

**Risk:** Burnout if you do all writing yourself; mitigated by hiring early

## Scenario 2: Founder + Engineer (Technical Team)

**Best Choice:** AI Content Generator

**Why:**
- €8K-12K capital achievable with co-founder
- You leverage technical skills
- Scalable from day 1 (SaaS)
- Year 1 revenue: €366K (10x better than single TG channel)
- LATAM expansion path built in (no content creation bottleneck)

**Path:**
