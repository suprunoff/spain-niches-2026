### 2. BRITISH-EXPATS-MOROCCO-NICHES.md
**Read time:** 30 min | **Audience:** Researchers, detailed analysis needed

**Ниша А: British Expats**
- Market size breakdown (412K Brits, distribution by region)
- 6 critical pain points with validation data:
  1. Residencia (50% haven't exchanged docs, 2-6 weeks processing)
  2. Pension tax (UK private pension taxed only in Spain, complex DTA)
  3. Healthcare (S1 vs Convenio vs private, confusing eligibility)
  4. Driving license (€65-125, 4-6 weeks, confusing process)
  5. Property tax/remortgage (double taxation, £500-5K/year risk)
  6. Double taxation (DTA complex, many pay wrong amounts)
- Competitive analysis (Expatica weak, Wise payments-only, no SaaS competitor)
- MVP: ExpatsOS (residencia tracker + pension calc + healthcare guide + driver license tracker + property tax tool)
- Monetization: Freemium (€9.99-14.99/mo) + affiliate (Wise, lawyers)
- Unit economics: 5-8K signups Y1 → 600-1.2K paid → €86-173K ARR, LTV/CAC 6-8x
- Go/No-Go: **GO** (fast MVP, high pain, no competitors, proven CAC channels)

**Ниша Б: Spain-Morocco B2B**
- Market size (€22.7B trade, 5-8K Spanish + 10-15K Moroccan SMEs, 0.79M diaspora)
- 5 critical pain points:
  1. Customs documentation (5-10 days, complex forms, €100-300 broker fee)
  2. Cross-border payments (SWIFT 3-7 days, 4-7% fee, bank SWIFT codes not recognized)
  3. Regulatory/tariff complexity
  4. Trade finance gap (no SME-friendly options, cash-in-advance friction)
  5. Remittance friction (€1.375B from Spain to Morocco, 2-3 days slow)
- Competitive analysis (no ES-MA specialist, Wise dominates remittance)
- MVP: TradeFlow (marketplace + docs automation + compliance + payments + shipping)
- Monetization: Listing fees (€50 Spanish, €20 Moroccan) + transaction fees (2-3%) + premium (€99/mo) + affiliate (shipping)
- Unit economics: 500-1K Spanish, 300-500 Moroccan sellers, 200-400 deals/mo → €250-700K Y1, LTV/CAC 10-20x
- Upside (Phase 2 remittance): 240K users × €150 avg × 1.5% fee = €1.944M/yr
- Go/No-Go: **CONDITIONAL GO** (strong B2B, but longer build, higher regulatory risk, needs domain expertise)

**Sources:** 20+ web sources, market data, trade statistics, customer interviews
