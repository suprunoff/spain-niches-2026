## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### YES if:
- Score ≥ 7.0 ✓
- Founder has domain knowledge OR product expertise ✓
- TAM ≥ €75M ✓
- MVP build time ≤ 16 weeks ✓
- Regulatory or competitive moat evident ✓

### MAYBE if:
- Score 6.5-6.9 with one strong advantage (huge TAM, very fast MVP, unique moat)
- Founder passionate + willing to test with €2-5K pre-MVP validation

### NO if:
- Score < 6.5
- No differentiation vs. global competitors
- MVP time > 20 weeks (too risky for validation)
- Team lacks relevant expertise (unless founding for learning)
