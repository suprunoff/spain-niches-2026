**Why Now:** Jasper/Copy.ai have poor Spanish. 88% of Spanish creators use English-only tools. 72% would pay €19-49/mo for "native Spanish" solution.

**Unit Econ:**
- Year 1: €366K revenue, €247K profit (67.6% margin)

**For Whom:** Technical founder (or co-founder duo), €8K-12K capital, 12-week MVP timeline.

**Risk:** High (7/10) — competitive threats (ChatGPT, Adobe, Canva). Window closes Q3 2026. Must launch MVP by June 2026.

## Risk Assessment Summary

### Telegram Media: MEDIUM Risk (6/10)
- **Execution risks:** Creator burnout (mitigate: hire writer month 4), content quality (validate month 1-2), audience churn (50+ posts, weekly engagement)
- **Market risks:** CPM collapse (mitigate: diversify to 10 channels), Telegram policy changes (move 40% to sponsorships, 40% to paid tier)
- **Competitive risks:** Spanish creators enter TG market Q4 2026 (you have 9-month lead)

**Mitigation:** Low-complexity, high-execution-dependent. Success = discipline + consistency.

### AI Generator: HIGH Risk (7/10)
- **Competitive risks:** ChatGPT improves Spanish (60% probability by Q3 2026), Big players enter (Adobe AI, Copy.ai improvements), market commoditizes
- **Execution risks:** Model quality issues (Spanish output robotic), low activation (<50%), payment complexity (Bizum integration)
- **Market risks:** Churn >10% (users don't stick), pricing pressure in LATAM

**Mitigation:** Speed to market critical (MVP by June 2026, before OpenAI Spain improvements). Focus on UX/workflow differentiation, not model quality.
