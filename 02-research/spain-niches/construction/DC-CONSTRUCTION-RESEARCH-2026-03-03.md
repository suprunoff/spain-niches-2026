## Construction Pain Points
1. **Permitting**: Water concession + EIA + grid access (scarce, competitive). 17 comunidades autónomas = fragmented
2. **Subcontractor coordination**: 50-100+ subs per project, CAE mandatory, no DC-specific platform
3. **Commissioning**: Level 0-6 (ISO/IEC 22237 + EN50600), only ALAN COMMISSIONING (Aragón) specializes locally
4. **New sustainability law**: >100MW DCs must prove top 15% PUE/WUE/ERF/REF — pre-condition for grid connection
5. **Talent**: 120K+ unfilled IT positions; DC-specific HVAC/diesel mechanics/electricians scarce

## Existing Software in DC Construction
- Procore (global, used by international GCs — unclear Spanish SME adoption)
- BrickControl, Bildia, AEC-ON (Spanish SME tools, not DC-specific)
- **NO Spain-specific DC construction/commissioning SaaS found**

## Opportunity Summary
1. DC Subcontractor Platform (CAE + MEP discovery + vetting) — urgent, 2026-2028 window
2. DC Commissioning SaaS (Level 0-6 tracking, EN50600 compliance)
3. DC Procurement Intelligence (long-lead item tracking)
4. Construction CAE + BIM hybrid for mega-industrial projects
