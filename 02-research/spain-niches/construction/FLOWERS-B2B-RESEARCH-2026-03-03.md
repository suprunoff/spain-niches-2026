# B2B Flower Wholesale Spain
**Date:** 2026-03-03 | **Source:** Researcher agent (web search)

## Market Size
- Spain = 4th EU producer, 11% EU production, €900M production value at origin
- 4,500 ha cultivated area; export growth +27.7% YoY (2023-2024) [worldstopexports.com]
- European floriculture: $19.05B (2024) → $31.30B (2032), CAGR 5.68% [marketdataforecast.com]

## Wholesale Infrastructure
- **Mercamadrid Flores**: 8 independent stands, 24h cold chain guarantee
- **Mercabarna-flor**: 44,000m², opened 2008, adjacent to El Prat Airport
- **531 wholesale florists** in Spain (Sept 2025): highest in Barcelona (69) + Cádiz (32) [poidata.io]

## Current Ordering (B2B)
- **WhatsApp** = primary informal channel (confirmed across multiple florist sites)
- Phone, email, in-person Mercamadrid/Mercabarna visits
- No price transparency, no centralized availability

## ⚠️ KEY COMPETITOR: BLOM MARKET (Valencia, 2020)
- "First digital marketplace for flowers and plants for professionals" [blommarket.com]
- 20+ leading Spanish wholesalers, 10,000+ product references
- Targets: florists, wedding planners, event organizers, HORECA
- Benefits: unified invoices, delivery within days, time savings
- **Market penetration unknown** (no public data on users/GMV)
- WhatsApp still primary channel → suggests low adoption

## Also Present
- **Floriday** (Royal FloraHolland): 5,500+ growers/buyers, 60 countries, Spain presence
- **Thursd**: Content/community platform, not ordering

## Key Wholesalers
Verdnatura (largest), COFLORES, Sanseflor, Gradyflor, Bruflor, Begastriflor, Flors Noè, Cámara

## Seasonality
- Valentine's Day = 28% annual sales, 80% volume in 72h [alibaba.com]
- Mother's Day = 25-30% annual sales
- Also: Todos los Santos (Nov 1), funerals year-round

## Payment Terms
- Legal max: 60 days (Law 15/2010)
- Standard B2B: Net 30 most common
