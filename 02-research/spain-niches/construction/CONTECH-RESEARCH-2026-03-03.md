# Spain ConTech Market Research
**Date:** 2026-03-03 | **Source:** Researcher agent (web search)

## Market Size
- €83.5B (2025), CAGR 3.1% → €97.78B (2030) [mordorintelligence.com]
- LEAST digitalized sector in Spain (67.9% still conventional methods)
- EU RRF: $154B available to Spain, ~70% flowing to construction [openpr.com]

## Key Pain Points
1. **BIM mandate** (April 2024 public contracts, escalating to 2030) — SMEs can't afford Autodesk
   - 440 tenders with BIM requirements (€752M investment, +230% vs 2017) [bimcommunity.com]
2. **CAE compliance**: Players exist (Nalanda 50K+ subs, CTAIMA 100K+, Metacontratas, eGestiona) — SATURATED but no marketplace layer
3. **Payment delays**: 82% face >30 days; Spain Law 15/2010; NO fintech solution for construction [pbmares.com]
4. **VeriFactu Jan 2027**: Affects all construction invoicing systems
5. **Labor shortage**: 60% workers lack professional education; aging workforce [bbvaresearch.com]

## Existing Players
- **CAE**: Nalanda, CTAIMA, Metacontratas, eGestiona — fragmented
- **Project Mgmt**: Aelis, Fixner, Sage Construction (SME), Procore (enterprise only, $400+/mo)
- **Startup hub**: Barcelona ConTech Hub (ITeC)

## Key Regulatory Dates
- Apr 2024: BIM Early Level (public contracts)
- Oct 2025: BIM Intermediate Level
- Jan 2027: VeriFactu for construction invoicing
- Oct 2027: BIM Advanced Level
