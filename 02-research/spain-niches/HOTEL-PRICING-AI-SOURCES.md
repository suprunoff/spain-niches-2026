## Research Limitations

1. **Vendor claims not independently verified:** RoomPriceGenie uplift (19-22%) comes from vendor marketing materials, not independent audits
2. **Spanish hotel market data gaps:** No detailed breakdown of 15K independent hotels by size; estimated from chain hotel percentages
3. **No direct Spanish competitor analysis:** No local Spanish startups found in dynamic pricing for hotels (may exist in stealth)
4. **API access uncertainty:** Booking.com partner API requires formal partnership; feasibility not pre-confirmed
5. **Churn rate estimates:** 5% annual churn estimated from general SaaS benchmarks, not specific to hotel revenue management software
6. **Labor cost estimates:** Dev cost estimates based on freelancer rates, may vary by region/team structure
