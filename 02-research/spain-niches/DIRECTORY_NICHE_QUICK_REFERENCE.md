## TIER 1 QUICK WINS (Score 8.0+) — GO NOW

### #1 Home Services Bundle — 8.9/10
**⭐ BEST FIRST PLAY**
- **TAM:** €90B+ (Spain home services market, CAGR 17.9%)
- **Demand:** "Electricista madrid" = 15K searches/month, "fontanero barcelona" = 10K/month
- **Why Now:** Digital adoption acceleration, COVID-driven home renovation boom ongoing
- **CAC:** €5-20 (organic search + WhatsApp referral)
- **LTV:** €200-400 (repeat maintenance 2-4x/year)
- **Break-Even:** 10-15 electricians = €1-2K/month revenue
- **First Customer:** Day 2-3 (WhatsApp outreach to local electricians)
- **Competitive Moat:** 50K+ verified providers + 100K+ reviews = 6-12 months for competitor to copy

**Founder Fit Check:**
- Can you make 50 WhatsApp calls to electricians without flinching? → If yes, go
- Do you know 2-3 people in construction/trades? → Helps for early traction
- Can you build basic web app + WhatsApp API integration? → Yes = go

**MVP Checklist:**
- [ ] Scrape 5K electrician phone numbers from Google Maps (use Bright Data or similar)
- [ ] Build landing page with city selector → list of electricians filtered by rating
- [ ] Twilio/WhatsApp API: Customer message → WhatsApp to electrician
- [ ] Review collection: SMS after service "Rate your electrician?"
- [ ] Payment: Stripe for lead gen billing or Bizum for customer-to-electrician

### #2 Physiotherapists & Sports Therapists — 8.7/10
**🏥 HEALTHCARE PLAY WITH INSURANCE PARTNERSHIPS**
- **TAM:** €2-3B (Spain physical therapy market growing with aging population)
- **Demand:** "Fisioterapeuta madrid cita previa" = 8K searches/month
- **Why Now:** Spain Digital 2026 mandates online appointments, insurance companies want networks
- **CAC:** €10-25 (insurance partnerships pay €15-30 per referral)
- **LTV:** €300-600 (10+ sessions per patient, repeat injuries)
- **Break-Even:** 40 practices × €30/month subscription = €1.2K/month revenue
- **First Customer:** Day 5-7 (email outreach to 100 practices)
- **B2B Upside:** Insurance partnerships (ADESLAS, Sanitas) = €50-100K/year contracts

**Insurance Partnership Angle:**
- ADESLAS: 3M customers, actively building therapist networks
- Sanitas: 2.5M customers, expanding mental health coverage
- CaixaBank Insurance: 500K+ customers
- **Pitch:** "Network of verified physiotherapists for your customers → we refer patients → you pay €15-30/referral"

**MVP Checklist:**

### #3 Wedding Vendors Marketplace — 8.6/10

- **Demand:** "Fotografo boda barcelona" = 12K/month, "catering bodas madrid" = 8K/month
- **Why Now:** Post-pandemic wedding boom (2024-2026 peak year), Instagram fatigue among couples
- **CAC:** €20-40 (Instagram ads to engaged couples)
- **LTV:** €150-300 (one-time 8-12% commission per wedding €20-50K deal)
- **Break-Even:** 50 weddings/month × €250 commission = €12.5K/month revenue

- **Network Effects:** 2K vendors → attracts 50K couples; 50K couples → attracts vendors

**Instagram GTM:**

- Ad creative: "Compare photographers & vendors for your wedding in 30 seconds"
- Budget: €500/week test = 30-50 couple sign-ups

**MVP Checklist:**

## PAYMENT INTEGRATION (CRITICAL FOR SPAIN)

**Must-Haves:**
1. **Bizum** (30.6M users, €67.7B transactions 2025, +53% growth)
   - Consumer preference #1 for online payments
   - Integration: Stripe/Adyen/Redsys support Bizum
   - Setup time: 1-2 weeks

2. **SEPA Transfer** (B2B, large invoices)
   - For trades paying deposits, business services
   - Integration: Stripe/Wise support this natively

3. **Card/Redsys** (legacy, still used)
   - For older customers, businesses
   - Integration: Stripe handles, or use Redsys directly (Spanish processor)

4. **WhatsApp Payments** (emerging, opt-in)
   - For trades who only use WhatsApp
   - Integration: WhatsApp Business API (requires approval)

**Recommendation:** Start with Bizum + SEPA, add cards month 2

## RED FLAGS (AVOID THESE)

❌ **Market Fragmentation Too Low** (market is consolidating)
- Auto repair chains: Midas, Mapfre service centers consolidating
- Salon franchises: Jean Louis David, Peluqueria Urbana chains exist
- Veterinary clinics: Large chains (Anicura, Affinity) consolidating

❌ **Customer Acquisition Too Expensive**
- If CAC > LTV/12 = death spiral (payback >12 months)
- Example: Legal services CAC €50, LTV €300 = 6-month payback = marginal

❌ **Trust Gap Unsolvable**
- Religious services: Too much dogma, trust is pastor-specific
- Artisan food: Reviews don't overcome brand familiarity + farmer relationships

❌ **Regulatory Complexity Too High**
- Healthcare clinics: RGPD + patient data = heavy compliance
- Food producers: HACCP + EU origin labels = logistics nightmare
