```

  1. Person searches "calculadora finiquito" → YOUR SITE

  - 100 finiquito calcs/month → 20 consultations → 5 conversions

  - Viral: "My lawyer uses this tool" → more organic traffic
```

### Competitive Moat

✅ **High:** AI trained on 10K+ actual finiquitos (sector conventions) vs free tools with hardcoded rules

## 2. Digital Due Diligence Inmobiliaria (Score 8.3/10)

### Existing Solutions

| Solution | Type | Features | Pricing | Weakness | URL |
|----------|------|----------|---------|----------|-----|
| **Idealista** | Portal | Real estate + Plus calc | Free (display) | Focused on sales, not legal; Plus calc only | idealista.com |
| **TaxDown** | Tax platform | Plusvalia calc | Free-€19 | Only Madrid; only tax angle | taxdown.es |
| **Registro de la Propiedad** | Government | Nota simple | €9-14 | 24-72h delay, manual process | registradores.org |
| **Lawyer firms** | Manual service | Full due diligence | €300-600 | Slow, expensive, inconsistent | Various |
| **Abogados** | Manual | Ad-hoc analysis | €100-300/hr | High friction, slow | Various |

### Your Competitive Advantage

```
Current flow (lawyer):
  Buyer asks: "Is this property clean?"
  Lawyer: "Send me CIF, I'll check everything"
  Process: 3-5 hours → nota simple (24-72h) + cargas search + plus/IAJD calc + risk report
  Cost: €300-600
  Time: 1 week
  Error rate: ~5% (missed encumbrances)

Your flow:
  Buyer enters: CIF + municipio
  System: Pulls nota (cached), searches cargas DB, calcs plus/IAJD, flags risks
  Result: Full report in 5 minutes
  Cost: €99-199
  Time: Instant
  Error rate: <1% (automated)

B2B partnerships (Idealista, Fotocasa):
  White-label your due diligence → 30-50% revenue share
  They get "Legal check included" → higher conversion
  You get distribution to 1M+ monthly users
```

### Competitive Moat

✅ **Very High:** All-in-one automation vs fragmented manual process

**Barriers for competitors:**
- Nota simple scraper (requires legal cover for 17 regional registries)
- Cargas database (manual curation from court records)
- Plusvalia calculator (17 different municipal formulas)
- Lawyer network for verification

## 3. AI Contract Analyzer (Arrendamientos) (Score 7.8/10)

### Existing Solutions

| Solution | Type | Features | Pricing | Weakness | URL |
|----------|------|----------|---------|----------|-----|
| **Jotform AI** | Generator | Generic contract generation | Free-$99/mo | Not specialized for rentals | jotform.com |
| **iWeaver** | Templates | 50+ legal templates | €29-99 | No analysis, just templates | iweaver.ai |
| **Venngage** | Generator | Generic contracts + NDAs | Free-$99/mo | Generic, no Spanish expertise | venngage.com |
| **ChatGPT** | LLM | Generic analysis | $20/mo | Not trained on Spanish law | openai.com |
| **Lawyers** | Manual | Ad-hoc review | €150-400 | Expensive, slow, overkill | Various |

### Your Competitive Advantage

```
Current flow (tenant):
  Landlord sends contract
  Tenant: "Looks OK?"
  Reality: Contract has 5 predatory clauses (deposit hold, high fines, unfair termination)
  Tenant: Pays €200 lawyer or signs anyway (50% lose money)

Your flow:
  Tenant uploads contract
  AI analysis: "⚠️ 5 risks detected in YOUR contract"
    - Deposit: Landlord can hold 100% for 'damage' (unfair)
    - Fines: €50/day late = €1500/month (excessive)
    - Termination: 3-month notice but only 1-week for landlord (unfair)
  Click: "Show fair version" → tenant-friendly template
  Click: "Talk to lawyer" → €20 consultation

2x market (tenant + landlord):
  Landlords use to generate fair contracts (avoid disputes)
  Tenants use to check landlord contracts (avoid predatory terms)
```

### Competitive Moat

✅ **Medium:** AI specialized for Spanish rental law (vs generic contract tools)

**Barriers for competitors:**
- Training data: 50K+ Spanish rental contracts (need legal/dataset partnership)
- Regulatory knowledge: Ley Arrendamientos + CC (tenant protection laws)
- Network: 100+ landlord associations + tenant unions

| Solution | Type | Users | Pricing | Strength | Weakness |
|----------|------|-------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Kleos** | Specialized | 50K+ | €100-500/mo | Mature, case mgmt, stable | Expensive, no Lexnet, old UX |
| **SuDespacho** | Specialized | 96K | €50-200/mo | Large user base, affordable | Basic features, slow innovation |
| **MN Program** | Specialized | 50K+ | €100-400/mo | Long history (20+ years) | Outdated, clunky |
| **Holded** | Generic | Growing | €29-199/mo | Affordable, clean UI, add-ons | Not specialized, missing features |
| **HubSpot** | Generic | Thousands | Free-$999/mo | Powerful, integrations, AI | Too generic for law |
| **Salesforce** | Generic | Thousands | $165-330/mo | Enterprise-grade | Overkill, hard to customize |

### Why NOT to Enter This Market

| Factor | Problem | Impact |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Barrier to Entry** | Lexnet integration (3-6 months development) | 🔴 HIGH |
| **Installed Base** | Kleos + SuDespacho have 50K-96K users | 🔴 SWITCHING COST |
| **Competitive Pressure** | Kleos/SuDespacho will add AI within 12 months | 🔴 TIME BOMB |
| **Sales Cycle** | Despachos take 2-4 months to decide | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| **Price Sensitivity** | Market only pays €50-200/month | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| **Adoption** | Only 30% of despachos use CRM today | 🟡 MEDIUM |

### What Will Happen If You Enter

**Timeline:**
- Month 0-3: Build generic CRM + time tracking (OK product, not amazing)
- Month 3-6: Add Lexnet integration (slow, bug-prone)
- Month 6-9: Acquire 50-100 customers (high CAC, slow)
- Month 9-12: Kleos/SuDespacho announce "Kleos AI" + Lexnet integration
  - Your customers: "Why switch?"
  - New prospects: "Kleos already does this + AI"
  - You: Dead product

**Better path:** If you really want this market, **wait 18 months for Kleos to mature, then build vertical AI on top** (e.g., "Kleos + Harvey AI for contract analysis").

## 5. Compliance Document Marketplace (Score 7.4/10)

### Existing Solutions

| Solution | Type | Coverage | Pricing | Weakness |
|----------|------|----------|---------|----------|
| **Facilita RGPD** | Free tool | RGPD only | Free | Incomplete, no LAC/VeriFactu |
| **Complylaw Privacidad** | Paid platform | RGPD + LOPDGDD | €500-2000 | Expensive, not SMB-focused |
| **GlobalSuite** | Software | RGPD management | €300-1000 | Process-focused, not template-focused |
| **Aranzadi (Complylaw)** | Platform | Legal templates | €600+ | Law firm focused, expensive |
| **DIY (Google Docs)** | Nothing | Nothing | Free | High risk, inconsistent |

### Your Competitive Advantage

```
Current flow (SMB):
  Owner: "We need RGPD compliance"
  Options:
    a) Facilita RGPD (free but incomplete)
    b) Lawyer (€1000-2000, overkill)
    c) DIY (free but risky)
  Reality: 70% choose (c) or (a), no real compliance

Your flow:
  SMB enters wizard:
    Q1: "Do you have employees?" → No
    Q2: "Do you process personal data?" → Yes (CRM, email)
    Q3: "Customer countries?" → EU only
    Result: Here's your compliance stack:
      - Privacy policy (template)
      - Cookie consent (ready-made)
      - DPA (ready-made)
      - DPIA (simple questionnaire → generate report)
  Cost: €99-199
  Time: 30 minutes
  Confidence: HIGH (from AEPD guidance)

Timing: LAC deadline Dec 2026, VeriFactu deadline Jan 2027
  Early adopters will pay premium for pre-built solutions
  Mid-market will scramble 3 months before deadline
  Your product: "Compliance in 30 minutes"
```

### Competitive Moat

✅ **Medium-High:** Integrated wizard + deadline-driven demand + accountant channel

**Barriers:**
- Regulatory knowledge (RGPD, LAC, VeriFactu, LSSI, CCPA, etc.)
- Template quality (must be legally sound, not liability)
- Accountant channel (200+ gestorias as resellers)
