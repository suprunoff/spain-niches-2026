## SPAIN-SPECIFIC TAILWINDS

### Regulatory Momentum
- **CSRD (Dec 2026):** ~4,000 firms mandatory, 80%+ willing to pay for compliance SaaS
- **CEE (Ongoing):** 500K certificates/year, new appraisal requirement Aug 2025, tightening 2030/2033
- **Cold Chain (RD 1021/2022):** 30% of exporters lack real-time monitoring
- **Customs Reform (Jul 2026):** €3 duty on <€150 parcels, France/Italy national fees → SMBs need automation
- **Ley Atención (Dec 2026):** Only 250+ employee firms (small impact)

### Market Trends
- **E-Commerce Growth:** 45.1% of Spanish companies now sell online (3x in 15 years)
- **Bizum Ubiquity:** 30M users, €44M daily transactions, native advantage for payment-integrated SaaS
- **Municipal Digital Gap:** 8,131 municipalities, 85%+ using 1990s software (Gestiona, SICALWIN)
- **Agricultural Exports:** 34.4M tons/year, 20% spoilage loss = cold chain ROI
- **SMB Base:** 3.3M+ SMEs (72% of workforce), mostly underserved by SaaS
