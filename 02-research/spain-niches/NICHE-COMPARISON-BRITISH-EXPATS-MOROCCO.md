# Niche Comparison Dashboard: British Expats vs Spain-Morocco

## Executive Summary

Two non-standard niches with strong market fundamentals. **British Expats** is faster, lower-risk MVP. **Spain-Morocco B2B** is higher ambition, higher upside.

**Recommendation:** Launch British Expats first (4-6 weeks), validate, then explore Spain-Morocco.

### Unit Economics Comparison

#### British Expats: SaaS Model

```
Assumptions:
- TAM: 90K-130K digitally active
- Conversion (freemium): 3-5% = 2.7K-6.5K signups
- Paid conversion (freemium→Pro): 15-20% = 405-1.3K paying
- Pricing: €12/month average (mix of €9.99-20/month)
- Churn: 8-12%/month (high pain = low churn)
- Lifetime: 20-24 months

Revenue streams:
1. Subscriptions: 600-1.2K users × €12 × 12 = €86.4K-172.8K
2. Affiliate (Wise): 5-10% users × €1.50 per signup × 12 = €9K-18K
3. Lawyer referrals: 200-400 referrals × €75 average = €15K-30K
Total Y1 ARR: €110K-220K

Costs:
- Development (MVP): €5K-10K (outsourced or founder dev)
- Hosting/DB: €100-300/month = €1.2K-3.6K/year
- Customer support: €500-1.5K/month = €6K-18K/year
- Marketing (CAC €30-50): €10K-20K
- Tools (Stripe, email, etc.): €2K-3K/year
Total Y1: €25K-55K

CAC: €50 × 600-1.2K users = €30K-60K
LTV: €12/mo × 20 months = €240 average
LTV/CAC: 4-8x ✅ Healthy

Profitability by Y2: Break-even by month 8-10 (with modest marketing)
```

#### Spain-Morocco B2B: Marketplace + Transaction Fees

```
Assumptions (B2B only, Phase 1):
- Sellers: 500-1K Spanish, 300-500 Moroccan
- Monthly deals: 200-400
- Deal value: €3-8K average
- Transaction fee: 2-3% = €60-240/deal
- Listing fees: €40/mo Spanish, €15/mo Moroccan = €20-40K/year
- Premium sellers: 10% × €99/mo = €12K/year
- Retention: 85% (B2B is sticky)

Monthly Revenue (avg):
- Transaction fees: 300 deals × €150 avg = €45K
- Listing fees: 750 sellers × €27.50 avg = €20.6K
- Premium: €1K
Total MRR: €66.6K → €800K ARR

Costs:
- Development (MVP): €15K-25K (more complex)
- Hosting/infrastructure: €500-1.5K/month = €6K-18K/year
- Payment processing (Wise integration): 1% of transaction volume = €8K-12K/year
- Customer support: €2K-3K/month = €24K-36K/year
- Sales/partnerships: €1K-2K/month = €12K-24K/year
- Tools + legal: €3K-5K/year
Total Y1: €68K-120K

CAC: €150 (B2B sales is slower but higher LTV)
LTV: €2-3K (2-year horizon, 15% churn)
LTV/CAC: 13-20x ✅✅ Excellent

Profitability by Y2: Break-even faster than B2B (transaction-driven)
```

**Winner:** Spain-Morocco has better unit economics (13-20x vs 4-8x), but requires 2x development effort

### Execution Complexity

#### British Expats: Simple ✅

**Technology stack:**
- React frontend (simple forms)
- Basic database (PostgreSQL)
- Third-party integrations: Wise API, basic email
- No real-time requirements, no complex algorithms

**Execution risks:**
- Legal risk: Minimal (providing information only, not legal advice)
- Regulatory: Low (no financial products)
- Integration: Low (just Wise API)

**Timeline to MVP:** 4-6 weeks (founder can solo)

**Team needed:**
- 1x full-stack engineer (founder or contractor €5K-10K)
- 0.5x legal/tax advisor (validation only, not ongoing)

#### Spain-Morocco: Medium-Complex ⚠️

**Technology stack:**
- React frontend (marketplace UI + RFQ system + document builder)
- Backend (Node/Python) for document generation, tariff lookup
- Database (PostgreSQL) with complex relationships
- Payment gateway integration (Wise API + escrow logic)
- Shipping API integration (DHL, FedEx, local couriers)
- Document generation engine (PDF templates, dynamic fields)

**Execution risks:**
- Legal risk: HIGH (customs compliance, payment licensing)
- Regulatory: MEDIUM (payment processor regulations, Morocco customs authority)
- Integration: MEDIUM (multiple APIs, complex data flows)
- Network effects: Chicken-egg problem (need buyers + sellers simultaneously)

**Timeline to MVP:** 8-12 weeks (team of 2-3)

**Team needed:**
- 1x backend engineer (marketplace logic, payments)
- 1x frontend engineer (RFQ + document UI)
- 0.5x lawyer/compliance (customs, payment licensing)
- 0.5x business development (partnership with customs brokers, shipping partners)

## Final Recommendation

### Phase 1: Launch British Expats (Next 4-6 weeks)

**Why:**
- ✅ Fastest MVP (4-6 weeks)
- ✅ Lowest risk (simple SaaS)
- ✅ Clearest acquisition path (FB groups, SEO)
- ✅ High pain (70%+ validation signal)
- ✅ Prove execution ability before tackling complex B2B

**Success targets:**
- 500+ email signups (landing page)
- 100+ MVP users (soft launch)
- 20+ paid signups (Pro tier)
- €5K+ MRR by month 6

**Kill criteria:**
- <50 email signups from €100 FB ad spend (wrong audience)
- <5% MVP users upgrade to Pro (no monetization signal)
- >15% monthly churn (product doesn't solve pain)

### Phase 2: Validate Spain-Morocco (Weeks 6-8, parallel track)

If British Expats shows traction, run validation interviews on Spain-Morocco:
- 10 Spanish exporters
- 10 Moroccan importers
- Gauge pain + WTP

**Proceed to MVP if:**
- ✅ 70%+ cite customs/payment delays as top pain
- ✅ 50%+ would use platform
- ✅ 40%+ would pay €50+/month

### Phase 3: Build Spain-Morocco B2B (Weeks 12-24, if validation passes)

Requires hiring/partnering:
- Backend engineer
