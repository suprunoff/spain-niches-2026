## DECISION MATRIX: Pick Your Path

### **IF YOUR GOAL IS: "Make €5K MRR in 4 months"**
→ **Pick: VUD Tourist Rental (9.1/10)**
- Fastest path to revenue (Week 6-8 first €500 MRR)
- Highest LTV:CAC ratio (4-12:1)
- Lowest technical complexity (relative to speed)
- Downside: 17 regional APIs (but necessary for win)

**Action:** Start with 1-2 regions (Valencia + Madrid), expand to others quarter 2.

---

### **IF YOUR GOAL IS: "Build a defensible SaaS moat"**
→ **Pick: Ley Atención al Cliente (9.0/10)**
- Highest compliance lock-in (law enforcement mandatory)
- 12+ month moat (voice + compliance rules hard to copy)
- Enterprise potential (€500-2K setup fees)
- Downside: Needs sales team, B2B complexity

**Action:** Raise €50K, hire 1 sales person, target 100 companies by Month 6.

---

### **IF YOUR GOAL IS: "Maximum TAM with lowest complexity"**
→ **Pick: Autónomo Compliance Bundle (8.7/10)**
- Largest TAM (€80-200M)
- Longest LTV (€800-1.5K per customer)
- Best land-and-expand (invoicing → tax → payroll → legal)
- Standard SaaS, cleanest code
- Downside: More competitive

**Action:** Build Tier 1 features (VeriFactu + quarterly tax + IRPF calculator), launch with freemium (5 invoices free).

---

### **IF YOUR GOAL IS: "Fast cash flow, then pivot or sell"**
→ **Pick: E-Commerce Seller Compliance (8.8/10)**
- 500K addressable market (huge)
- Fast sales cycle (3-7 days)
- Easy to acquire (Google Ads)
- Product-market fit in 8 weeks
- Downside: High competition risk

**Action:** Validate with 20 sellers (Reddit, Facebook). If 70% say "urgent", build MVP.

## FINAL RECOMMENDATION

### **Pick VUD Tourist Rental if:**
- You want revenue fastest (Month 4-5)
- You're comfortable with API integrations
- You want max competitive moat (regional complexity)
- You want to prove concept and flip to acquirer (Airbnb, Booking)

### **Pick Autónomo Bundle if:**
- You want the biggest long-term SaaS business (€80-200M TAM)
- You want cleanest code and standard SaaS fundamentals
- You plan to raise money or hire a team
- You want land-and-expand (invoicing → tax → payroll → accounting)

### **Pick Ley Atención if:**
- You're ready to build a B2B sales machine
- You can raise €50K and hire an SDR
- You want enterprise deals (€500-2K setup + €150/mth)
- You want 12+ month moat from compliance complexity

### **Pick E-Commerce Seller if:**
- You want maximum TAM with standard SaaS architecture
- You're comfortable with Google Ads acquisition
- You want product-market fit validation fastest
- You're willing to compete on features
