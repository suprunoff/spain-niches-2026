## Revenue Model Hacks

### **1. Government Subsidy as CAC Hack**
- **Kit Digital** grants cover up to €29K SMB digitalization
- VeriFactu SaaS or WhatsApp CRM = Kit Digital eligible
- **Result:** Customer gets free tool, you get €1K-2K installation fee per customer

### **2. Revenue-Share for Subsidies**
- Agricultural subsidies: 1-2% of claimed subsidy value
- Construction licitaciones: 5-10% of contract value sourced
- **Example:** Help farm claim €100K subsidy → earn €1-2K

### **3. Freemium to Forced Upsell**
- Free: 5 invoices/mth (VeriFactu)
- Paid: €15/mth unlimited (natural upgrade when hitting 6th invoice)
- **LTV:** €180-360/yr with 60% conversion

### **4. B2B2C via Resellers**
- Sell to tax advisors (€500-2K/yr) → they resell to 20-50 autónomos each
- Advisors get 20% commission
- **Example:** 500 tax advisors × 30 autónomos × €15/mth = €2.7M ARR

## Red Flags to Avoid

| Red Flag | Impact | How to Check |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Deadline already passed** | No urgency to buy | Verify on government website |
| **Competitor already exists** | Price war inevitable | Google: "[niche name] Spain" + LinkedIn |
| **TAM <€10M** | Not worth 6+ months of building | Bottom-up: # of businesses × WTP |
| **Multi-region regulation** | Each region has different rules (nightmare) | Check: is it Spain-wide or autonomous community? |
| **Requires procurement (Licitaciones)** | 6-12 month sales cycle | Only pick if TAM is €100M+ |

## Validation Checklist (Before Building)

- [ ] Regulation confirmed (found source on La Moncloa, AEPD, EU site)
- [ ] Deadline confirmed (not passed, not vague "2026-2030")
- [ ] TAM estimated (interviews with 5+ target customers)
- [ ] 70%+ say "urgent" or "already losing money"
- [ ] 50%+ say "willing to pay €X/mth"
- [ ] 0 Spanish competitors (Google search + LinkedIn)
- [ ] Estimated build time ≤12 weeks (solo dev)
- [ ] No requirement for sales team (need to validate with 10 customers, not 100)
