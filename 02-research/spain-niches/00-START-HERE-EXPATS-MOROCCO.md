## Pain Validation (Data-Backed)

### British Expats Top Pains:
1. **Residencia (9/10)** — 50% of Brits haven't exchanged residency docs correctly; 2-6 weeks process; confusing forms
2. **Pension tax (8/10)** — UK pension taxed only in Spain; complex DTA; risk of €500-5K/year errors
3. **Healthcare (7/10)** — S1 vs Convenio vs private; unclear eligibility; long registration
4. **Driving license (6/10)** — €65-125; 4-6 weeks; needs medical exam; confusing process
5. **Property tax (7/10)** — Double taxation on UK rental income; £500-5K/year risk

### Spain-Morocco Top Pains:
1. **Customs docs (8/10)** — 5-10 days delay per shipment; complex forms; €100-300 broker fees
2. **SWIFT payments (8/10)** — 3-7 days settlement; 4-7% fees; SWIFT codes often not recognized
3. **Compliance (7/10)** — Tariff changes; double-reporting; uncertainty
4. **Trade finance (7/10)** — No SME-friendly options; cash-in-advance friction
5. **Remittance (6/10)** — €1.375B/year flow at 2-3% fee loss; slow settlement
