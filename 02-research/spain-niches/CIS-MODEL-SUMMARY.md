## Scoring Methodology (Quick)

**Formula:** (Demand × 0.25) + (Competition Gap × 0.25) + (Revenue Potential × 0.20) + (Spain Tailwind × 0.20) + (MVP Speed × 0.10)

- **Demand:** Search volume + addressable market size
- **Gap:** Competitors (5-30 is sweet spot), feature parity
- **Revenue:** LTV × retention rate
- **Spain Tailwind:** Language fit, regulatory, payment method fit, cultural fit
- **MVP Speed:** Inverse scoring (faster = higher score bonus)
