## GO/NO-GO CHECKLIST (Неделя 1)

### Если ДА на все → GO

- [ ] Юрист: "Лицензия не требуется для lead gen или white-label" → **CRITICAL**
- [ ] CustDev (10 семей): ≥40% говорят "платил(а) бы €50-100 за помощь"
- [ ] Звонки (10 фунерариев Madrid): ≥50% готовы платить €80+/лид
- [ ] LinkedIn outreach (Mapfre/Caser): ≥1 "интересно, давайте обсудим"
- [ ] Funos анализ: Не захватила 100% трафика (есть место для #2)

### Если НЕТ на 2+ → PIVOT

- Юрист говорит "нужна лицензия" → Блокирующее
- Менее 30% семей готовы платить → Низкая PMF
- Менее 40% фунерариев готовы платить → B2B модель невизинна
- Все страховые говорят "нет" → Нет B2B моста

### Lead Gen Path

**Go/No-Go Decision:** After 50 leads, if conversion rate ≥10% → Scale

### White-Label Path
```
Week 1-2:  Validation (legal, Mapfre/Caser meetings)
Week 3-4:  Technical design (API, embedded form, funeral DB)
Month 2:   MVP for Mapfre (50 funeral homes, simple UI)
Month 3:   Pilot with 1 regional insurance product
Month 4-6: Contract negotiations, deployment, KPI tracking
```

**Go/No-Go Decision:** After 1st contract signed → Scale (second insurer easier)

## MOAT STRATEGY

### Weeks 1-6 (MVP)
- First-mover in white-label (Funos = B2C only)
- Emotional positioning vs. cold comparison

### Months 3-6 (1st Customer)
- Mapfre + 22.3M customers = data moat (know funeral patterns by region, age, income)
- Lock-in via integration (hard to switch after embedded in portal)

### Months 7-12 (2-3 Customers)
- Network effect: more insurers → better funeral home pricing + faster fulfillment
- Predictive AI: "Based on customer profile, recommend €X funeral package"

### Year 2+
- Acquisition target: Mémora could buy to add white-label to their platform
- Or: Become de facto B2B standard for Spanish insurance + funeral industry

## KEY METRICS (DASHBOARD)

### For Lead Gen

```
Daily:
  - Leads generated (total)
  - Lead quality score (funeral home feedback)
  - Cost per lead (€)
  - Conversion rate (inquiry → call)

Weekly:
  - MRR (monthly recurring revenue from contracts)
  - Funeral home satisfaction (NPS)
  - Churn rate (funeral homes dropping)

Monthly:
  - CAC (actual spend / leads)
  - LTV (lifetime value per funeral home)
  - LTV:CAC ratio (target: >3x)
  - Unit economics margin
```

### For White-Label

```
Daily:
  - Platform availability (uptime %)
  - API latency (msec)
  - Errors/bugs reported

Weekly:
  - Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) per contract
  - Customer support tickets
  - Contract renewal risk

Monthly:
  - NPS (insurer satisfaction)
  - Churn (contracts lost)
  - Expansion (upsell features)
  - Payback period (CAC / monthly contract value)
```
