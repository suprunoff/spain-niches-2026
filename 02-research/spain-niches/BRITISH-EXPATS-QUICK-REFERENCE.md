## The Opportunity (30 seconds)

400K+ British nationals in Spain have no single English-language platform for:
- NIE/residencia bureaucracy
- UK pension tax in Spain
- Brexit compliance
- Driving license exchange
- Health insurance comparison

**Market gap:** Expatica (generic), The Local (news), no specialist player.

**Our angle:** All-in-one platform + affiliate monetization (lawyers, tax advisors, insurance brokers).

## Why Now?

1. **Brexit-induced pain:** Exponential growth in demand for UK pension + tax + residencia info
2. **Post-Brexit maturation:** 5 years → information needs are concrete, not transient
3. **English language moat:** Uniquely positioned (Expatica = generic, The Local = news)
4. **Affiliate ecosystem ready:** Lawyers (€300-500 per client), tax advisors (€500-2K), insurance (€50-300/yr)

## Top Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Churn >20% | Medium | Sticky features (tracker, calendar), affiliate lock-in |
| Low pricing willingness | Low | Freemium (50% free, 50% paid), sponsorship |
| CPC inflation | Low | Organic (SEO) + community partnerships |
| Affiliate low uptake | Low | Direct B2B outreach, low barrier |

**Biggest risk:** Churn. Mitigation = build community + affiliate lock-in, not just portal.

## Key Competitors & Our Moat
