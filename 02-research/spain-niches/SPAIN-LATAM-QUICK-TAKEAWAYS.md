## Quick Localization Checklist (Must-Do Before Launch)

- [ ] **Language:** Spanish (not Castilian — use neutral Spanish in copy)
- [ ] **Payments:** Bizum (Spain), local bank transfers (each LATAM country)
- [ ] **Support hours:** Madrid/Barcelona timezone (9am-6pm) + Mexico City overlap
- [ ] **Compliance:** RGPD (Spain) + local tax laws (each LATAM country)
- [ ] **Pricing:** Regional (ARS/COP/MXN volatile) — accept or offer local currency
- [ ] **WhatsApp API:** Business account + support routing (mandatory for LATAM)

---

## Common Mistakes (Avoid)

❌ **Launching in English** = Automatic failure in LATAM (English proficiency low)
❌ **Using US payment gateway only** = LATAM can't pay (Bizum, local transfers needed)
❌ **Treating LATAM as "one market"** = Tax/compliance rules completely different per country
❌ **Starting in Mexico/Colombia directly** = Use Madrid diaspora as test market first
❌ **Cold email outreach** = WhatsApp only (40-60% vs 0.5% reply rate)
❌ **No founder +1 in target country** = Sales cycles 2x longer without local presence
