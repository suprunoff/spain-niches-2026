## The 3 Niches (Ranked by Score)

### Niche #1: VeriFactu Compliance SaaS ⭐ **HIGHEST PRIORITY**

**Score:** 7.8/10

**The Opportunity**
- LATAM entrepreneurs opening SL in Spain need VeriFactu compliance (mandatory Jan 2027)
- Target: 50K+ LATAM-owned Spanish businesses
- ARPU: €50-99/month

**Status:** Ready for validation

### Niche #2: Multi-Country Payroll SaaS

**Score:** 7.5/10

**The Opportunity**
- Spanish companies expanding to LATAM need unified payroll platform
- Covers: Spain (SEPA), Mexico (SAT), Colombia (DIAN), Chile (SII), Argentina (AFIP)
- Target: 5K+ SMEs expanding to LATAM
- ARPU: €150-300/month
- TAM: €9M annually

**Why Now**
- Spanish SaaS companies expanding faster (Mexico funding +53% 2025)
- Competitors expensive (Factorial = €200+/emp) or enterprise-only
- No "cheap, easy" multi-country solution for SME (€50-150/month range)

**MVP Timeline:** 12-16 weeks
**GTM:** B2B2C via accountant partners + content marketing

**Status:** Ready for validation

### Niche #3: LATAM Business Diaspora Community

**Score:** 7.2/10

**The Opportunity**
- 4.7M Latin Americans in Spain need directory + community + services
- Multiple revenue streams: directory, events, job board, sponsorships
- Target: 70K premium members (5% of 1.4M business owners)
- ARPU: €30-100/month + event sponsorship
- TAM: €7M annually

**Why Now**
- Zero competitors (no dedicated platform exists)

- Low barrier to entry (mostly sweat equity)

**MVP Timeline:** 4-6 weeks
**GTM:** WhatsApp groups + Facebook + free events in Madrid/Barcelona

**Status:** Ready for validation

### Proven Models
- **Factorial HR:** Unicorn (€120M Series C 2022), Barcelona → Mexico → Brazil
- **Holded:** Barcelona HQ + 4 LATAM offices (Mexico, Colombia, Chile, Argentina)
