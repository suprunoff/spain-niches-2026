```
D (Demand) = search volume + urgency + problem intensity [20%]
G (Gap) = competitor analysis + market whitespace [20%]
R (Reachability) = CPC + ease of customer acquisition [15%]
S (Size/TAM) = addressable market in EUR [15%]
M (Monetization) = LTV + churn + unit economics [10%]
```
