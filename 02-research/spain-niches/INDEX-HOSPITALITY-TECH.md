## SCORING METHODOLOGY

**Formula:**
```
Score = (D×1.0 + G×1.5 + R×1.2 + S×1.3 + M×1.0 + A×1.2 + F×1.5 + T×1.0) / 9.2

Where:
  D = Demand intensity (1-10)
  G = Technology gap size (1-10)
  R = Revenue potential (1-10)
  S = Solve difficulty (1-10, inverted: higher = harder)
  M = Margin potential (1-10)
  A = Addressable market size (1-10)
  F = Founder fit / solo-buildable (1-10)
  T = Timeline to MVP (1-10, inverted: shorter = better)
```

**Weights Rationale:**
- **G×1.5:** Gap size is paramount (no solution = instant market)
- **F×1.5:** Solo-buildability critical for bootstrapped founders
- **S×1.3:** Solve difficulty weighted high (hard tech = longer timeline)
- **R×1.2, A×1.2:** Revenue & market size matter but secondary to gap

## KEY LEARNINGS (Spain Hospitality Tech Market)

1. **Regulatory Tailwind:** VUD/NRA compliance creates automatic demand (no marketing needed)
2. **SME Tech Gap:** 60%+ of hospitality still uses legacy systems or spreadsheets
3. **WhatsApp is Standard:** 40% of Spanish SMEs prefer WhatsApp for business comms
4. **Language Matters:** English-first products lose 20%+ of Spain market; Catalan +20% CR
5. **Margin Sensitivity:** Hotels/restaurants sensitive to >€50/month tools; lower pricing = higher adoption
6. **Trust Through ROI:** Businesses only convert when they see measurable ROI within first month
7. **Bizum is Essential:** Payment processing without Bizum loses 30%+ of Spanish customer willingness to pay
