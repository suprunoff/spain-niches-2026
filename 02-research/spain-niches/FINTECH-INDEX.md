## The 5 FinTech Niches

### 🥇 #1 — Rental Insurance Comparador (8.5/10)

**What:** Online comparador for tenant + landlord rental insurance.
**Why:** 14-16M renters, no aggregator, Idealista proved demand (June 2025).
**TAM:** €1-2B | **Growth:** +5.81% | **Competition:** Weak
**Status:** ✅ **GO** | **MVP:** 4 weeks | **CAC:** <€10

👉 **Start here if:** You have insurance contacts or know real estate market

---

### 🥈 #2 — Non-Resident Mortgage Broker (8.2/10)

**What:** 100% online mortgage broker for foreign property buyers.
**Why:** 100K+ foreigners buy annually (14-20% of market), process = 6-8 weeks.
**TAM:** €300-600M | **Growth:** +7.5% | **Competition:** 4-5 weak
**Status:** ✅ **GO** | **MVP:** 6-8 weeks | **ACV:** €1500-2500

👉 **Start here if:** You have bank contacts or expat networks

---

### 🥉 #3 — VeriFactu SaaS (RegTech) (8.0/10)

**What:** Invoice SaaS with automatic tax compliance (VeriFactu).
**Why:** 100% mandatory adoption by July 2027, 80% unprepared, €3000+ penalty.
**TAM:** €250-500M | **Growth:** 100% | **Competition:** None mid-market
**Status:** ✅ **GO** | **MVP:** 8-10 weeks | **CAC:** <€30

👉 **Start here if:** You code, understand tax, have autónomo contacts

---

### 4️⃣ #4 — SME Open Banking API (7.8/10)

**What:** Accounting + cash flow SaaS via Open Banking (PSD2).
**Why:** 95% banks ready, 80% SME do accounting manually, clear pain.
**TAM:** €300M-2B | **Growth:** +20% | **Competition:** Strong (BBVA)
**Status:** 🔍 **EXPLORE** | **MVP:** 12-16 weeks | **CAC:** <€40

👉 **Start here if:** You want long-term moat, integrations don't scare you

---

### 5️⃣ #5 — Embedded Insurance (7.5/10)

**What:** Insurance widget for e-commerce checkout (product protection).
**Why:** Zero competition in Spain, 70%+ use e-commerce, clear pain.
**TAM:** €2-3B | **Growth:** +12% | **Competition:** None
**Status:** 🔍 **EXPLORE** | **MVP:** 10 weeks | **Barrier:** Very High

👉 **Start here if:** E-commerce network + 6-12 months for licenses

## CustDev Validation Plan

### Phase 1: Parallel Interviews (Week 1-2)

**For each niche, 10-20 interviews:**

1. **Rental Insurance**
   - 5 renters (30-45 age, urban)
   - 3 landlords (property owners)
   - 2 insurance agents (brokers/companies)

2. **Non-Resident Mortgage**
   - 5 expats searching for property (UK, US, LatAm)
   - 3 bank relationship managers
   - 2 competing mortgage brokers (win-loss)

3. **VeriFactu SaaS**
   - 10 autónomos (various industries)
   - 10 SME founders (5-50 people)
   - 1 call with AEAT (Tax Agency)

4. **Open Banking API**
   - 10 SME owners (accounting pain)
   - 3 accounting software companies (partnership potential)
   - 2 banks (integration feasibility)

5. **Embedded Insurance**
   - 8 e-commerce site owners (traffic >1K/month)
   - 3 insurance companies (white-label interest)
   - 2 payment processors (integration)

### Phase 2: MVP Proof (Week 3-4)

- Build landing page mockup
- Run €100-200 ads test
- Measure: CTR >5%, conversion rate >10%

### Phase 3: Go/No-Go (Week 4)

Based on feedback:
- **Go:** Proceed to MVP build
- **Pivot:** Shift niche based on feedback
- **No-Go:** Move to next opportunity

## Spain Context (MANDATORY CHECKLIST)

Before you build **anything**, verify:

- [ ] **Warm intros:** Do you have personal connections?
- [ ] **Spanish:** Can you communicate in Spanish?
- [ ] **WhatsApp:** Will you use WhatsApp for customer acquisition? (40-60% reply rate)
- [ ] **Bizum:** Do you accept Bizum for payments? (B2C only)
- [ ] **Google Reviews:** Will you target 4.0+ rating? (92% check before buying)
- [ ] **Freemium:** Will you offer free trial? (85% try before paying)
- [ ] **Avoid August:** Will you launch Sept-Oct or May-June? (August = 0 activity)
- [ ] **Regional:** Do you understand Madrid (fast) vs Barcelona (slow)?
- [ ] **Regulatory:** Have you read the latest compliance requirements?
