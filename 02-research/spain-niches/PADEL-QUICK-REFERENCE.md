## 📱 Market Context (Spain-Specific)

✅ **Strengths:**
- Mobile-first: 80%+ of padel bookings mobile
- Bizum adoption: 32.5M users, €67.7B volume 2025
- Personal relationships: WhatsApp = 40-60% response rate vs email 0.5%
- Young clubs: Built 2015-2025, use modern tools
- High digital adoption: B2B SaaS growing in Spain

⚠️ **Challenges:**
- Playtomic network effect (hard to displace)
- SMB budgets: smaller than US (€100-150/month realistic max)
- Slow decision-making: 2-4 weeks for SMB (vs 1 week in US)
- Regional differences: Madrid fast, Andalusia slow
- Churn risk: Small businesses churn 5-10%/month if unhappy
