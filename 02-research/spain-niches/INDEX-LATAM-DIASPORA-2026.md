## Business Model Options

### Model 1: Directory + Premium Listings
- **Revenue:** €1M–3.6M/year
- **Unit economics:** 30% penetration, €20/month
- **Pros:** Simple, Yelp-like, no compliance
- **Cons:** Low margin, high competition

### Model 2: Job Board (Recommended for specialization)
- **Revenue:** €500K–1M/year
- **Unit economics:** €100–300/posting, 50% conversion
- **Pros:** High engagement, unique LATAM angle
- **Cons:** Needs 100+ vacancies/day to scale

### Model 3: Services Marketplace
- **Revenue:** €75K–300K/year
- **Unit economics:** 15% commission, €2K avg project
- **Pros:** High margin, sticky users
- **Cons:** Trust hard to build (freelancer scams)

### Model 4: Remittances Hub (HIGHEST UPSIDE)
- **Revenue:** €700K–10.7M/year potential
- **Unit economics:** €5–10 affiliate per transaction
- **Pros:** €10.7B market, viral potential, passive income
- **Cons:** Highly competitive, regulatory burden

### **Recommended:** Hybrid (Directory + Services + Remittances)
- Combines all 4 models
- Y1 forecast: €1.34M MRR = €1.15M profit

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Weak)
- **Directorio Latinos** — Old UI, no mobile, no monetization
- **Conexión de Negocios Latinos** — Event-driven, not online
- **La Red de Hispanos** — 5K businesses only (tiny)
- **Une Americas** — B2B focus, not SMB-friendly

**Verdict:** Ripe for disruption. No strong player = opportunity.

### Indirect Competitors (Strong)
- **Remittances:** Wise, Remitly, WorldRemit (fragmented)
- **Jobs:** Indeed, InfoJobs, Computrabajo (not LATAM-specific)
- **Services:** Upwork, Fiverr, Toptal (no Spain focus)

**Competitive advantage:** Aggregate these + add cultural layer (language, trust, community)

## Validation Checklist (Pre-MVP)

**Target:** 20 interviews with LATAM businesspeople in Madrid

### Critical Questions
- [ ] Would you pay €20/month for a premium listing? Why/why not?
- [ ] Where do you currently find customers? (word-of-mouth %, Google %, Facebook %)
- [ ] Do you send money home to LATAM? How often? Which service? (Wise, Remitly, WhatsApp, other)
- [ ] What's your biggest pain point as LATAM business owner in Spain?
- [ ] Would you use a job board specifically for LATAM workers? Why?

### Success Criteria (Go/No-Go)
- ✅ **GO** if:
  - 1K+ businesses register organically (month 1)
  - 4.2+ avg rating (trust)
  - 50%+ say "yes" to €20/month question
  - 70%+ send money home + pain with current services

- ❌ **NO-GO** if:
  - <100 businesses after 2 months
  - <3.0 avg rating
  - CAC > €20 with paid ads

## MVP (Month 1–2)

### Core Features
1. **Directory**
   - Search by nationality, profession, city
   - Business profiles + reviews + rating
   - WhatsApp integration (direct message to business)
   - Photo gallery, hours, location

2. **Remittances Aggregator**
   - Wise API integration (show best rate for each country)
   - Remitly affiliate links
   - Comparison tool (ES → Colombia, Venezuela, Ecuador, etc.)

3. **Analytics Dashboard (for businesses)**
   - Profile views, search traffic, message volume
   - Basic reporting

### Tech Stack (Recommendation)
- **Frontend:** Next.js (React) + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) or Firebase
- **Payments:** Stripe (directory), Wise API (remittances)
- **WhatsApp:** Twilio or WhatsApp Business API
- **Hosting:** Vercel (frontend) + Supabase (backend)

### Timeline
- Week 1–2: Design + database schema
- Week 3–4: MVP build (directory + WhatsApp)
- Week 5: Soft launch (500 testers)
- Week 6–8: Iterate + add remittances integration

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Network effects failure (chicken-egg) | High | Very High | Seed with founder's friends, pay early adopters, community leaders |
| Low monetization (free preference) | Medium | Medium | Freemium model, affiliate revenue (remittances = passive) |
| Regulatory (PSD2, AML/KYC for remittances) | Low | High | Partner with Wise/Remitly (they handle compliance) |
| Fraud/chargeback on marketplace | Medium | Medium | Stripe Radar, KYC for businesses, escrow for services |
| Slow product iteration | High | High | Weekly releases, aggressive feedback loops |
| Competition from large players (Yelp, Wise) | Medium | High | Build community moat, focus on LATAM culture (not replicable) |
| Cultural distrust (scams in marketplace) | High | Medium | Reviews + verification badges, community moderation |
