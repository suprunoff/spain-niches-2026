**Status:** Discovery Phase | **Score:** 8.2/10 | **Date:** 2026-03-02

## The Opportunity (30 seconds)

**What?** Platform for 4.7M Latin Americans in Spain: directory (find businesses) + job board + remittances hub + services marketplace.

**Why?** Huge diaspora (Colombia 857K, Venezuela 600K, Ecuador 449K) with zero dedicated platform. They use fragmented WhatsApp groups + old directories. Timing: immigration wave + VeriFactu 2027 deadline.

**How much?** €235M TAM, €2–5M SAM, €1.15M profit Y1 (85% margin), 48x LTV/CAC.

**When?** 6 weeks to MVP, 3 months to first €100K MRR.

## Competition (Weak = Opportunity)

**Direct:**
- Directorio Latinos — old UI, no mobile, no monetization
- Conexión de Negocios Latinos — event-driven, not online
- La Red de Hispanos — 5K businesses (tiny)

**Verdict:** Ripe for disruption.

**Indirect:**
- Wise, Remitly, WorldRemit (remittances) — fragmented, no aggregation
- Indeed, InfoJobs (jobs) — not LATAM-specific
- Upwork, Fiverr (services) — no Spain focus

**Your edge:** Cultural + language + community (not replicable by Yelp)

## Validation Criteria (20 interviews required)

### Critical Questions
1. **Pricing:** Would you pay €20/month for premium listing?
2. **Current solution:** Where do you find customers? (% breakdown)
3. **Remittances:** Do you send money home? Which service? Pain points?
4. **Pain:** Biggest challenge as LATAM business owner?
5. **Jobs:** Would you use LATAM-specific job board?

### Go/No-Go Thresholds

✅ **GO** if:
- 1K+ organically register in month 1
- 4.2+ avg rating (trust)
- 50%+ willing to pay €20/month
- 70%+ send money + dissatisfied with current service

❌ **NO-GO** if:
- <100 businesses after 2 months
- <3.0 avg rating
- <30% willing to pay
- CAC > €20 with paid ads
