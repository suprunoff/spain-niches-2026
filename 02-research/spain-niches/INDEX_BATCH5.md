## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

**Scoring Formula:** (D×20 + G×20 + R×15 + S×15 + M×10 + A×10 + F×5 + T×5) / 100

| Factor | Weight | Definition |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **D** Demand Signal | 20% | Search volume, pain, community size |
| **G** Competition Gap | 20% | No Spain-specific solutions (0-1 competitor = 10) |
| **R** Revenue Potential | 15% | LTV × TAM / monetization ease |
| **S** Spain Tailwind | 15% | Regulatory mandate, cultural fit, timing |
| **M** MVP Speed | 10% | 1 week=10, 4-6 weeks=8, 10-14 weeks=6, 16+ weeks=4 |
| **A** Accessibility | 10% | Ease of first 10 customers (CAC efficiency) |
| **F** Defensibility | 5% | Data, API, network, regulatory moat |
| **T** Timing Urgency | 5% | Regulatory deadline, closing window |
