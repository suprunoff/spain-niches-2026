EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

NICHE: Funerarias Platform Spain (Digital funeral services aggregator)

MARKET SIZE: €1.5B-2B (433K deaths/year × €4,430 avg funeral cost)

OPPORTUNITY: Digital market is 100% uncontested for white-label B2B to insurers
(22.3M Spaniards with seguro de deceso currently served via 1990s phone)

THREE BUSINESS MODELS:
1. Lead Gen (Funos-like): B2C comparison → funeral homes pay €50-150/lead
   - Year 1: €270K revenue, 83% margin, 5-month payback
   - Risk: High competition (Funos dominates)

2. Pre-Planning Subscription: In-life funeral planning (€10-20/month)
   - Year 1: €75K revenue, 55% margin, 8-month payback
   - Risk: Slow adoption, psychological barrier, 6-month vesting

3. White-Label B2B (RECOMMENDED): Platform embedded in insurer portals
   - Year 1: €500K+ revenue, 77% margin, 5-month payback
   - Risk: Enterprise sales (3-6 months), higher CAC
   - Advantage: UNCONTESTED, huge TAM (22.3M), defensible moat

RECOMMENDATION: Hybrid strategy
- Primary: White-Label B2B to insurers (Mapfre, Caser, Mutua)
- Secondary: Lead gen as cash flow bridge
- Expected Year 1 profit: €200K-250K

GO/NO-GO DECISION: 7 DAYS
- Legal: "Is licensing required?" (CRITICAL)
- CustDev: 40%+ families willing to pay for help
- Sales: 50%+ funeral homes willing to pay per lead
- Insurers: 1+ positive meeting signal
✅ If ≥4/5 checks pass: GO
❌ If ≤2/5 checks pass: PIVOT

KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

MARKET TAM:
- Deaths/year in Spain: 433,547 (INE 2024)
- Average funeral cost: €4,430 (2025, +4.3% YoY)
- Market revenue: €1.716B (2024), €1.92B calculated TAM
- Insured with seguro de deceso: 22.3M (45% population)
- Growth: +1.5% CAGR (demographic tailwind)
- Digital adoption: +5-7% CAGR (opportunity gap)

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE:
- Funos (B2C lead gen): Market leader, 5K-20K monthly traffic
- Cofune, Funeonline: Weak players
- Mémora (traditional funeral operator): Offline monopoly, not yet digital threat
- White-label B2B space: COMPLETELY UNCONTESTED (0 competitors)

UNIT ECONOMICS:
- Lead Gen: €100 revenue/lead, €20-30 CAC, €70 margin, 3-5x LTV:CAC
- White-Label: €3-10K/contract, €10-30K CAC, 12-month payback, 3-12x LTV:CAC
- Pre-Planning: €15/month subscription, €40 CAC, 180 LTV, 60-month payback

FIRST-MOVER ADVANTAGE:
- Funos validated B2C model (good for validation, bad for competition)
- White-label is completely uncontested (Funos doesn't compete here)
- First company to sign Mapfre/Caser gets lock-in (hard to switch)

COMPETITIVE POSITIONING
================================================================================

vs. Funos (Lead Gen Leader):
- Our advantage: Emotional angle (Aura-style) vs. clinical comparison
- Our advantage: White-label (uncontested) vs. B2C only
- Their advantage: First-mover, traffic, brand awareness
- Our strategy: Don't compete on lead gen; win on white-label

vs. Traditional Funeral Homes:
- Their advantage: Offline relationships, local trust
- Our advantage: Digital, price transparency, family support
- Our strategy: Complement (send them leads), not displace

vs. Mémora (Funeral Monopoly):
- Their advantage: 135+ locations, 50K+ services/year
- Their disadvantage: Offline-first, no digital transparency
- Our strategy: Partner or be acquired (white-label becomes product)

vs. International Platforms (Beyond.life, Aura, Ever Loved):
- Their advantage: Proven models in US/UK
- Our advantage: Localized for Spain, first-mover here
- Our strategy: Replicate + adapt to Spanish market

FINAL RECOMMENDATION
================================================================================

✅ GO with WHITE-LABEL B2B + LEAD GEN HYBRID strategy

Why White-Label Primary:
1. Zero competition (Funos doesn't pursue B2B enterprise)
2. 22.3M TAM via insurance customers
3. High margin (77%) with bigger absolute revenue
4. Defensible moat (contracts, integration lock-in)
5. Better exit potential (Mémora acquisition €20-50M)

Why Lead Gen Secondary:
1. Validates market demand (proven with Funos)
2. Generates cash flow while white-label sales cycle
3. Same customer base (funeral homes)
4. Easy pivot if white-label stalls

Timeline:
- Validation: Week 1-2
- MVP: Week 3-4 + Month 2
- First revenue: Month 4-6 (lead gen) or Month 5-6 (white-label)
- Scale: Month 7-12

Success Criteria (Year 1):
- 1+ insurer contract OR
- 500+ qualified leads/month OR
- €100K+ revenue (either model)

Kill Criteria (Year 1):
- Legal blocks white-label AND lead gen shows <3x LTV:CAC
- 0 positive insurer meetings after 3 months of outreach
- CustDev shows <30% willing to pay
