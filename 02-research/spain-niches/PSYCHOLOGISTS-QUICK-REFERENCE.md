# Psychologists Online Directory Spain — Quick Reference

## Elevator Pitch
**B2C/B2B marketplace for licensed psychologists in Spain**. Post-COVID therapy boom (+40%/year), 97.7K psychologists mostly invisible online. €140-160M TAM. Unit economics: LTV/CAC 3.5x (B2C), 240-400x (B2B).

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|

| **Size** | €140-160M TAM (€202M B2C + €150M B2B) |

## Unit Economics — Simplified

### B2C Marketplace
```
Patient pays: €50/session
Platform takes: 15% = €7.50
Therapist keeps: €42.50

Target patients per therapist: 50/month
Revenue per therapist: €375/month × 15% = €375
Premium subscription (therapist): €25/month
Total: €400/month = €4,800/year

Scale: 200 therapists = €960K/year gross revenue
Costs: €400K (support, infrastructure, salaries, marketing)
Gross profit: €560K = 58% margin
```

### B2B EAP
```
Company size: 100-500 employees
Contract value: €150K/year (average)
CAC: €4K (enterprise sales)
Churn: 12.5%/year

LTV = €150K / 0.125 = €1.2M (8-year customer lifetime)
LTV/CAC = 1,200K / 4K = 300x ✓✓ (exceptional)

100 contracts = €15M revenue
Minus: payouts (30%), salaries, infra = 66% margin
```
