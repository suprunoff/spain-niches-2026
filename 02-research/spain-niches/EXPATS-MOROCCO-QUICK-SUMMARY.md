# British Expats + Spain-Morocco: Quick Summary

## Ниша А: British Expats in Spain — ExpatsOS

**Market Size:** 412K Brits in Spain, 90K–130K digitally active, 25K willing to pay SaaS
**Location:** Alicante (99K), Málaga (74K), Canary/Balearic Islands
**Pain:** Residencia bureaucracy (2–6 weeks), pension taxes, healthcare setup, driving license, double taxation

**MVP:** All-in-one checklist + calculator platform
- Residencia tracker (what to do, deadlines, checklist)
- Pension tax calculator (Spain obligation + UK relief)
- Healthcare guide (S1 vs Convenio vs private)
- Driving license tracker
- UK property tax tool

**Monetization:** Freemium + Pro (€9.99–14.99/mo) + affiliate (Wise €1–2, lawyers €50–100)

**Unit Economics:**
- Y1: 5–8K signups, 600–1.2K paid @ €12/mo = €86K–173K ARR
- CAC: €30–50 (FB + organic)
- LTV: €300–400 (3 yrs)
- LTV/CAC: 6–8x ✅

**Go/No-Go:** ✅ **GO** — fast MVP (4–6 weeks), high pain, no direct competitors, proven CAC channels

**Start:** 15–20 validation interviews (Brits in Spain) → landing page test (€100 FB ads) → MVP build

## Ниша Б: Spain-Morocco B2B Trade — TradeFlow

**Market Size:** €22.7B trade corridor, 5–8K Spanish + 10–15K Moroccan SMEs, 0.79M diaspora in Spain
**Key Pain:** Customs docs (5–10 days), SWIFT payments (3–7 days, 4–7% fee), no trade finance, compliance chaos

**MVP: TradeFlow — B2B marketplace + docs automation + payments**
1. Supplier directory (Spanish + Moroccan)
2. RFQ system (smart bidding)
3. Automated documentation (commercial invoice, CoO, HS codes, tariff calc)
4. Compliance checker (duty calculator, trade agreement benefits)
5. Escrow payments (Wise integration)
6. Shipping partner integration

**Monetization:**
- B2B: Listing fees (€50 Spanish, €20 Moroccan), transaction fees (2–3%), premium seller (€99/mo), compliance docs (€10)
- Remittance (Phase 2): 0.79M Moroccans × 30% = 240K users, €150 avg, 1.5% fee = €162K/month

**Unit Economics (B2B only):**
- Y1: 500–1K Spanish, 300–500 Moroccan sellers, 200–400 monthly deals
- ARR: €250K–700K (listing + transaction)
- CAC: €100–200
- LTV: €2–3K (2 yrs, 12% churn)
- LTV/CAC: 10–20x ✅✅

**Upside (Remittance Phase 2):** €1.944M/yr if executed

**Go/No-Go:** ✅ **CONDITIONAL GO** — strong B2B fundamentals, but longer build (8–12 weeks), regulatory complexity, need domain expertise

**Start:** 10 Spanish exporters + 10 Moroccan importers interviews → partner with customs broker → MVP build
