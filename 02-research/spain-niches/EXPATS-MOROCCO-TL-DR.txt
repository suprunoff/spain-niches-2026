NICHE A: BRITISH EXPATS IN SPAIN — MVP: "ExpatsOS"
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Market Size:
  • 412K Britons in Spain
  • 90-130K digitally active
  • 25-40K willing to pay SaaS
  • Growing +1-2%/year despite Brexit

Top Pain Points (ranked by severity):
  1. Residencia bureaucracy (9/10) — 2-6 weeks, confusing docs, 50% haven't done it
  2. Pension tax confusion (8/10) — UK pension taxed in Spain, DTA complex
  3. Healthcare setup (7/10) — S1 vs Convenio vs private, unclear eligibility
  4. Driving license exchange (6/10) — €65-125, 4-6 weeks, medical exam needed
  5. Property tax/remortgage (7/10) — Double taxation risk, £500-5K/year

MVP Features (4-6 weeks to build):
  • Residencia Tracker — timeline, checklist, nearest office, appointment help
  • Pension Tax Calculator — input pension type/amount → tax owed Spain + UK relief
  • Healthcare Decision Tree — Q&A → S1 vs Convenio vs private recommendation
  • Driving License Tracker — deadline, required docs, nearest Jefatura
  • UK Property Tax Tool — rental income calculator, CGT scenario planner

Monetization:
  • Freemium (free tracker + basic calcs)
  • Pro tier: €9.99-14.99/month (advanced calcs, PDF export, advisor chat)
  • Affiliate: Wise (€1-2/signup), lawyers (€50-100 referral), mortgage brokers

Unit Economics (Y1):
  • Signups: 5-8K
  • Paid users: 600-1.2K @ €12/month
  • Revenue: €86-173K
  • CAC: €30-50 (FB ads + organic)
  • LTV: €300-400 (3 years, 8% churn)
  • LTV/CAC: 6-8x ✅ HEALTHY

Competition:
  • Expatica (content-only, acquired by Wise)
  • Spain Expat (static FAQ forum)
  • Wise (payments-only, no expat-specific help)
  • NO direct SaaS competitor solving residencia/tax/healthcare

Growth Channels:
  • Facebook groups ("British Expats Spain" = 50K+ members)
  • SEO ("UK pension Spain tax", "residencia timeline")
  • Affiliate partnerships (Wise, lawyers)
  • WhatsApp communities

Risk Assessment:
  • Market adoption: LOW (high pain, clear use case)
  • Product-market fit: LOW (direct CAC channels exist)
  • Regulatory: LOW (information-only, not legal advice)
  • Monetization: LOW (freemium model proven)
  • Team: LOW (standard SaaS tech)

Decision: ✅✅ GO (8.5/10 score)
  • Fastest MVP (4-6 weeks)
  • Lowest risk (simple SaaS)
  • Clearest acquisition (FB + SEO)
  • High pain validation (70%+)

Timeline:
  Week 1-2: Validate with 20 CustDev interviews
  Week 2-3: Landing page + €100 ad test
  Week 3-4: MVP build
  Week 4: Soft launch
  Week 6-8: Growth + iterate

First paying customer: Week 6-8
Break-even: Month 9-12

NICHE B: SPAIN-MOROCCO B2B TRADE CORRIDOR — MVP: "TradeFlow"
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Market Size:
  • €22.7B annual trade (2024, record high)
  • Spain exports €12.86B to Morocco
  • Morocco exports €9.83B to Spain
  • 5-8K Spanish SMEs trading with Morocco
  • 10-15K Moroccan SMEs exporting to Spain
  • 0.79M Moroccan diaspora in Spain (remittance upside)

Top Pain Points (ranked by severity):
  1. Customs documentation (8/10) — 5-10 days delay, complex forms, €100-300 broker fee
  2. SWIFT payments (8/10) — 3-7 days, 4-7% fee, SWIFT codes often not recognized
  3. Regulatory uncertainty (7/10) — Tariff changes, compliance burden, double-reporting
  4. Trade finance gap (7/10) — No SME-friendly options, cash-in-advance friction
  5. Remittance inefficiency (6/10) — €1.375B/year, 2-3% fee loss, 2-3 day settlement

MVP Features (8-12 weeks to build):
  • B2B Supplier Directory — Spanish exporters + Moroccan importers (verified profiles)
  • Smart RFQ System — buyers post needs, sellers bid, aggregated offers
  • Automated Documentation — template-driven docs (commercial invoice, CoO, HS codes)
  • Compliance Checker — tariff calculator, trade agreement eligibility, duty estimator
  • Payment Gateway — Wise integration, escrow (buyer deposits, seller ships, release)
  • Shipping Integration — DHL, FedEx, local couriers bundled with tracking + insurance

Monetization:
  • Listing fees: €50/month Spanish, €20/month Moroccan
  • Transaction fees: 2-3% on settled deals (core revenue)
  • Premium seller: €99/month (featured listing, priority support)
  • Compliance reports: €10/export doc batch
  • Affiliate: 2-5% commission on routed shipping

Unit Economics (Y1, B2B only):
  • Sellers: 500-1K Spanish, 300-500 Moroccan
  • Monthly deals: 200-400
  • Deal value: €3-8K average
  • Revenue: €250-700K ARR
  • CAC: €150 (B2B sales)
  • LTV: €2-3K (2 years, 15% churn)
  • LTV/CAC: 10-20x ✅✅ EXCELLENT

Upside (Phase 2, Remittance):
  • 0.79M Moroccan diaspora in Spain × 30% digital = 240K users
  • €150 average transfer
  • 1.5% fee = €162K/month = €1.944M/year

Competition:
  • Wise (dominates remittance, no B2B trade tools)
  • Bnext (blockchain, limited adoption)
  • B2BMap (generic, no ES-MA specialization)
  • NO direct ES-MA specialist platform

Growth Channels:
  • Spanish export associations (ICEX, Chamber of Commerce)
  • Moroccan business associations (CGEM)
  • Google Ads (high intent: "Morocco export docs", "Spain tariff calculator")
  • LinkedIn B2B marketplace
  • Trade publications

Risk Assessment:
  • Market adoption: MEDIUM (chicken-egg problem with marketplace)
  • Product-market fit: MEDIUM (B2B sales cycle longer, need validation)
  • Regulatory: HIGH (customs compliance, payment licensing)
  • Monetization: MEDIUM (transaction-dependent, unpredictable volume)
  • Team: HIGH (needs trade/logistics/legal expertise)

Decision: ⚠️ CONDITIONAL GO (7.0/10 score)
  • Strong unit economics (10-20x LTV/CAC)
  • Huge market (€22.7B trade corridor)
  • Longer build time (8-12 weeks, team of 2-3)
  • Higher regulatory risk (customs, payments)
  • Needs domain expertise

Timeline:
  Week 6-8: Validation interviews (10 Spanish + 10 Moroccan)
  Week 9-10: Decide to proceed or pivot
  Week 12-24: MVP build (if validation passes)

First paying customer: Week 14-16
Break-even: Month 12-18
