## THE IDEA IN 30 SECONDS

**Problem:** 433K deaths/year in Spain. Families in crisis moment compare 0 funeral homes, get overcharged €2,500-3,000. Funeral industry fully offline & opaque.

**Solution:** Digital platform that:
- **Lead Gen:** Shows families 3-5 funeral homes + prices → funeral homes pay €50-150/lead
- **Pre-Planning:** Subscription (€10-20/mo) for in-life funeral planning + escrow
- **White-Label:** Platform embedded in insurance company portals (Mapfre, Caser, Mutua) → 22.3M users get funeral comparison tool

**Why Now:**
- Spain is 3-7 years behind UK/USA in digital adoption
- Funos (lead gen model) proved market exists
- **White-label is completely uncontested** (nobody doing it)
- 22.3M Spaniards with "seguro de deceso" still use 1990s phone-based service

```

Funeral home pays platform €50-150/lead
```

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue/lead | €50-150 |
| Leads/month target | 500+ |

**Best if:** Traffic/SEO expertise, want fast cash flow


| Metric | Value |
