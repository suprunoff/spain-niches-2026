## МОДЕЛЬ 1: Lead Generation (Funos Template)

### Структура

```
Family in crisis
    ↓
  Input: "Мадрид, захоронение"
    ↓
Platform: Shows 3-5 funeral homes + prices
    ↓
Family calls/WhatsApp (with funeral home contact)
    ↓
Funeral home PAYS platform €50-150/lead
    ↓
Platform revenue: 500 leads/month × €100 = €50K/month
```

### Финансовая модель (год 1)

| Месяц | Лидов | Цена/лид | Расходы | Доход | Profit |
|-------|-------|----------|---------|-------|--------|
| 1-3 | 50 | €80 | €5K | €4K | -€1K |
| 4-6 | 250 | €100 | €10K | €25K | +€15K |
| 7-12 | 600 | €120 | €30K | €72K | +€42K |
| **YEAR 1** | **2,700** | **€100** | **€45K** | **€270K** | **€225K** |

**Margin:** 83%
**Break-even:** Месяц 5

### Плюсы ✅
- Быстро к revenue (лиды платят сразу)
- Low CAC (cold calls + social)
- Высокая маржа (83%)
- Фунерарии уже знают value (экономия €500-2K на 1 похоронах)
- Funos валидировал модель (значит рынок существует)

### Минусы ❌
- **Конкуренция с Funos** (лидер, уже захватил Madrid)
- Низкая LTV:CAC (< 2x if Funos dominates pricing)
- Фунерарии могут саботировать (нежелание платить, если монополист)
- Зависит от Google Ads (CPL может подняться до €50-80)
- No emotional connection (transactional only)

### Когда выбрать эту модель
✅ Если Funos слабая (трафик <5K/месяц)
✅ Если фунерарии готовы платить €100+ (validation скажет)
✅ Если нет интереса от страховых (white-label не проходит)
❌ Если Funos доминирует (>20K traffic, 1000+ leads/месяц)

### PMF Signal
- CPC ≤ €2.50 (Google Ads profitability)
- Conversion rate: input → contact ≥ 10%
- Funeral home repeat rate: 80%+ (платят за второй лид)
- NPS > 40 (funeral homes satisfied)

## МОДЕЛЬ 2: Pre-Planning Subscription

### Структура

```
Live person (age 55-75)
    ↓
Chooses: "Direct cremation + flowers + memorial"
Decides: "Pay €15/month"
    ↓
Platform holds funds in escrow (€15 × 12 months × 10 years = €1,800)
    ↓
Person dies → Platform releases to funeral home + takes 5-10%
Platform revenue: €1,800 × 10% = €180/customer
    ↓
Monthly subscription: 2,000 customers × €15 = €30K/month (recurring)
    ↓
Plus: Premium designs (€20-50), digital memorial upgrades (€5-20)
```

### Финансовая модель (год 1)

| Месяц | Subscribers | MRR | Escrow Revenue | CAC | Profit |
|-------|-------------|-----|----------------|-----|--------|
| 1-3 | 100 | €1.5K | €0 | €3K | -€1.5K |
| 4-6 | 400 | €6K | €2K (first cohort dies) | €5K | +€3K |
| 7-12 | 1,200 | €18K | €8K (monthly) | €10K | +€16K |
| **YEAR 1** | **1,200** | **€18K/mo** | **€24K** | **€30K** | **€50K** |

**Margin:** 55% (lower, зависит от escrow management)
**Break-even:** Месяц 8-10 (долго!)

### Плюсы ✅
- **Sticky model** (люди платят за свою смерть, мотивация высокая)
- Высокая LTV (€180 за customer × 10 лет)
- Emotional connection (люди хотят контролировать свой funeral)
- Less competition (pre-planning новый в Испании)
- Viral (когда человек планирует, рассказывает друзьям/семье)
- Multiple revenue streams (subscriptions + escrow + premium features)

### Минусы ❌
- **Долгое вызревание** (первые лиды/деньги только в месяц 4-6)
- CAC высокий (€40-60 on content + paid ads)
- Escrow complexity (нужен финтех-партнёр или лицензия брокера)
- Legal risk (RGPD для долгосрочных данных, escrow regulations)
- Churn (если человек забывает платить)
- Psychological barrier (люди избегают думать о смерти)

### Когда выбрать эту модель
✅ Если есть финтех-партнёр (escrow уже есть)
✅ Если верите в emotional angle vs. transactional (Aura-style)
✅ Если willing to wait 6-12 месяцев на первый revenue
✅ Если нацелены на millennial + Gen X (tech-savvy, emotional)
❌ Если нужны деньги прямо сейчас (bootstrap mode)

### PMF Signal
- Retention rate: 80%+ (не отменяют подписку)
- Referral rate: 20%+ (NPS > 50)
- ARPU growth: €15 → €20 (upsell premium features)
- Escrow completion rate: 95%+ (people actually die with account active)

## МОДЕЛЬ 3: White-Label B2B для Страховых (РЕКОМЕНДУЕТСЯ)

### Структура

```
Mapfre has 31M customers with "seguro de deceso"
Currently: Phone call to call center when death happens (bad UX)
    ↓
Mapfre integrates our white-label platform:
  - Customer logs in → sees "funeral planning tool"
  - Compares 50-100 funeral homes by price + distance
  - Gets coverage details (what insurance pays vs. out-of-pocket)
  - Completes documents (burial wishes, beneficiaries, etc.)
    ↓
Mapfre pays platform: €5K/month (contract)
Plus: Revenue share on leads (€50-100 per funeral home intro)
    ↓
Year 1: 1 contract (Mapfre) = €60K/month × 12 = €720K revenue
Year 2: +2 more insurers (Caser, Mutua) = €180K/month = €2.16M revenue
```

### Финансовая модель (год 1)

| Период | Contracts | Contract Revenue | Leads Revenue | Dev Cost | Net Profit |
|--------|-----------|------------------|---------------|----------|-----------|
| Month 1-3 | 0 (sales) | €0 | €2K | €15K | -€13K |
| Month 4-6 | 1 (Mapfre) | €15K | €5K | €15K | +€5K |
| Month 7-12 | 1 | €30K | €12K | €20K | +€22K |
| **YEAR 1** | **1** | **€135K** | **€51K** | **€50K** | **€136K** |

**Margin:** 77%
**Break-even:** Месяц 5-6

### Плюсы ✅
- **HUGE TAM** (22.3M insured with seguro de deceso)
- Contract = guaranteed revenue (not dependent on leads)
- Very high margin (75-80%)
- Lock-in: Hard to switch (embedded in Mapfre portal)
- Defensive moat: First white-label player (Funos = B2C, can't compete here)
- Expansion path: 2nd & 3rd insurer = 3x revenue (easier sales after first)
- **Zero competition** (literally no one doing this for Spanish insurers)

### Минусы ❌
- **Enterprise sales** (3-6 months negotiation cycle, multiple stakeholders)
- Complex integration (API + legal + compliance)
- Higher CAC (€10K-30K per contract, but LTV >> CAC)
- Longer MVP (12 weeks vs. 4 weeks for lead gen)
- Dependency on insurer roadmap (slower iterations)
- Less control over customer experience

### Когда выбрать эту модель
✅ **RECOMMENDED** if:
  - You can handle 3-6 month sales cycle
  - Have enterprise sales experience or co-founder
  - Can articulate value to Mapfre (€X savings, customer satisfaction, compliance)
  - Willing to invest €50K+ in MVP before first revenue
  - Can manage technical integration (API design, security)

❌ If:
  - Need money in month 1-2 (no runway for sales)
  - Uncomfortable with B2B enterprise sales
  - No technical co-founder (integration too complex for agency help)

### PMF Signal
- 1st insurer contract signed (yes/no)
- Contract value: ≥€3K/month (proves value)
- Deployment time: ≤3 months (feasibility)
- Customer support tickets: <5/month (smooth operation)
- Expansion interest: 2nd insurer asks within 6 months (momentum)

## HYBRID STRATEGY (RECOMMENDED FOR MAXIMIZING OPTIONS)

### Phase 1: Months 1-2 (Validation Everything)

```
Parallel workstreams:

Lead Gen track:
- CustDev: 10 families (willing to pay?)
- Sales: 10 funeral homes (willing to pay?)
- Traffic: Analyze Funos (is there room?)

White-Label track:
- Meetings: LinkedIn outreach to Mapfre/Caser
- Legal: Can we do white-label for insurers?
- MVP concept: What would Mapfre need?

Decision point: End of week 2
- If lead gen data strong + white-label interest weak → Go Lead Gen only
- If white-label interest strong → Pivot to white-label focus + lead gen as side stream
- If both strong → Hybrid (whitelist first, lead gen second)
```

### Phase 2: Months 3-6 (Execute Primary + Secondary)

**If pursuing White-Label Primary:**
```
Primary focus (70%):
- MVP for Mapfre (embedded form + 50 funeral homes)
- Sales: Caser + Mutua outreach
- Legal: White-label contract negotiations

Secondary focus (30%):
- Lead gen test: PPC in Barcelona (€1K budget)
- Measure: Can we get 100 leads/month with low CAC?
- Purpose: Diversify if white-label stalls

Result:
- Month 6: 1st insurer contract signed OR 1st lead gen funeral home paid
```

**If pursuing Lead Gen Primary:**
```
Primary focus (70%):
- MVP comparison in Madrid
- PPC ads (€2K budget)
- Sales: Contract 3-5 funeral homes

Secondary focus (30%):
- White-label: Prepare deck for insurers
- MVP design: What would Mapfre need?
- Purpose: Pivot if lead gen not scaling

Result:
- Month 6: 200+ leads/month OR 1st insurer meeting
```

### Why Hybrid Works
- Lead gen = fast cash (low uncertainty, known model via Funos)
- White-label = big upside (unknown territory, but huge TAM)
- Portfolio approach (don't bet on one horse)
- If one stalls, other accelerates
- Natural progression: Lead gen → insights → white-label easier to sell

## DECISION MATRIX (CHOOSE YOUR PATH)

### Question 1: Do you have 6 months of runway?

- **YES** → Can pursue white-label (long sales cycle)
- **NO** → Must pursue lead gen (fast cash) or hybrid

### Question 2: Do you have enterprise sales experience?

- **YES** → White-label is faster (warm intros, relationship-driven)
- **NO** → Lead gen is easier (B2B sales to funeral homes = simpler)

### Question 3: Do you want to build features or sell?

- **Features person** → Lead gen (simple, static data)
- **Sales person** → White-label (complex, high-touch)
- **Balanced** → Hybrid

### Question 4: What's your founder composition?

- **Solo founder** → Lead gen (lower complexity)
- **Tech + Sales co-founders** → White-label (needs both)
- **Tech + Tech co-founders** → Hybrid (divide & conquer)

### Question 5: Can you interview Mapfre/Caser by week 2?

- **YES** → White-label likely viable, pursue parallel
- **NO** → Stick to lead gen (no insider access = harder)
