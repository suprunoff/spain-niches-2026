## Key Findings Summary

### НИША A: BRITISH EXPATS (★★★★★ RECOMMENDED FIRST)

**Market:** 412K Britons in Spain, 90K-130K digital active, 25K-40K paying potential

**Core Pain:** Residencia bureaucracy (9/10) — 50% haven't done correctly, 2-6 weeks processing, confusing documents

**MVP:** "ExpatsOS" — all-in-one platform with:
- Residencia tracker (timeline, checklist, nearest office)
- Pension tax calculator (Spain liability + UK relief)
- Healthcare decision tree (S1 vs Convenio vs private)
- Driving license tracker
- UK property tax tool

**Why This Works:**
- Fastest MVP (4-6 weeks, one person can build)
- Lowest risk (simple SaaS, no complex regulations)
- Clearest acquisition (Facebook groups 50K+ members, SEO)
- Proven CAC channels (€30-50 per user)
- High pain validation (70%+ will validate)

**Revenue (Y1):**
- 5-8K signups, 600-1.2K paid @ €12/month = €86-173K
- Affiliate upside (Wise, lawyers) = €24-48K
- **Total Y1:** €110-221K

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: €30-50 (FB + organic)
- LTV: €300-400 (3 years)
- **LTV/CAC: 6-8x** ✅ HEALTHY

**Verdict:** ✅✅ **GO** (8.5/10 score)

**Timeline to revenue:** 6-8 weeks (first paying customer)

---

### НИША B: SPAIN-MOROCCO B2B (★★★★ CONDITIONAL GO)

**Market:** €22.7B trade corridor, 5-8K Spanish + 10-15K Moroccan SMEs, 0.79M diaspora

**Core Pain:** Customs documentation delays (8/10) — 5-10 days per shipment, complex forms, €100-300 broker fees

**Secondary Pain:** SWIFT payments (8/10) — 3-7 days, 4-7% fee, SWIFT codes often not recognized

**MVP:** "TradeFlow" — B2B marketplace with:
- Supplier directory (Spanish + Moroccan verified sellers)
- Smart RFQ system (buyers post, sellers bid)
- Automated documentation (commercial invoice, CoO, HS codes, tariff calculator)
- Compliance checker (trade agreement eligibility, duty estimator)
- Payment gateway (Wise + escrow)
- Shipping integration (DHL, FedEx, local couriers)

**Why This Works (but harder):**
- Huge market (€22.7B trade annual)
- Network effects (more sellers → better deals → more buyers)
- Sticky product (once integrated, hard to leave)
- Best unit economics (10-20x LTV/CAC)

**But:**
- Longer build (8-12 weeks, team of 2-3)
- Regulatory risk (customs, payments licensing)
- Needs domain expertise (trade, logistics)
- Chicken-egg problem (need buyers + sellers simultaneously)

**Revenue (Y1, B2B only):**
- 500-1K Spanish, 300-500 Moroccan sellers
- 200-400 deals/month @ €3-8K each
- Transaction fees (2-3%) + listings + premium = **€250-700K**

**Upside (Phase 2, Remittance):**
- 240K Moroccan diaspora users × €150/transfer × 1.5% fee
- = €1.944M/year additional

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: €150 (B2B sales)
- LTV: €2-3K (2 years)
- **LTV/CAC: 10-20x** ✅✅ EXCELLENT

**Verdict:** ⚠️ **CONDITIONAL GO** (7.0/10 score)
- Green light only after successful Spanish SME + Moroccan SME validation interviews
- Needs partner/hire for domain expertise

**Timeline to revenue:** 14-16 weeks (longer, more complex)

- Spain Expat: static FAQ, no tools

**Window to own market:** WIDE ✅

- Bnext: blockchain, limited adoption
- B2BMap: generic, no specialization

**Window to own market:** MODERATE (first-mover advantage in automation)
