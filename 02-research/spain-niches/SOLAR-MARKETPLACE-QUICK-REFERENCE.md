# Solar Energy Marketplace: Quick Reference

**Score:** 8.2/10 | **Complexity:** Medium | **Time to MVP:** 8 weeks | **Capital Required:** €50-100K

---

## The Pitch

Agrega instaladores solares en España + calculadora ROI = Lead gen marketplace para propietarios que quieren autoconsumo. Modelo: instalador paga €50/lid (como Habitissimo, pero solar).

---

## Competitors

| Competidor | Modelo | Fortaleza | Debilidad |
|-----------|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Otovo.es** | Marketplace + financiación | Calculadora, conocido | Solo financiación, lento |
| **Holaluz.es** | Utility + installations | Gestiona subsidios | No marketplace |
| **Habitissimo.es** | Lead gen genérico | 40K profesionales, trusted | No especializado en solar, bajo precio (€12-20) |
| **LeadsClientes.es** | Lead gen especializado | Solar focused | Generic, sin calculadora |

**Diferencial:** Calculadora ROI hermosa + foco en subsidies + 3+ ofertas → Faster conversion que Otovo, quality que Habitissimo.

## Business Model (MVP)

```
Homeowner fills form
    ↓
Platform calculates ROI + subsidies instantly
    ↓
Installer gets notified + pays €50/lead
    ↓
Platform keeps €35 (70% margin)
    ↓
Repeat: 1K leads/month = €35K/month revenue
```

**Unit economics:**
- CAC (Google Ads): €43 per lead
- Revenue per lead: €35
- Breakeven: ~200 leads/month
- Profitability: >500 leads/month

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |

## Why This Works (Spain-specific)

1. **Personal relations matter:** WhatsApp outreach to installers = better response than LinkedIn
2. **Subsidies are THE barrier:** Most homeowners don't know INEA/INCEA exists → calculator fixes this
3. **Installers are drowning:** 9.3 GW installed, 36K new homes/year = demand > supply
4. **Price sensitivity:** 60% of Spaniards use coupons → ROI calculator removes friction
5. **Mobile-first:** 80% mobile traffic required → calculator must work on iPhone (it does, simple form)
6. **Payment habits:** Bizum ready (30M+ users), no issues paying online
