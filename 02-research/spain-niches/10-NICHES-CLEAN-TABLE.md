## Scoring Legend

- **Demand (D):** Search volume, pain intensity, addressable market size. 1-10 scale.
- **Competition Gap (G):** Absence of Spain-specific solutions. 1-10 scale. <3 competitors = 9-10.
- **Revenue Potential (R):** LTV × retention. 1-10 scale.
- **Spain Tailwind (S):** Regulatory mandate, cultural fit, timing window. 1-10 scale.
- **MVP Speed (M):** Weeks to launch. 1-4 wks=10, 5-8 wks=8-9, 9-12 wks=6-7, 13+ wks=4-5.
- **Founder Accessibility (A):** Ease of acquiring first 10 customers (GTM leverage). 1-10 scale.
- **Defensibility (F):** Data moats, regulation, network effects, switching cost. 1-10 scale.
- **Urgency (T):** Market window closing speed; regulatory deadline proximity. 1-10 scale.

**Formula:** Score = (D×20 + G×20 + R×15 + S×15 + M×10 + A×10 + F×5 + T×5) / 100
