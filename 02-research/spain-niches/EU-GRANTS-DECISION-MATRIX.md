## Risk Scenarios & Probability

### Scenario 1: Consultant Validation Fails (Probability: 20%)
**If <50% of consultants say "I'd pay":**
- Pivot to Model A (SMB freemium)
- Increase marketing spend 3x
- Time to revenue: 12+ weeks (slower)
- Still viable, but lower unit economics

**Mitigation:** Interview 10+ consultants in week 1 (not just 5)

### Scenario 2: BDNS API Closes (Probability: 10%)
**If government shuts down open data access:**
- Fallback to web scraping (already coded)
- 24-hour transition, no customer impact
- Slightly slower updates (daily instead of real-time)

**Mitigation:** Code scraper as backup before launch

### Scenario 3: FANDIT Adds Autofill (Probability: 30%)
**If FANDIT launches PDF autofill in months 2–3:**
- You already own consultant relationships
- They have no partnership network (hard to build)
- You move into compliance + workflow extensions
- First-mover advantage = 6 months ahead

**Mitigation:** Lock in 10+ consultant partners in month 2 before FANDIT moves

### Scenario 4: Aug 2026 Cliff (Probability: 100%)
**If deadline passes and demand drops 50%:**
- Expected and planned for
- Pivot to Horizonte Europa (€15B, 2027–2030)
- Expand to other EU countries (Portugal, France, Italy)
- Revenue drops but business continues

**Mitigation:** Build for post-Aug 2026 (product not just deadline-driven)

**Overall risk level:** MEDIUM (manageable, with mitigations)
