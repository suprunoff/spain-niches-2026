## FAQ

**Q: Why Club Operations SaaS and not Tournament SaaS first?**
A: Club Ops has higher TAM (€86k vs €72k), better unit economics (25x vs 14x LTV/CAC), and solves a more urgent pain (commission). Launch Club Ops, add Tournament features 3 months later.

**Q: What if Playtomic copies the feature?**
A: Unlikely — their business model is commission-based, they won't cannibalize revenue. But move fast (6-8 weeks) and lock in clubs with long-term contracts.

**Q: Can we integrate with Playtomic or will they block us?**
A: We can read-only through their API (for analytics). They won't block because we're not stealing their players/bookings, just improving club management.

**Q: Why not just build for Playtomic as a plugin?**
A: Playtomic ecosystem is closed. Better to build standalone and gradually integrate.

**Q: What's the churn risk?**
A: 5-10%/month is typical for SMB SaaS. Mitigation: personal onboarding, fast CS, constant feature updates.

**Q: When do we expand internationally?**
A: After 100 clubs in Spain (€12k+ MRR stable). Then Portugal (6 months), then France (12 months).
