## KEY FINDINGS

### Finding #1: Government Programs Create Demand Waves
- **VeriFactu deadline Jan 2027** → Gestoría firms MUST buy software NOW
- **INEA/INCEA subsidies Q1-Q2 2026** → 1.5M homeowners suddenly able to afford solar
- **Plan de Mejora subsidies (started March 2026)** → 500-1000 qualified leads/month per region for elderly care

**Implication:** Timing is CRITICAL. These windows close quickly (subsidy windows, deadlines).

### Finding #2: 200K+ Caregiver Shortage in Spain
- 8.2M people 65+ (20% of population)
- Deficiency: 200K+ caregivers needed
- Government subisidy removes price barrier for families
- Zero government-integrated marketplace exists

**Implication:** Acute pain + government support = easiest product-market fit opportunity.

### Finding #3: Solar Installers Overwhelmed + Fragmented
- 1.5M potential Spanish homeowners
- New subsidies make solar affordable for first time
- Installers report 2-3x demand boost
- Only scattered catalogs online (Sunwatt, Autosolar) — no integrated marketplace

**Implication:** First-mover wins by connecting supply + demand + financing.

### Finding #4: Gestoría Market Stuck in 1999
- 50K Spanish accounting firms
- 80% still use Contaplus (legacy software, becoming obsolete)
- VeriFactu mandate Jan 2027 creates deadline-driven urgency
- Sage 50 expensive (€300+/month), Holled/Anfix designed for freelancers, not firms

**Implication:** Multi-client SaaS (50-500 clients per firm) is white space.

### Finding #5: Spain = Fertility Tourism Hub, but Fragmented
- 180K IVF procedures/year, 30% international patients
- Success rates (70% with donor eggs) beat Europe
- Zero integrated platform for international experience (visa, housing, financing, coordination)
- Current solutions: scattered clinics, generic aggregators

**Implication:** Integrated platform (like Fertility Angels but Spain-focused) = missing layer.

### Finding #6: Pet Economy Growing But Fragmented
- 30M+ pets in Spain (more than children!)
- Telemedicine adoption +45% annually
- Zero integrated solution (telemedicine + insurance + marketplace)
- Current: Barkibu (vet AI), UVI24 (clinic finder), Gudog (dog walking) — all separate

**Implication:** Horizontal integration opportunity (unbundling -> rebundling).

**Market Size:**


### 2. ELDERLY CARE PLATFORM — Cuidadores.es
**Score: 8.1/10** (D:9, G:8, R:7, S:8, M:7, A:9, F:8, T:8)

**Market Size:**
- TAM: €4-6B (Spain only)
- Available: 8.2M people 65+
- Shortage: 200K+ caregivers needed

**Why Now?**
- Government program "Plan de Mejora" started March 2026
- Subsidizes care services for low-income elderly families
- Creates 500-1000+ qualified leads per month per region
- Zero government-integrated marketplace exists

**MVP (4-6 weeks):**
```
Cuidadores.es — Caregiver Marketplace
├── Caregiver profiles (background checks, rate €1.2-2K/month)
├── Matching algorithm (age, language, specialty: dementia, diabetes, disability)
├── Payment routing (SEPA → caregiver, family pays through app)
├── Government integration (auto-submit to Servicio Social for subsidies)
├── Family dashboard (track care, messaging, scheduling)
└── Compliance (contrato mercantil, seguridad social paperwork)
```

**Monetization:**
- Freemium for families (€2-5 per caregiver contact)
- SaaS for care agencies (€100-200/month + comms)
- B2B partnerships with insurance (DKV, Sanitas) for referrals

**Unit Economics (Estimated):**
- CAC: €45 (organic + subsidized channels)
- LTV: €480 (36-month relationship, 8-10% churn)
- Payback: 10-12 months
- Margin: 60%

**Key Risk:** Liability insurance (increases CAC), economic sensitivity (first budget cut)

**Competitive Advantage:** Government-integrated (auto-submit for subsidies). Competitors (Qida, Depencare) are B2C only or corporate-focused.

**Timeline:** SUBSIDY WINDOW OPEN NOW (March 2026+) = acute urgency

### 3. MODERN GESTORÍA SaaS — GestoriaHub.es
**Score: 8.1/10** (D:9, G:8, R:8, S:8, M:8, A:8, F:8, T:7)

**Market Size:**
- TAM: €200-400M (Spain only)
- Available: 50K gestoría firms
- Revenue per client: €3-10K/year (50-500 clients per firm)

**Why Now?**
- VeriFactu mandate: January 2027 (SL) → July 2027 (autónomos)
- 9 months = deadline-driven urgency
- Legacy software (Contaplus, A3) becoming obsolete
- Sage 50 expensive (€300+/month), Holled/Anfix not designed for multi-client gestoría workflow

**MVP (4-6 weeks):**
```
GestoriaHub.es — Gestoría SaaS Platform
├── Multi-client dashboard (manage 50-500 clients per firm)
├── VeriFactu compliance (auto-integration with SES.Hospedajes API)
├── Modelo 303 auto-generation (tax form automation)
├── Facturación (invoice + payment processing via Redsys)
├── Client portal (clients see documents, status, contact gestoría)
├── AI document recognition (receipt → accounting entry)
└── WhatsApp/SMS notifications (compliance alerts, deadline reminders)
```

**Monetization:**
- €50-200/month SaaS per gestoría (based on client count)
- €2-5 per managed client (scalable metric)
- Premium: €100-300/month (advanced reporting, AI features)

**Unit Economics (Estimated):**
- CAC: €55 (LinkedIn outreach + accounting conferences)
- LTV: €620 (48-month relationship, 6-8% annual churn)
- Payback: 9-11 months
- Margin: 75% (SaaS advantage)

**Key Risk:** Regulatory compliance required (high legal risk), data migration complexity (high switching cost to onboard)

**Competitive Advantage:** Multi-client management (Sage 50, Holled designed for single-client workflow). First to combine compliance + multi-client + affordable pricing.

**Timeline:** DEADLINE JAN 2027 = 9 months urgency. Early adopter advantage (market consolidation happens Jan 2027+).
